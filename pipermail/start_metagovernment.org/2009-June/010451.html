Kris, thanks for bringing the Governance Assessment Portal to our attention.
I documented its mission, goal, and objectives in StratML format at
http://xml.gov/stratml/index.htm#Other or, more specifically,
http://xml.gov/stratml/GAPortal.xml

 

It is the 526th plan indexed in Mark Logic's StratML search service
prototype -- http://xml.gov/stratml/index.htm#SearchServices - in which it
currently ranks 2nd among 104 referencing "governance".  (Metagovernment
ranks 3rd.)  It also ranks 2nd among 117 plans referencing "assessment".

 

Soon I will also add UNDP's plan to the StratML collection.

 

Whether government agencies are posting their strategic and performance
plans on the Web in conformance with a standard like StratML is one of the
criteria on which the quality of their governance should be assessed, in
support of the purposes outlined at
http://xml.gov/stratml/index.htm#DefinitionPurposes - particularly the
facilitation of feedback from citizen stakeholders.
