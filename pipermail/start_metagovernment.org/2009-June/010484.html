I have updated the Conferences page to be more flexible: accommodating  
any conferences, not just those we are presenting at:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Conferences

If you know of upcoming conferences that may be of relevance to this  
community, please add them to this page. Unless one of us is a  
confirmed speaker there, please put the conference in italics, as is  
currently done with the July conference "Open Government &  
Innovations" (which apparently did not accept my proposal).

Also, video of my presentation at Participation camp is linked from  
that page and available at:
http://www.livestream.com/pcamp/ondemand/pla_2180618837199382505

The first nine minutes are technical problems caused by someone having  
opened up a tab with a feed of the audio output, causing a feedback- 
loop. So if you want to avoid a lot of screeching sounds, just skip  
ahead to 9:00 in the video.

I'm not particularly proud of this presentation: I don't do as well  
when I am sitting down, when I have no visual feedback, and when I  
think there may be no-one listening (though apparently it turns out  
that 50 people tuned in by the end). I got a little flustered, but I  
think I got the basic point across.
