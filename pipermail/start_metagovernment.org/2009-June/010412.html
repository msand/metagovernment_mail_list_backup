Matías, this is wonderful! The Wikimania crowd is a great target  
audience for this project: people who really understand the power of  
crowdsourcing. Please don't hesitate to ask the list if there's  
anything we can do to help with your presentation.

And as for the reviewers' comments about "open source governance," I  
think it emphasizes my comment in my proposal of earlier today that it  
is time to simplify that word. And if you wish to expand the title,  
feel free to use my OGI proposal as a model:
http://my.opengovinnovations.com/events/event/76
