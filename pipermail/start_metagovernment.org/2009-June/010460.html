I got the same advert.  It looks like MixedInk was actually chosen by

  http://lists.thataway.org/scripts/wa-THATAWAY.exe?A2=ind0906D&L=NCDD-DISCUSSION&T=0&O=D&P=5160

It's a good technical choice.  MixedInk is only system that uses P2P
recombinant text (aside from Votorola, still in alpha) and there's no
other way to scale the collaborative drafting (my opinion).  Their
only problem is the mass voting system.  I bet you anything they'll
soon be replacing it with a P2P voting system.  Then they'll shift to
a higher gear.  Although they're closed and commercial, they could
still win in the short term (like 5 years).

BTW: here's another commercial project (in a different field) that's

chasing them down?
