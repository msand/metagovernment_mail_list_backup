On Jun 10, 2009, at 10:28 PM, Owen Ambur wrote:

> In order to avoid causing that effect, it is important first to be  
> pretty
> clear about what it is that we are aiming to do and for whom we aim  
> to do
> it.

My first stab would be to say that the aims are:
- To promote the use of internet technologies to supplement and/or  
supplant representative democracies, with the aim of vastly increasing  
citizens' input into the communities to which they belong.
- To facilitate and coordinate thoughts, efforts, and (when possible)  
technologies among different projects working toward the above.

and the stakeholders are:
- Those involved in developing governance software
- Those wishing to contribute to the effort to open governance through  
software
- (later) Anyone who wishes to participate in the governance of any  
community
