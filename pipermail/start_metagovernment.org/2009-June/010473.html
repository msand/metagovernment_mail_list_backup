Up until now, vanity domains on Facebook have been reserved primarily for
Profiles. Facebook Pages have also had the opportunity to sign up for their
own personalized URL, but needed to have been created a few months ago and
have at least 10,000 members. At midnight tonight, the barriers are being
lowered for Facebook Pages, requring a measly 25 members to qualify.

Don't forget: The Rush is On Facebook, Tonight at Midnight, to grab your own
Facebook URL before someone else beats you to the punch!
