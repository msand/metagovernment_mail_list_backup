As a non-developer, I'm a fan of the --1, -1, 0, +1, ++1 system I saw
earlier.

This easily translated into "i'm strongly opposed," "i don't care," or "I
strongly agree" to me.

All the extra numbers seem add an unnecessary level of complexity.

If you're keen on complexity, the --/++1 system included decimal points for
those wishing a finer level of specificity, WITHOUT requiring everyone else
to do so.
