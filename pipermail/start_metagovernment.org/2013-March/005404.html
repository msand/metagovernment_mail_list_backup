Dear Knight News Challenge Applicant,

Thank you so much for submitting your idea to the Knight News Challenge: Open
Gov. We are writing to let you know that, after careful review, your project has
not been selected as a semifinalist.

Please be assured that this isn’t a reflection on the quality of your idea. We
received more than 880 entries for 40 semifinalist slots.

This contest is only part of [1]Knight Foundation’s broader support of open
government work. We’ll let you know as we announce new projects and initiatives
and hope you’ll continue to be a part of our community.

This contest was the first that we have run using our new platform, and we want
to thank you for participating with us. We created the platform to make the
process more open and participatory, and we hope you will continue to share,
discuss and collaborate. We’ve already seen new partnerships and ideas come from
many of the submissions. Your proposal will stay on the site unless you choose
to unpublish it.

Over the next week, semifinalists will post brief videos and more detailed
information about their projects. We hope that you will check back on April 5 to
see how their ideas have evolved and to offer thoughts and questions for them.

Thanks again for joining us, and we hope to see you soon.

Kindly,

Chris Sopher, Chris Barr & the News Challenge team
