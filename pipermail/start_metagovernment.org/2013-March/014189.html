Mike,

Thanks for adding Agreedis. I actually added Agreedis to the Knight
Challenge a while back, but then did not have time to follow up on it. So I
doubt I made it past the first round. You are certainly welcome to add
Agreedis to the list of providers in your entry.

We admittedly have a slightly different focus than other sites -- namely
the focus on not fostering discussion, but fostering compromise. Our
concept is that we basically pick up where the discussion and
idea-suggestion systems leave off. They are great at soliciting ideas and
determining their popularity, but they do not actually help to make the
final decision. That is our core -- analyzing people's preferences and
funneling them into a compromise decision that the majority of people can
accept. (Now we just need a few million people ... :)

cheers,
  Steven
