Unfortunately I can't attend SMVcon.  But I'd like to share my answer
to one item of the proposed questionnaire (Fragekatalog):

  * Are you a developer?  If so, can you imagine cooperating with AG
    Meinungsfindungstool in the development of a discussion tool?

Yes, I'm a developer for Votorola.  Speaking for myself, I'd like to
cooperate.  My main requirement would be that the core platform remain
open to all technical designs, including Votorola's.  I think the
competence to delimit the user's choice of tools belongs to the user
alone, while we as responsible engineers are competent only to enhance
and extend that personal choice, never to impede it!  We've long
understood how to give each user complete freedom of choice in regard
to discussion and opinion-forming tools.  We know of at least one
solution (already posted to these lists) that nobody has yet found a
fault with; namely free-range voting based on vote mirroring.

Please let's not surrender our responsibilities and freedoms to a
monopoly controlled by technocrats, or by anyone else.  Instead, let's
root out the would-be monopolies and bust them up.  Or better yet,
let's prevent them forming in the first place.  (Again, we know how,
because it's the same solution.)
