And it seem that it is philosophy that divides us. It is difficult to talk
about personal philosophy, it is scary and may be painful. I hope we are
both ready for it.
I see persons more as emotional beings. In my opinion the whole
"rationality" thing is here just to serve emotions.
Also I am not entirely in the "idea of progress", progress is meaningless
if you do not enjoy life while progress is done. If we push too strong for
progress, I think that we believe that we will enjoy life later. So the
idea of enjoyment and emotions is there in "progress", just in an odd way.
And Eudaimonia feels like progress. Is it like progress?
