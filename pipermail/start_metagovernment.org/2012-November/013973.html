Does it sound harsh in English too, to be told the rules in case you 
want to participate are such and such?

Alex and Paul, I think you are the first ones who ask this, after we had 
this poll about this a while back. But back then we agreed on those 
rules for us, who were participating already, so this sounded ok to me. 
But now I think "Procedure" or so would sound better.

But anyways, just to mention, these rules can OFC also be changed! ... 
by the same procedure, only that the majority is higher and the 
waiting-period is 3 weeks. (see the second paragraph here: 
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Rules)

;-)
