For a newcomer who stumbles on it?  We're aiming to reward exploration
long enough to get across a two-fold message:

 (1) What you see (whatever's on the page) is being discussed.
 (2) You can join the discussion.  It's happening here --->

We haven't got there yet.  But from there, it'll be a job for people.
If the tools are useful to folks who already understand them, then
those folks will take responsibility for newcomers.  That may entail
demanding further tool changes, but exactly what we don't know.

The geotrack isn't coded yet.  Another difference: I arrange the
top-left links in the same order as the corresponding tracks below.
As mooted in the post above, we're thinking we might use tactile
lighting effects between the two to give the user a sense of what each
track is for.  Little things like that.

Since the aim is to funnel newcomers into the discussion (1,2), you
can see how Conseo's talk track will have to shine.  We don't quite
know how yet.  But the design of the Crossforum Theatre stage allows
us to experiment.  We'll get it right eventually.

I'm coding the plug-in toolset switch now (top right).  It's nearly
ready (maybe tonight or tomorrow).  I'm very grateful to Metagov for
making it possible!  We have no right to showcase Votorola here and
test its design etc. if we aren't equally open to other toolsets.
