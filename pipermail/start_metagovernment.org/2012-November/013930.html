The working group 'AG Meinungsfindungstool' of the Pirate Party Germany
would like to invite you to the 5th International E-Democracy Talk!

Please note that all session are going to be held in english.


WHEN?

On Saturday 10th November 2012
At 09:00 PM (UTC+1 Berlin time)


WHAT?

**Roger Eaton** presents the concept and idea of **intermix.org**:

InterMix Collective Communication Software recombines group participants
online to create six collective Voices of Humanity: Women, Men, Youth,
Experience (middle-aged), Wisdom (seniors) and the Voice of Humanity as One.

See http://intermix.org/overview.htm for more information.

The working pad for the session can be found here:
http://meinungsfindungstool.piratenpad.de/InternationalEDemocracyTalk-2012-11-10
