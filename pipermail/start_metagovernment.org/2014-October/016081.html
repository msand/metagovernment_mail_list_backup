Hi Michael,
you are welcome to copy anything from Vilfredo outside.

But for Vilfredo to work properly proposal should be anonymous, and not
referencing to material outside, especially that can be changed
(referencing a wikipedia page is ok, referencing a personal page in a blog
is definitely not).

Sorry, but the limitations are there to assure a fair evaluations of all
the proposals, and not give an advantage to some people (especially to more
social people)
