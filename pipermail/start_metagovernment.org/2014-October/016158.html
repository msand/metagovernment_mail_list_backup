every holding (e.g. Ltd. owned and operated by a single person) represents
a DIGITAL NOMAD and will hold one or many other (e.g. Ltd's).
Every project, (e.g. creating an "App") will be owned by 1 or many DIGITAL
NOMADs.

The contracts how those entities will collaborate with each other shall be
open sourced and collaboratively evolved.
Every DIGITAL NOMAD (in the above definition) should work completely
transparent (even the monetary transactions and current balance sheets) in
order to protect against corruption.
There will be a strong focus on automation of all kinds of bureaucracy...
and because projects are co-owned and there are no employees, there is also
a need to experiment how to  coordinate/synchronize with each other and/or
agree upon stuff.

The or a next step would be to play around with crypto currencies and
crypto shares (ethereum.org). The former, because i personally believe that
real democracy lies in a correctly structured monetary systems, where money
represents votes.
