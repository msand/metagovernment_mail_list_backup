would like to note to this that Double Highs and other high RWAs will 
probably seem ridiculously week in global context, unable to collect

I was also thinking about the filters a little bit more. The thing is 
that we are unable to enter most of the useful filters into the voting 
mechanism ( and that is maybe right, it is so easy to make a mistake). 
On the other hand we might be able to enter a mechanism that allows to 
create personal filters. We may allow the citizen to say that anybody 
who suits some conditions can in some proportion decide for her. You may 
see it as a proposal for improvement of proxies. E.g. when you will not 
have enough time, you will bind your vote with condition, that the group 
of people who have passed your psychological amygdala test may 
proportionally decide for you. Your condition will be public and others 
may join it, too. You may also say that in all proposals that contain 
the word "chemistry", you will be represented just by people who have 
certain degree in chemistry (from certain universities)(you may make 
your amygdala test to apply too).
