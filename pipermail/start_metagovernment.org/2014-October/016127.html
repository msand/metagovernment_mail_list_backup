Hi Jacopo, the next version has group participation. Which means that the
software will know when everybody have participated. Still you cannot
simply move on when all have voted, because in Vilfredo there is a whole
phase when people are allowed to change their vote after they voted and
they see how others have voted. We might be able to do this in two steps:
you vote, and then you sign that you accept the particular result that came
out. Of course as soon as someone changes the result your signature is no
longer valid. You would have to sign that you accept any result, and will
never change your vote, but then it looses the power of people changing the
vote. And it makes it way more complicated. So I am still thinking how to
best do this

>>> aiming for global, decentralized, and/or non-specific systems and
