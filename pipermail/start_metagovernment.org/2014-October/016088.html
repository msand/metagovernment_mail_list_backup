On 2 October 2014 09:22, Michal Štěpánek <michal at eclipso.eu> wrote:

>  Pietro,
>
> Vilfredo proposals have "Abstract" and "Full Text". What do you think
> about adding a "Comment" section for the author ? or the possibility to
> comment a proposal I agree with in Endorsment phase ?
>
>
This is exactly how the new system works. Each proposal can be commented in
the endorsment phase. When you comment you should say if you are raising a
point in support of a proposal, against a proposal or asking a question.
The author can also answer questions. You can also "like" comments of
others, so the comments can later be ordered by number of likes.

In the old system we made those changes but they never went live. We had
some bugs, but discovered them while we were well in the process of
developing the new Vilfredo, so we just decided to leave it like this, and
it will be different in the new system. We are a very small team with
limited resources and big dreams :-)
