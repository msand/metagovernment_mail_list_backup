My guess is that whatever system humans puts together for the next iteration of *Democracy*, it’s going to look more and more like what’s happening at *Valve*. 
In my mind, it will be self-organizing, very creative, employ the assistance of AI, ….and will most likely be way more complex than we can possibly imagine from our current vantage point. Scaling won’t be an issue. We will be so connected and empowered technologically that coming to decisions in large groups will be relatively simple and routine.

In a decade or two, I don’t think there will be much *government*, per say. That job will be (is being) taken over by the Corporate model of managing our affairs. The Corporate model will be taken over by the Cooperative model. The Cooperative model will run on a continually evolving platform/network/program/intelligence for managing the mission of our self-organization. The mission is *All for All*.  This will be (is being) driven by the same shift in consciousness that bring all of us to this list….and will be built on the bits and pieces now developing among us.

As together we choose, together it will be.  :)
