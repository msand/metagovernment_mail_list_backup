True! I agree this is a flaw, but it works. And I cannot see how ANY system

False! Moderators (which are only called facilitator for a reason) do NOT
help you see through flaws in your reasoning. They only make sure we, as a
circle, follow the correct procedure (now there is this phase, now there is
that phase). I do not have enough experience to know what facilitators do
when people start to act unreasonable (we all have done it), maybe Gustav
could chip in here. Maybe they just need to be trained enough to spot it
and adjourn the session when everybody is more relaxed and less stressed
out.
