Pietro, I think you are bringing up a very important point.

And again, I want to point to Aktiv Demokrati's model as something to  
at least consider. Sorry, I don't have the link handy at the moment,  
but the basic idea is that the smaller the percentage of participants,  
the longer a proposal takes to become enacted (with their example  
being that something supported by only one person would take 10,000  
years to convert to law).
