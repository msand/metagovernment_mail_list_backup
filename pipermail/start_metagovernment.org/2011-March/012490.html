I'd like to get the application basically working in time for a big
ColdFusion conference in mid-May, where I hope to at least lead a
Birds-of-a-feather discussion on all of this:
http://engage.cfobjective.com/index.cfm/topicSuggestions/

But with a full-time job and four kids, I'm lucky to find more than an hour
or two a week to devote to this.  :)

It would be nice to get it done in time for next year's SXSW conference,
where https://popvox.com/ just took first place in SXSW Accelerator contest:
http://sxsw.com/node/7079

VoxPop is similar to CrowdVote, only you can't delegate your vote, you can't
vote from Twitter or FaceBook, and there's only one Docket: the
Congressional Agenda.

Hence I sort of think of CrowdVote as "VoxPop on steroids."

But as far as my own personal goals, I just want to build this app for use
in my own condo community of 62 members, who are eager to try this out.

Votorola definitely looks very interesting.  Still wading through it.  Heady
stuff!
