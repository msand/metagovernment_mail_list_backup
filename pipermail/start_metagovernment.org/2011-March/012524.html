Yes, this would imo actually be best ... if you draft your version, so 
we really can see specifically, where and how we differ.

For that you just copy my draft for instance, modify it and put it under 
your userpages in the wiki, similiar to mine.

Your voters and your candidate (in case you have such) appear in the 
navigation thing on the left of your page. From there you can create 
diffs and so on. If you want another user there (not a voter or 
candidate of yours) you can pin him there:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Template:Draft
