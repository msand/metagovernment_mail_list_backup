Hello Jason,

Welcome to the list!

I find your idea very fascinating and I like the sense of openness I get 
from it, because you dont try to pull the users into your app more than 
necesarry but instead go to where they are already are, i.e. in blogs, 
twitter, ... . But I'm not sure, if I got the picture right already. Is 
it like that I can follow the votestream of A who follows the votestream 
of B who follows C ... D ... F ... ?

One thing, that feels limiting is the fact, that I should only be able 
to vote yes/no on given proposals. But that can be changed easily. This 
is the field we have focused most of our work on so far: to enable the 
voter to make own proposals or improve given ones. The first problem 
that comes to mind here is scalability, right? If you allow this, you'll 
soon have thousands of cloned proposals! But this is solved. Now I would 
be interested in looking, if there are possiblities to combine both.

What is your opinion on this draft of an open architecture? Your concept 
would (beside others) mainly adress the personal autocasting, right? :
http://u.zelea.com/w/User:Mike-ZeleaCom/Opening_an_architecture

Greetings,
Thomas
