Anouncement:

Big statement:

I would like to suggest that we should try to 

1.maximize net *procedural* utility of social choice for
(average neurotypical) citizen.
(net = positive utility - negative utility)

2. keep the costs within budget, where budget may range from 0 up
to the limits of technological development. It depends on your resources
and resources of your movement.


That is all, nothing more.
The utility, *quality* and *quantitity* of produced decision is
important just as a part of procedural utility.
Resources are important just if you cannot "pay the bill" as a movement.


I say the old performance functions are imprecise, especially those of
1.maximum decisions for minimal economic costs.
2.maximum quality of decision for minimal costs.

To sum it up, my performance fucntion is
most pleasurable decision-making for accepted costs.

What do you say ? Do you think that there is something more important
than procedural utility ?
