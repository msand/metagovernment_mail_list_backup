Fair enough. I think editing posts are asking for trouble and confusion
though. Edits gives power to the proposer to try and manipulate the
discussion by restarting it each time they preform an edit. It's easier for
the casual user to follow along if it's not changing ever week. The debates
will flow smoothly because no one can just change what they said which
might make the rest of the thread not make sense.


> Free speech is indeed a major issue here and why I've never been

But juries aren't an attainable position, they are assigned randomly. Say
the site picks 10 random users and asked them to uphold the rules of the
site e.g. no hate speech. Get the 10 to decide if the post made qualifies
as hate speech. If found guilty - time penatly from posting.
