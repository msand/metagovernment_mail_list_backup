Дмитрий: I see capitalism as an economic system to be improved
—with effective tax and wealth distribution policies— rather than replaced.
I have two very good reasons:

   - Today's economy is way to complex to be controlled, better leave that
   work to the free market laws.
   - It is relatively straightforward for a capitalistic nation to patch its
   current system, in the other hand total reform requires much more work and
   you have to be sure that the new system is going to be more successful.

For a patch, *corruption must be reduced first*, because it is the cause
that prevents the appliance of more equitable policies.
