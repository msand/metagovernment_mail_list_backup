My brief bio is available at http://mysite.verizon.net/ambur/bio.htm

 

I rendered the information provided at
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Main_Page in Strategy Markup Language
(StratML) format at http://xml.gov/stratml/index.htm#Other or, more
specifically, http://xml.gov/stratml/MGP.xml  

 

It is the 452nd "plan" indexed in Mark Logic's StratML search service
prototype:  http://xml.gov/stratml/index.htm#SearchServices 

 

I look forward to seeing Metascore (as well as other means) used to improve
the processes by which stakeholders provide feedback on the strategic goals
and performance objectives established by government agencies, as well as
nonprofit, public service organizations and commercial enterprises in which
they have interests.

 

For more information on the emerging StratML standard, see
http://xml.gov/stratml/index.htm
