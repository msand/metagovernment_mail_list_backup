Hi Scott,
I think we should not delete those links. Somehow they represent the
history of metagovernment. Some of those project has been discontinued
(like Candiwi and Telematics Freedom Foundation), some has changed and
moved on (like NationBuilder).

I think we should move them into a list that recognises their role. Maybe
ordered by when they appeared or left. And ask the old authors for a final
statement explaining what has happened. What did they learned from that
experience. Something that would make sure the experience is not lost but
remains as part of the collective experience of this community.

Regards,
