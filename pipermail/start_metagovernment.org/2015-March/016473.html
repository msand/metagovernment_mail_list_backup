My intervention in this thread is to compare your system to the one working
in Airesis.

Regarding the question Airesis have for every paragraph the possibility to
contribute, if someone does not understand and have a question he must
request to clarify.
Each new contribution instead have 3 possible evaluation Agree Disagree and
Neutral + the opportunity to comment.

For the record Airesis work just like that, regarding redraft I do agree
that reading a proposal after being edited because some of the "welcome by
the community" suggestions have been integrated, is time consuming and
sometime a turnoff, and as a solution I will propose to our programmer to
turn the previous read text in a different color from the one that have
being edited so I can concentrate only in the new one.
Every time I revisit a proposal all the text will be reset to color 1 and
the new text to color 2.
The DB must keep track of every visit and " remember for me" what I read!
I think is possible and not so difficult to program.
Regards
Jacopo

 PS
After many (3 now) years of use we do not record any gaming or try to
disrupt the system in any of the groups, no one can cancel any of the
comment and there is the option of flagging and the association Tecnologie
Democratiche retain the power to delete a comment or contribution.
This power have never being used.
