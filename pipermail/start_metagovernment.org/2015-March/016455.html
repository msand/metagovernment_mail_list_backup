Hi All. I discussed the idea of a conference with Alessandro before. And
just wanted to share with everybody how I think it is a great idea. Somehow
we need a middle ground between general offline conferences that cost a lot
of money (both to host and to attend) and mailing lists that risks being
attended by few people.

Something where each person is given half an hour of video presentation on
google hangout. WHich thus has the possibility for few people to be present
in hangout asking questions, and everybody else to be present on youtube.
And the presentation is then stored for posterity. Something people
prepare, but not so much like conferences.  Conferences those days are a
bit a medium too slow for a world that progresses every day so rapidly.
While we simply do not have the time to follow every conversation on a
topic. So an online conference that happens anything between once every 2
months and once every 6 months sounds optimal. With the number of
presentations to inversely proportional to how often the conference is
(once every 6 months would make longer conferences).

Personally I have results to present both in terms of Vilfredo, and in
terms of my personal research. Although I will chose what to present to
focus on only one domain (or none at all) on each occasion.

Well done Alessandro.
