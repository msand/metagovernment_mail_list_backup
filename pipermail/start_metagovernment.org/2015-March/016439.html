>>> Hi everyone,
>>>
>>> I’d like to add a suggestion from Jacopo, Teo Martini, and Pietro
>>> Speroni of Vilfredo.
>>>
>>> Again, the idea is to create a group of exchange of information, and NOT
>>> a group of common consensus and merge your different experiences.
>>> The idea is to become "friends of eDemocracy", but every eDemocracy
>>> experience should be respected in its diversity.
>>> It is like if we had different bikes, a “freestyle” one, an old
>>> fashioned one, a competition one and a folding bike. We can share hints or
>>> experiences, share maps or “critical mass” events, help each other if we
>>> want but everyone will keep liking her/his biking style, and should be
>>> respected in that.
>>> We do not need to decide a winner nor vote for the best, but leave
>>> natural diversity to keep multiple solutions in order to have more chance
>>> to adapt to different contexts.
>>> Do you like the plan?
>>>
>>> So if you know about any other eDemocracy experience just feel free to
>>> add people in the loop or to let us know about the new path.
