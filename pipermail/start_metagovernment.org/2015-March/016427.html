I think there is difference between this syndicate and other syndicates 
and syndicalism.

I will compare it to crony capitalism.
Crony capitalism is the most competitive form we know. It has some 
weaknesses I am trying to exploit. The weakness are
cronyism (meritocracy should be more effective) and
class war, which is limiting motivation.

The usual syndicate is quite charitable organisation, its main argument 
are better wages for ordinary workers.

On the other hand my syndicate is political, its main argument is making 
"neurotypical politics" with the help of corruption (a way that has 
proved to be effective).
Cronyism is very limited here and should have no significant impact on 
decisions.
Motivation should be also better.
Contrary to other syndicates, this syndicate should not be charitable. 
Wages will be still little, but the syndicate will advice how to spend 
those money effectively. The goal of this syndicate is to establish 
direct democracy (not syndicalism), it should be marketed as important 
motivation.
Is it operational ?
