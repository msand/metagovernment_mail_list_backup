Not sure if you appreciate the irony of running for representative on
the platform that representative government doesn't work ;-)

I'm sometimes tempted to do this, or engage the existing powers
(representatives and/or bureaucrats) to gain their cooperation, but
have decided that this isn't the way the revolution happens. We need
to press those people to release the information we need to run a
government in parallel with theirs, but I'd have to recommend against
jumping into the fray ourselves because the issues of corruption,
legitimizing the opposition, and wasting precious resources (our time
needs to be spent building systems to enable the people to run their
government, not wasted actually trying to run the government ourselves
;-)
