Hi Everyone,


Thanks for the invite. I made some wiki edits as "idealpragmatist".

I see folks are tangling on the website with the concepts of user ranking
and scoring.  Perhaps this is out of scope, but has it been suggested or
discussed of having a "soft security" (human based) such that individuals
(and groups of individuals) invite and vouch for other individuals (and are
to some degree accountable). Scoring and karma could then accrue to groups
rather than individuals. Individuals would desire to be from a "reputable"
group . ..  .

Sock-puppeting is very hard to counteract but if individuals are required to
form/join social groups who have a collective reputation, there is a
reasonable chance for self-organizing governance, self policing and
distributed ID verification.


Michael Pilling
