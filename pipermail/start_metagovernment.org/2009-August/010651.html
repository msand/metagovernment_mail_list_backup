Michael,

Ed drew to my attention that you had completed the questionnaire. First of
all thanks for taking the time to do so, it's much appreciated.

As you know, the OpenKollab project is aiming, among other things, to
identify specific collaboration opportunities by matching projects together
on the basis of the information that we are collecting. In Phae I of our
plan is to post this information in an open space:
<start at metagovernment.org>http://wiki.openkollab.com/wagn/ProjectInfo. In
doing so and in actively marketing the the fact that we are doing this, we
hope to collect data on roughly 100 projects/smaller organizations.

Phase II of the project will then involve manually reviewing the data and
mapping organizations together to the best of our ability. In this process
we hope to identify the information that is relevant to actually match
organizations to each other.

In Phase III of the project, we will modify the questionnaire as well as try
to automate, in the platform, the matching of projects to each other. We
will also then try to roll the idea out more widely and get a larger number
of projects on the platform.

I am going to include the version of the questionnaire that you provided if
that's OK. Feel free to join the OpenKollab community to modify it, or let
me know if you want to make any additions or changes.

Thanks again,
