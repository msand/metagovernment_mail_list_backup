> Hi James, and welcome.
>
> Part of the solution is in forward thinking.
> Here in metagovernment we have a very long range of looking forward,
> and we expect the internet to keep on growing, until virtually
> everyone will be hooked up.
>
> Of course we are caught a bit surprised by how fast things seem to
> speed up from time to time. Like Obama attention toward e-Gov was
> mostly unexpected, and caught many of us off guard.
>
> But generally wer are not expecting all this to happen so early. So we
> prepare by working on the software, and testing it in small
> communities.
>
> But there are some of us who are also more on your line. For example
> Giovani Spagnolo, who works for Freedom Foundation in Italy, has a
> strategy for direct democracy that includes people voting by snail
> mail for those that are off grid.
