I met with Matias yesterday night. He went through the whole presentation as
if he was actually presenting. I have to say it looked and sounded really
good. Matias was trying to get a friend of his a press credential in order
to tape the talk. It will be in Spanish so we might add some subtitles
after.

Regarding the closing phrase, I really like it, and Matias makes the
clarification that we got in a point in history where technology is a TOOL
that enables us to propose massive collaborative democracy as an option, but
it's up to us to make that work. It was a real nice closing and I wouldn't
change it.

I proposed for an initial impact to open with this reflection:
- How many times have you criticized a government/directory/council?
- How many times did you do anything to actually change what you were
criticizing?
- How many times did you have/know the way to actually do something about
it?
- How many times did you think whatever you did would just make no
difference?

I absolutely agree that a live tour through the projects would be real nice.
Matias, I know you have laptop issues so let me know if you want to borrow
mine.

Good Luck!!

Juan
