The BBC just started work on a documentary about the "digital  
revolution," which is divided into four segments:
1. power on the web
2. the fate of nations
3. the cost of free
4. the web and us
http://www.bbc.co.uk/digitalrevolution/

Obviously, these topics are of special interest to this group. I have  
posted about it in a comment on their blog, here:
http://www.bbc.co.uk/blogs/digitalrevolution/2009/08/the-rise-of-the-enation.shtml
and would encourage other members of this project to get involved as  
well.

This would be a great venue for a wider audience to be introduced to  
this idea of open governance.
