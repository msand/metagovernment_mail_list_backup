Michael,

Thanks again for the show of support. Since, at this point the primary focus
is just to collect data we hadn't considered focusing strictly on the online
participation/e-democracy space. That said, the path we follow will be
largely defined by those that want to work with us to help us to realize
this vision.

If it should transpire that the participation/e-democracy 'ecosystem'
reflects the most interest in our model, completes the questionnaire and
engages with us we will quite naturally focus our attention on this group.

It would be great if others in the group help us out by completing the
questionnaire and sending it to me.

To the extent, MIchael, that you can help to stimulate this through your
relationships within the group it would be much appreciated!

Thanks again for the interest and support!
