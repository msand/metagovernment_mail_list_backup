I think there was a misunderstanding. As Ed correcctly pointed out a
conference will need plenty of time to prepare.
We are not refering here to a skype 3 person inter nos video
conference, but a real 30-100 people involved (virtual) meeting.

as time unfolds. We could start a new format.
