Please see the message below. I have filled out a copy of this  
questionnaire for the Metagovernment project, but related projects  
(and individuals) may also wish to fill it out to help them build a  
map of collaborative opportunities. As an additional incentive, they  
are also mapping possible collaborative opportunities in terms of  
funding sources.

If you do fill it out, please return it to Suresh, cc'd on this  
message. He and this survey are with the relatively newly-formed  
OpenKollab: http://openkollab.com/ which has a lot of overlap with us  
in terms of collaboration, though they are not as focused on  
governance. This group grew out of ParticipationCamp, which I spoke at  
in June.

Thanks.
