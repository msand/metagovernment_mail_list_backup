I agree that it is time for the Metagovernment project to organize an  
online conference.

So how do we proceed? We need:
- a date/time
- a technology (since presumably this will be mostly/entirely virtual)
- a title and theme
- a page on the wiki

I am again under a time crush, so can't work on these at the moment,  
but anyone is free to jump in. The easiest way to start would be to  
start discussing the date and technology here on the list while also  
making a page on the wiki to start coordinating some of the other  
aspects.

For a date/time, there is the issue of global participation. 8:00 AM  
California-time is already getting into mid-afternoon in Europe... and  
is midnight in Japan. How do we organize a conference that goes on for  
hours considering this disconnect?

I'm cc'ing the OK list in case they have some helpful ideas along this  
line.
