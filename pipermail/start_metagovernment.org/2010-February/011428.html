Hello

If I may add my thoughts on the subject of our meetings, I would add the
following thoughts:

First, I'll give my impression. I participated in one meetingand it was very
busy, with a lot of participants. I was dazzled by the huge amount of
information. I could not find anything that is of interest to me.

   1. Not all subjects are of interest to all of us. We are interested in
   some subjects.
   2. Too many participants in any meeting may cause overflow of
   information, and lack of interest. I would try to limit any meeting to 4-6
   participants and 10 to 20 viewers (that can comment or vote if needed).
   3. Therefore I'll suggest. every meeting will be on a different subject,
   in MG project.
      1. The subject of the meeting will be notified in advance.
      2. The meeting will be held on skype-conference (Voice is more easy to
      follow)
      3. agreement will be reached on any point, by debate, there after,
      deliberation and finely, voting, if needed.
      4. The agreement will be written on wiki for permanent memory of the
      process.
      5. after the meeting, the wiki page can be further developed by the MG
      projects teams.

Cheers
