When this list server started, with 11 members, I figured it would be  
transient. I figured we would move to a different venue pretty  
quickly, so I called it "startup." Fate seems to have decided  
otherwise, and after two years the list seems to still be going strong  
with over 100 members.

To that end, I'd like to change its appearance slightly. It would be a  
huge hassle to change the actual mailing address (basically would have  
to make a new list), so I'd like to avoid that. But I do intend to  
change the prefix from [Start] to [MG].

If there are no objections, I'll do that tomorrow(ish). I doubt there  
will be objections... this is more of a warning for people who may be  
auto-filing messages based on the prefix.
