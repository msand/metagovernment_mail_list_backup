If the "rust filter" is personal (as I think you suggested) and not
central, then I agree with the solution.  Here again, it's a socially
mediated solution (free interpretation) as opposed to a systematic
one.  The raw, unfiltered results are still visible to everyone else.

As long as the system doesn't hide or distort the truth, then I think
we'll be OK.  It's only when the machine starts making the decisions
for us that we get into trouble.  I think that's the status quo:
society is a machine out of control and we have no say in it.  We
pretend that we do because it makes us feel better, but it's untrue.

Mike
