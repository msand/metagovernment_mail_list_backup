Sorry to sound like a broken record, but this question doesn't seem to be getting much traction. Stated simply:

1. Do you see value in creating a formal entity called the Metagovernment Project?*

2. Do you agree with this simple document as the basis of an organizational charter?
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Charter/Draft1

If there is enough agreement on the above, then I'd like to begin exploring what legal structure we can use to create the organization.


* - My purpose in proposing it is both so we can be eligible to apply for grant funding and thus fund project-wide initiatives and also so we can have a real-world case study (ourselves) to prove and test the capabilities of leaderless organizational governance.
