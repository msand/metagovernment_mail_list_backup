Hey Alex,

yes I agree with everything you wrote.

> Dont you think in case of complete long time inactivity of a voter who 
> voted for proposal A and B, vote for A should rust faster than vote 
> for B, in case of proposal A was changed by a lot of lines through 
> editing ...

Only here: I would not make it dependent on the number of changed lines, 
but let the author tell his voters if it was a minor or major change. 
Because as Pietro pointed out, a single word can change everthing 
completely. If he says "major change" the rusting accelerates faster 
than if he says "minor change". And if he cheats, i.e. lies, he cheats 
on his voters and they will turn away.

Thomas
