On Dec 1, 2009, at 4:21 AM, Pietro Speroni di Fenizio wrote:

> So, how could I use Vilfredo for sport?
> Should I start a room called "football" and ask: who will win the  
> next world championship?

It should be something that will have a real-world impact, not just an  
opinion-measure.

So I would suggest something like, in the context of a particular  
recreational sport club:
- when and where should our next practice be?
- what other clubs should we compete against this season?
- who should be our goalie?
- who should buy the beer after the next game?
- what bar should we drink at after the next practice?

You know... important stuff. :)

If that team likes the process, it could eventually expand out to the  
league for decisions about season schedules and field rental budgets  
and so forth.

These sorts of real-world, immediately-relevant issues are likely to  
get much more involvement than esoteric questions.
