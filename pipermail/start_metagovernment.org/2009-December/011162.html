2009/12/4 Ed Pastore <epastore at metagovernment.org>:
> I have already written a new proposal in Generation 4 which incorporates the
> parts of the below which I like and removes those I do not.
>
> I very much like the idea of giving immediate automated feedback on writing
> quality, and I have significantly expanded upon that idea in my proposal
> (also incorporating and synthesizing some ideas from other proposals along
> the way).
>
> I do not necessarily support the Karma idea. I think it may be useful, but I
> do not think it is immediately necessary to solving this problem. So I
> synthesized that as well: Other proposals suggested applying automated
> complexity-scores to writing, so I propose sorting proposals by that score.
> That achieves the same effect as the Karma system, but greatly simplifies
> both the programming and the end-user experience (ie, nobody needs to bother
> with karma votes).
>
> In my proposal, I also integrated yet more proposals which favor using
> abstracts. I think my new proposal has a very good shot at building
> consensus, as it is a strong synthesis of numerous other proposals
> (including several which did not make it into the 4th generation).
>
