> after talking about the open network of voting tools way to long (mostly on

> the votes of the Nationbuilder-Sites (USA, Canada, Australia) over to 
> Votorola. That means: now you can vote in any of these 3 tools and your 
> vote will correctly be displayed in Votorola. (It takes about 2-6 hours for
