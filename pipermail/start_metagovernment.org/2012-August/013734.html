What kind of board? A board of elected politicians? Do you really think

Do you really think that the crowd is not able to do that by itself?

It has staffs and staff managers? WTF?!? :-)

It has a "party mass", but shouldnt that be a private thing? Everyone
should do as he pleases, and if people want to meet, they could...

It has a presidium which has many different goals to acomplish. Some of
them sound legit, but are not... and others are just strange.
It says it should do "youth work" and "editorial work" ...the only thing
that sounds reasonable at first is... it should develop and host the EIPW,
but as its such an important tool, it should be open source and not bound
to a comittee to foster development.

Compared to what is reality today, your proposal is visionary, but even
compared to the internal processes of the pirate party and their "liquid
feedback 2.0"-system, i'm not sure if you could still call EIPW visionary.

I mean, i don't want to be mean, it's just my opinion.

I think your goals are not ambitious enough and some of your goals cannot
be reached with such a conservative approach as you layed out.
