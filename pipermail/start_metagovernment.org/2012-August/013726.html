Hello there,

how far are your plans to buy out Facebook? In the mean time you may 
want to take a look at the Electronical Infrastructure for Political 
Work (EIPW) 
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Electronic_Infrastructure_for_Political_Work_%28EIPW%29. 
It's functionality is inspired by Facebook (and XING, StudiVZ and all 
the other social networks), plus it solves some of the problems Facebook 
has, if being used as a political tool.

Major advantage: it's free! No need for buying out.

So far it's all in German, but it's ready for translation (text strings 
are separate from code).

If you want to play with it, here's a living demo-system (German only, 
so far):

http://www.qanav.com/eipwTst/
