Hello,

when I started out with my concept 10 years ago, the nucleus of my 
thoughts was, that we need to get more people to participate in the 
political process, independent from the solution. And to get Joe Sixpack 
and John Dough on the boat requires three things from the solution:

It must be simple
It must be transparent and
It must be rewarding

So, sorry guys, as much as I admire the intellectual beauty of your 
concepts: for Joe and John most of your concepts are too brainy, they 
will not understand and therefore they will stay away. But not only will 
they refuse to participate, they will not note give their votes to a 
party they don't understand. And this is not only true for Joe and John, 
I've spoken to some people that do have the intellectual capacities to 
understand all these concepts and still refuse to participate and that 
is because they shy away from anything experimental. They want to see it 
actually work, before they participate. All this ends up in an endless 
loop where the concepts never proof their righteousness because no-one 
participates, because the concepts never proof their righteousness ... .

So, as a result, so far none of your efforts will bear fruits, because 
democracy is about majorities and in Germany that means to get 33 
million people to support you!

If you don't believe me and my perception of our comrade citizens, 
there's a simple test: try to convince your next door neighbour with 
your concept, or, even better, your grandmother. I did it, it was a very 
frustrating lesson, but I can claim that I know what I am talking about. 
And, by the way: I also participated in a conventional party and from 
that experience I can say: in some points you are drastically 
underestimating the complexity of the political process, be it 
grassroots or not.

This is why I came to the conclusion to try to correct the current 
system rather than to replace it. The advantage: by recognizing familiar 
structures people are more willing to accept the new.

The path I can sees is: once we managed to get more and more people to 
participate in what is their very own belonging: their community and 
education has become so much better, that even John and Joe are ready to 
understand even more complex concepts, then comes the time for Votorola 
and all the other, nice concepts.

For me the goal is to get these 33 millions on board, no matter what the 
solution looks like. If any one has some thoughts on how to get there, 
please share!

If you are interested: I got some ideas!

@fragro: very interesting, but to brainy for grandma!
