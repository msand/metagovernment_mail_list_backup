Hello Alex,

it's funny how close we are in our thoughts and still so far apart. I do 
not have the time to dig into the entire conversation, but there's a few 
things I would like to say:

First of all, if you could loosen on your aggression attitude you could 
use my thoughts as a source to either gain new insights and find new 
solutions or prove the rightness of your thoughts. And we could help 
each other to improve our knowledge.

I am not interested in any intellectual duel for the same reason why I 
shy away from working in workshops: too much energy wasted in fighting 
each other instead of making use of that energy for fruitful, 
constructive work.

Fruitful, constructive work comes in questioning. And the answers to 
these questions either reveal the mistake that has been made or proves 
the rightness of the concept. If there's anything that raises your 
curiosity I am more than willing to explain, but I'm not spending my 
time on any fruitless discussion on who is right.

So let me state my question: do you think this discussion will lead to 
any result that is worth the energy for you and me or the audience?
