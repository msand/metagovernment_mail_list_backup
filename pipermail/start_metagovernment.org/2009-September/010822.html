Malmo09: the popular conference

The first popular European e-government conference takes place in
Malmö, Sweden 19-20 November 2009. It coincides with the 5th official
EU e-gov event in the same city. The popular Malmo09 event aims to
offer a memorable creative statement of what Europeans really want
from e-enabled government.

Anyone interested is welcome to apply to speak, participate contribute
ideas or help in any other way. It is particularly aimed at European
digital-rights organisations, consumer advocates, and those with a
political, academic, artistic or design interest in e-government. No
presentation will last longer than eight minutes. Music, pictures and
video content is welcome. The event will close with awards for the
"pertinent art" which best expresses popular feeling about
e-government, and for the best independently coded or mashed-up
projects based on public data or public services.
