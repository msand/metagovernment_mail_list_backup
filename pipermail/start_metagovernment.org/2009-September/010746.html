Recently a segment on "shared space" aired on one of the national TV news
programs here in the U.S.:  http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shared_space 

 

While it is hard for those of us who live in highly populated, urban areas
to believe it could work in our communities, it is good to know that new
ways of encouraging responsible behavior are being explored.  Hopefully,
they will help to shape the formation of move livable communities in the
future.

 

It is interesting to contemplate the implications for online communities as
well as online tools designed to support physical communities.
