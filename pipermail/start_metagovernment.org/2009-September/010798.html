Yesterday finally a talk show which is very uncomfortable for Berlusconi was
allowed to air.

One of my friends on facebook is also a journalist. He has seen the show
while on chat with other netizens, and has today wrote an article on
L'Espresso (one of Italian major weekly newspapers). You can find the
article here:
http://espresso.repubblica.it/dettaglio/annozero-visto-da-voi/2110717&ref=hpsp

What I was thinking is:
would it be really hard to have a chat program where a person can, on the
spot, while it is going, just click on phrases written by others, if they
agree on them. Something simple, fast and functional. Maybe it already
exists. Maybe there is an open source chat program out there which could be
modified to add this functionality. Could it be an IRC add on? Does IRC have
adds on?

Of course the phrases that are voted would move on the side, where they
scroll slower. A bit like the mountains from a train (poetic image). In fact
the more a phrase have been clicked, the slower it should scroll.

Your thoughts,
