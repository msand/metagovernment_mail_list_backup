What people do in those cases is to accept extended abstracts. And
then offer them talks. If we keep the video of the talk it would be a
good compromise.

Another solution: The non academic is usually called a workshop
alongside. And is usually easier to get in. But if we have extended
abstracts is that really necessary?

Also do not think people not in academia have no interest in papers.
If you want money from funding agencies EVEN if you are in the private
sector you need to specify if it is for research or for application.
Research is paid more (75% vs 50%) but you need to prove you have done
research before.
