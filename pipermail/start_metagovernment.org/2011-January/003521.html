Well, most of the time I wanted to spend on replying to the  
interesting threads on this list has instead been devoted to fighting  
new spammers who have been swamping the site:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Special:RecentChanges

I just installed VisualMathCaptcha:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Special:Version
http://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Extension:VisualMathCaptcha
as an add-on to ConfirmEdit:
http://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Extension:ConfirmEdit

I'm not that happy about it, as it is another barrier to  
participation, and it is a little confusing to deal with. I considered  
other captcha options, but they weren't working out due to limitations  
of the hosting provider or, in the case of FancyCaptcha (which seems  
nice), the requirement that I register it with Google.

So I just wanted to let you know it is there. I haven't had time to  
even test it properly, but I believe you will see it in one of two  
cases:
1. if you try to make a new account
2. if you try to post a link to an external site and have not  
confirmed your e-mail address.

Please fell free to test those or other use cases. I'll try to do so  
soon, but cannot right now.

Thanks.
