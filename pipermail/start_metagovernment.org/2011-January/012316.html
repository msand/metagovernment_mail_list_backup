Welcome, Thiago!

We haven't been ignoring you... this list just tends to focus on  
direct questions at the moment. Are you in the process of building a  
party now?

A lot of us are not particularly party-oriented. They way I se  
collaborative governance evolving is as a ground-up gradual  
replacement for current systems, not an internal replacement. But  
there is certainly a lot of value to at least trying the approaches of  
DemoEx, PDI, Activ Demokrati, and Senator Online. I would note as I  
did in my previous post that the reason DemoEx appears to have been  
successful is that they thought small. Trying to take over the  
government of Brazil is a gigantic task. Trying to get a foot into  
your town council is much more plausible in the current landscape.

Regardless, whatever approach you take, please post more here and let  
us know. We would be happy support you as we can.
