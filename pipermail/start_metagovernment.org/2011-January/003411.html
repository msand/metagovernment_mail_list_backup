It was incredibly fun to see this story on Slashdot about disempowering admins!

About 5 years ago I've written my sociology thesis about control structures in IT, and how they always seem to lead to admins and hierarchies.

After that I started working for the University of Twente in the CS department and I've always wanted to continue working on it, but could never find the appropriate form... in the end the topic is about norms and values and these are not in high esteem in the CS community.

An alternative would be a survey paper - research how companies now counter the almighty admin (think insider threat problem) and the propose a solution - how a collective of admins could improve security and lower operational cost.

At the same time, maybe it would be fun to just build something - and see how it could work.

In the Netherlands there are funding options for such projects, consider for example NLnet. http://www.nlnet.nl/foundation/can_do.html

Concluding, I did not figure out completely how this site and its organization works, but I am willing to put some time and effort in realizing some of the ideas behind it.
