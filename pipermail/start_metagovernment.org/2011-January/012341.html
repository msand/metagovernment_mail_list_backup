thread have also been doing."  And I do mean it.

Especially the statistics that said that:

"If 2% votes "yes" and 1% votes "no", it will be resolved in 700 days.

note that for decades already, we have been developing distributed 
collaboration support tools and platforms, including our knowledge 
resource entitlement, modeling, management, and sharing DNAOS 
thechnology, that we are currently using to support StratML applications.

Just for fun, I will add that many years already, realizing the 
importance of the design of our DNAOS knowledge resource entitlement 
technology, we have been trying (e.g. working, paying, opening-up our 
IP) to contribute to new knowledge sharing and management standards 
(e.g. OMG) that could really enable a knowledge and information economy, 
for generations to come, and have been systematically out-voted by all 
the IT majors (and governments that base their IT decisions on advices

mayhem, apart from being afraid of a leveled playing field.  They all 
each still hope to "win" and that everyone will adopt their 
(ill-defined) ways.  Still we are not too worried about that, as indeed 
it could take more than 20000 years before Google adopts Microsoft's

In the mean time many are suffering, and as the industrial economy is 
saturating, and not providing the required wealth and growth anymore, if

But, we are out-voted, out-financed, and the "majority" wins.

Thank you also for the reference and I will at least keep myself posted
