I was contemplating "anonymous polls" today.  Let's say we have a
group of people that all knows and trust each other, 100 of them.

1 person decides there's an elephant in the room and asks a question.
It is hated by all and even though the group is tolerant they punish
him in some way, perhaps socially, for posing the poll.

What if he could pose it anonymously, then?  And everyone could chime
in anonymously?

Would this not be useful?  Perhaps in my example group anonymous polls
don't "count" and won't effect an outcome, but they could be useful
for gathering opinion.
