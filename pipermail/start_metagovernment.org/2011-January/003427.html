On December 8, the White House Open Government Initiative and General
Services Administration with the support of OMB requested your input
in helping us design a next generation public engagement platform
where public officials pose questions to citizens and obtain useful
and informed contributions for consideration and action.

Because the online consultation has been so informative, we are
extending the comment period by two weeks until January 23, 2011 and
encourage you to participate and to invite others. Keep in mind you
can follow additions and changes to the consultation by going to
expertnet.wikispaces.com and clicking on Notify Me.

http://expertnet.wikispaces.com/Background+and+Principles+Editable

An official at the Department of Innovation would solicit this
expertise from the public in a four step process:

Step 1 : Setting up a topic with public consultation questions;
Step 2: Distributing questions to professional networks;
Step 3: Facilitating structured, online responses to those questions; and
Step 4: Synthesizing public input and returning feedback.
