To whom it may concern,

the Pirate Party Germany, in particular the working group "AG
Meinungsfindungstool" wants to invite developers and people who are
interested in the topic, to join them in a mumble conference.
If you know people who develop tools that foster eDemocracy and the like
(discussion, voting, ...) please feel free to forward this email to them.

Every developer or person in general, who wants to advocate a specific tool
or a specific approach is invited to share their knowledge and thoughts on
the topic.

Please help us to find the perfect time for this meeting and register here:
http://doodle.com/m86qghm4hy5ehty5#table

We would also encourage you to join the working groups mailing list:
https://service.piratenpartei.de/listinfo/ag-meinungsfindungstool


Thank you very much for your attention
Alexander Praetorius

"Michael Allen", "Conseo" - *Developers of Votorola*
