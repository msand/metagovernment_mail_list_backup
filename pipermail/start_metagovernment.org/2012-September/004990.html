spambot.  Possible solutions: (1) disallow usernames ending in
numbers: http://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Extension:TitleBlacklist

http://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Extension:ConfirmAccount

required approval.  Not sure this is possible.
