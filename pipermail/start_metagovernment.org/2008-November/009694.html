Thank you for the info. 

Site http://mexnap.info/  is aimed at developing and promoting direct democracy mechanisms among citizens of the Former Soviet Union. We are distributing and developing theoretical materials on the subject anв seeking to build a coalition of citizens and political forces sharing the ideas of direct democracy.

In summary, our vision is as follows: mobile communications transition the world to direct democracy: citizens directly vote on laws, budgets and performance of government leaders.

Direct democracy of ancient societies became impossible due to population growth and lack of communications. Modern mobile technologies allow people to connect instantly and effortlessly. Opinions of individual citizens on any subject of public life can be quickly and easily aggregated. Voice of the people can now be heard directly. Transition to direct democracy is driven by the technological change and is inevitable. Representative democracy is an anachronism of the XXI century.

By the direct democracy we mean the following:
    * All legislative initiatives are approved by a referendum before becoming a law
    * Any legislator can be dismissed at any time as a result of a continuous referendum by constituents
    * Ministers are approved by a referendum
    * Rewards (or punishment) of government leaders are determined as a result of a popular vote
    * Make up of national budgets is determined by preferences of citizens
    * Production is largely planned on the basis of direct consumer orders

We are developing a system of informal voting on existing laws, legislative proposals, and actions of execute powers of all levels. In the future, such a system will rely on mobile communication devices to ease the voting process.
