*Kick-Start the Movement to Make an Internet
Connection a Right of Every American *

InternetforEveryone.org announced Los Angeles as the location for our first
town hall meeting on the future of America's Internet.

The meeting -- to be held on Dec. 6 on the USC campus -- is the first in a
nationwide series of public conversations to get every American connected to
an open, fast and affordable Internet. The event is designed to bring
together people from across the city to formulate a broadband plan for the
new administration and Congress.
