The authors of the video haven't a clue about Anarchism so their
conclusions about it are irrelevant. However they are technically correct
about America NOT being a democracy. But then I argue that no other
country on the planet is a democracy (with the possible exception of
Switzerland which gets closest).

There is nothing mystical about what democracy is: People Power is a
simple concept. It means WE make the decisions. NOT we choose who make the
decisions. Voting is the least important part of the process and marks a
failure of the process because a vote only becomes necessary if we fail to
reach consensus.

The debating process - through which we attempt to reach consensus - is by
far the most important element of democracy because it is how the ideas
get aired and improved, refined or rejected. And the most important
elements of the debating process are to ensure that the dissenting voices,
the minority voices and the voices of the weak are all heard with the same
clarity as the voices of the articulate, the bombastic and the popular.

A quick word on the Nolan chart. I prefer the original because it
emphasises the importance of the polarity between Liberty and Authority.
The Nolan chart blurs or even avoids that distinction.
