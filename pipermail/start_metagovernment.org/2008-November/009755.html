But also a man and a woman who don't want to be married but want to
have a "civil union" can. So I have two friends of mine who have a
family. But they are not married in church, not in front of the state,
but made this "civil union". I know they are an open couple. Very
stable and with 2 beautiful kids. I am not sure what their rights are.
How is it different from the "being married in front of the state"
case. But I have the sensation that if more and more options were
available, to everybody, a lot of the pressure would be relieved for
everybody.
