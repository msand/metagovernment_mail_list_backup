I'm not so great at introductions, so here I go.

I found the site and this list through Slashdot,  and I'm very interested in
it.  I'm an anarchist, a real one, not one who just advocates breaking
things.  What got me interested in your project is the focus on consensus as
a method of governing, something which I hold as a central part of my
anarchist ethics.  I hold a degree in philosophy, and I focused on political
philosophy.

The one aspect of your project I'm confused, and sceptical, about is the use
of synthesis and what exactly that means.  It seems like that could be the
path to a government that no one wants and yet everyone "agrees to".

I'll have a lot more to say at later times, but I thought it best if I
introduce myself before I ramble on too much.
