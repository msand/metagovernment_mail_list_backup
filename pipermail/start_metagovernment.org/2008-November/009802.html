I don't see the need for having both Disagree and Veto.

My thoughts are to give Members the ability to +1 or -1 a Proposal.

The "positive" and "negative" could be described a number of ways:

+ : Agree / Approve / Yes / Yea / Jaa
- : Disagree / Deny / Veto / No / Nay / Nix

I'm fine with whatever decision is made, I'm just sticking to the point of
two options: +1, or -1.

!1 doesn't make any sense to me -- being the non-math type that I am.
