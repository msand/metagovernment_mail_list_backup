Hello people,

I write you because with another friend of the list I'm coordinating a
workshop in Madrid, about collaborative work, and maybe somebody here
could be interested.
The workshop will be hold at the Medialab-Prado, in the Interactivos
research line, from 15 to 27th of April. Very briefly, the idea of the
project is to develop an internet debate platform that allow thousands
of people to debate at the same time, without it generating chaos and
noise as occurring in traditional discussion tools. All the
information can be found here:

http://incomaproject.org/call-for-collaborators-interactivos13-tools-for-a-read-write-world-10-27-april/
http://medialab-prado.es/article/convocatoria_colaboradores_lgru
[English version clicking in in the upper right button]
http://medialab-prado.es/article/interactivos13_proyectos_seleccionados
[English version clicking in in the upper right button] [project under
the title "Incoma. Exploting the collective' of the collective
intelligence"]

If somebody wants to join, you are welcome. And we will be very
pleased if you can send the info to other people you think could be
interested on the idea. Or suggest us other names to write to.

Thank you very much!
