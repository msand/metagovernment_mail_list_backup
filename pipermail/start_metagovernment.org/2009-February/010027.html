Hello! I just finished editing the new Community Portal. I hope you find it
useful, visit it with certain periodicity, and make edits to it. I want this
portal to be our "operation center", it condenses: a to-do list,
resolutions, meetings, administrative roles.

While Metascore's PrototypeA is being tuned up, consider taking resolutions
through the wiki; I wanted to try a different approach for this after our
past failed intents of producing decisions. This time the resolution to
solve a problem is taken in the same page of the article that holds that
problem. This gives to the debate much more visibility than two separate
pages: the article and the resolution referring that article. Nowadays you
shouldn't miss the "Forming a formal organization" debate, you can access to
it —of course— from the Community Portal ;)

Bye!
