>If anyone else is interested in seeding this, here's a stab at the
>prerequisites.  This is to develop an open framework for proposing and
>tracking standards (it might be a first, even the IETF wasn't *this*
>radically open):
>
>  1. Technical XML and RDF.  This is for the design of the distributed
>     compliance records (distributed among the projects).  Also for
>     specifying the Wiki requirements, and the format of the master
>     lists (projects and standards).
>
>  2. Technical scripting of the query engine.  The engine will
>     assemble the compliance records, and run the queries.
>
>  3. Management.  A project manager to help smooth out the bumps, and
>     bring in resources.  We'll need a couple of pages on a neutral
>     Wiki, for the master lists.  We may also need a bit of a
>     promotion lift.
>
>    (The plan may change.  It hasn't yet been researched.  And other
>     ideas may be proposed...)
>
>An alpha would take roughly 2 man-weeks of effort.  I can do (1) or
>(2), and bring in Votorola.  We need at least one other project that
>has running code.  Who else can commit?

DemocracyLab would be willing to participate, but we won't have any coding
resources to devote to this for at least a month or two.
