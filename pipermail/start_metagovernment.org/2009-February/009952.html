be built.  Then you'll pile into it, and go sailing off... but to

steer.

  http://zelea.com/project/votorola/d/theory.xht

  http://lists.electorama.com/pipermail/election-methods-electorama.com/2009-February/024194.html

highest priority task.  (Get it wrong, and your ship won't be sailing
anywhere pleasant.)  Is this being done?
