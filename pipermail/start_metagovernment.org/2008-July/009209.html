I'm Oriel.
I would like to inform you that we, at http://myvote.org.il (Hebrew) started
translating the Basic principles of MetaGorvement, in this wiki
pages<http://myvote.org.il/index.php?title=%D7%9E%D7%98%D7%90-%D7%9E%D7%9E%D7%A9%D7%9C>.
Myvote is part of the Israeli Direct Democracy Movement.
One of our main issues is the developing of Direct/Open/Dynamic democracy
platform. Personally, I'm a favor of "Dynamic Democracy"(or Liquid
democracy) which is a dynamic mixture of Direct democracy and Representative
democracy, with these basic rules:

1. Every citizen can become a representative on his choice of domains
2. Every citizen may choose a representative, while accepting all
representative domains, or specific domains. (He can change representatives
or "cancel" them as his representatives)
3. Every citizen can participate on any vote, even if his representative
also participates. Whenever citizen vote, his vote cancels the specific vote
made for him by his representative.
4. It is always possible to make all of your votes by yourself, without
having any representatives. (Pure direct democracy)

Needless to say, Radical Transparency, Open source platform, etc...
