Every change to some piece will have a revision number, by virtue of
being in a repository. If in a discussion you want to talk about a
change made to some legislation, you can use a revision tag. If
however, you want to refer to a piece of legislation rather than any
particular revision, you would use the legislation tag.

Kinda like we refer to Wikipedia entries, without being sure that what
the person who clicks on the link will actually be what you originally
saw when you sent it. Wikipedia also has the ability to link to
particular revisions I think.

Mercurial can also mark specific revisions, so if you want to specify
a version number for some piece of legislation, we could also add a
tags for that.
