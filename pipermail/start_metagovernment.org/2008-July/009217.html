Another question about Manuel's Prototype (but more generally  
something that is applicable to any instance of Metascore) - about  
version vs revision.

Manuel, currently you are suggesting
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/PrototypeA#Tagging
that legislation be given a unique ID, but then that revisions of a  
bill be given rev numbers of that ID.

I don't see how we can distinguish between what is a revision of a  
bill and what is a complete change. Even if someone only changes one  
word, they might radically change the meaning and/or intent of the  
whole bill.

Wouldn't it be easier for every resolution to have an independent ID,  
and for people to associate them with mutual tags?

A more complex version of the above is what I was suggesting with  
Synthesis Scoring:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Scoring_system#Synthesis_scoring
