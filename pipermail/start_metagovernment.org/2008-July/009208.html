FWIW, today I have been working on a message to the Debian community,  
inviting them to have a look at the project. They have a reputation in  
both governance and open source philosophy, so I would imagine that  
some of their developers may want to see what we are up to.

I am planning on some other limited publicity efforts as well. I don't  
think we have cause to publicize to the mass media, so I am mostly  
directed toward communities of people who may be able to contribute at  
this stage (programmers, mathematicians, wiki editors, philosophers,  
etc.). For example, see:
http://www.mwusers.com/forums/showthread.php?t=8610
https://sourceforge.net/forum/forum.php?thread_id=2123606&forum_id=710

I would encourage other members of this group to do the same, if you  
are able. I should note that other members (and random other people,  
apparently) are in fact already doing such publicity in various  
venues; largely in discussion forums, blogs, and blog comments.
