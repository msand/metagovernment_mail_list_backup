Dear All,

apologies for cross-posting, but I would like to invite you to join us
at CeDEM16 in Krems, Austria or to submit a paper to CeDEM Asia 2016:

CeDEM16: 10 Years of e-Democracy Conferences (2007 - 2016)
18 - 20 May 2016, Danube University Krems. Austria.
www.donau-uni.ac.at/cedem16 

The CeDEM16 conference provides 3 days of programme including:
* 4 keynotes (Mila Gascó on "Co-Production and ICT-enabled
Co-Production"; Hans Jochen Scholl on "Profiling the Academic Domain of
Digital Democracy and Government"; Efthimios Tambouris on
"Multidimensional Open Government Data"; Robert Krimmer on "Is there an
Estonian E-Democracy? Co-Creation in the Digital State")
* 6 Workshops (http://tinyurl.com/cedemworkshops)
* Panel Discussion on "Co-Creation" (*new!*)
* 25 full paper presentations
* Reflections Session
* PhD Colloquium
* Open Space (*opportunities to contribute a presentation, workshop,
etc. available up until the start of the Open Space!*)
* Best Paper Prize: 5 nominees, find out who this year's winner is on
Day 2!

CeDEM16 Registration: http://bit.ly/1XuKJUm

Looking forward to seeing you in Krems or in Daegu!
