People,

There a number of functions that require compensation.  This will often
involve money.  So maybe not after we all have abundance.  This coming year
I will want to look at a micropay donation system for funding.  Figure that
discussion moderation can have a strong money component.  Assume this is
what will work in a transitive representation scheme and try to figure out
how to tune it to deal with the shills and trolls.  Easy enough, but always
temporary.

Max
