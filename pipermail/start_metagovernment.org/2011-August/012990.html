Can I at least get some feedback on the tone and scope of the movie?

What I have scripted so far follows this outline:
- Problems with representative democracy
- Traditional problems with direct democracy
- Introduction to collaborative governance
- Some introduction to some member projects, perhaps


This is turning out to possibly be somewhat long. How long should a  
movie like this be? I was initially thinking of 5 minutes, but I  
wonder if 15 or even 30 minutes would be more engaging. Or would it  
just be boring?

Another approach would be to make it a series of three or four 5- 
minute movies; one about each of the points above.

What do you think?
