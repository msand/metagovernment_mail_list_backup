Maybe there's a way to answer Thomas without going into technical
details, disclosing competitive secrets, etc.  If you're willing, I'd
like to ask you a series of questions regarding (a) the aims of WH2;
(b) the general technical facilities to achieve those aims; and (c)
the steps that are involved once the facilities are in place.  I could
ask these questions of any project, but I'll start with you.  Aside
from Telematics Freedom, which is currently bound to silence, yours is
the project that I know least about.

The idea is to discover potential points of synergy, where WH2 can
collaborate with other projects.  Synergy is not entirely a question
of aims (I agree with you and Pietro) but let's begin there:

  a) What is the aim of the project?
 
So for example, in regard to Votorola, I would answer that the aim is
a direct democracy (plus).  I would then add a few sentences of
explanation, defining roughly what I mean by a direct democracy (and
by the "plus").  I might say that I mean the transfer of decision
making from government and political parties to the general
population, such that people at large decide who holds official power,
what policies they pursue, what plans they enact, and what laws they
pass.  (By the "plus", I might say the same thing, but with regard to
socio-cultural issues that are normally beyond the scope of
governmental power.  These too would be deliberately decided by people
at large.)

Can you answer in the same pattern?  (Can other projects answer, too?
Owen, is this a good approach to take, in response to Thomas's plea?)
