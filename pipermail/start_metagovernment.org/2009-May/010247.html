The WH2 platform will be called NationBuilder, so I'm going to start using
that name here.

It's kind of hard for me to lay out the goal, mostly because there are goals
on a lot of different levels, but also because I'm still figuring out how to
succinctly communicate my vision.  But here goes..

I want to provide tools for people to organize themselves democratically
(democracy, not anarchy) online and make things happen.  I'm less concerned
with figuring out the proper way to put together a bill, and more concerned
with connecting people and resources together to actually do things, whether
the context is their city, their country, or their company, doesn't matter.

To use more business-like language, I believe the successful endeavors of
the future will be all about bringing all the "stakeholders" together,
whether it's citizens, employees, management, customers, shareholders,
whoever, into a network that can achieve things that weren't possible before
in a top down management system.  A "nation" is the metaphor I'm using to
pull this concept together into something I can evangelize, hence the name,
NationBuilder.

Jim
