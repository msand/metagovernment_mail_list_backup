> So, up today a network of trust is the only feasible solution to the

The more I think about it, the more sense this makes. After all...  
identity is other people knowing we are who they think we are. So in  
essence, identity is nothing but a question of trust.

this person before me now the same person I know from before." Is  
there a way to verify consistency-of-identity by one's network of trust?
