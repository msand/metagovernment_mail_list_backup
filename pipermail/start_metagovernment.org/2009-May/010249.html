This conversation, and your example Ed, have changed the way I think about
vote delegation. Now I don't like it. Anyway, to enrich this discussion I
would like to speak in its favor: Without it you can't empower someone you
really know and trust. People who really aren't interested in get involved
will desire to be represented in some cases, not to participate but to feel
like they do.
