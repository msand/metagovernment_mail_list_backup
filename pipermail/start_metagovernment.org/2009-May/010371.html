Voltaire said: "Don't let the perfect be the enemy of the Good".

For sure, we have to build proper security. As I said, a combination of what
you have, what you know and what you are, may be good enough for
authenticating transactions.  When all 10 finger prints are stored in the
smart card, the chances of failure can be minimized. Iris can be an
alternative where the finger prints fail.
