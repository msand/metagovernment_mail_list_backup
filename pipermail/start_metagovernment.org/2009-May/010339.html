That's great guys. Given the "openness in everything" principle of the  
Metagovernment project, it should be assumed that Metascore is also  
open for all. Just to be clear, though, we're licensing it under the  
Affero GPL
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Metascore#License
which is a viral copyleft, if I understand the terminology correctly.  
So others are welcome to reuse it as long as they too give it away  
freely.

As an aside to the Metascore team... the Affero GPL site now  
recommends switching to the GNU Affero GPLv3.
http://www.affero.org/oagpl.html (see note at top of page)
Any objections to moving over?
