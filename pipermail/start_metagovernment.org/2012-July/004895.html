Hello,

I'd like to share with you my latest work and projects :

I developed demopolitique.fr a crowdsolving tool made to allow a p2p
governance by instrumentalising dialectic. Sadly it's in french, except for
this disucssion : http://demopolitique.com/node/71

I'd like to share about it, and i'm also looking for help to further
develop the platform which is a technical challenge.

Demopolitique (as the city of people's affairs) is a possible answer to the
actual need of alternate forms of governance.
It's a platform aiming to use new technologies to instrumentalise dialectic
with tools to collaboratively enhance and document arguments, projects,
policies...
Actually, a first prototype has been released around political issues like
education and capitalism and also around the idea of démopolitique.
A lot of tools are designed and waiting further help to facilitate and
reward constructive contributions and collaboratively resolve problems in
various fields and scales.
This project is meant to be a social serious game, using reward systems
inspired from community games where when you win, you act for the common
good.
