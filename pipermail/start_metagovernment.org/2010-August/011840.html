Thomas and I have confused everybody, because (despite the thread
title, and the poll description) the issue of our positions (if Thomas
agrees) is no longer the long-term ownership of the domain name.  We
only care about the transfer of control to the public.  What happens
*after* the transfer is a separate issue, a separate poll.  So it is
decoupled, in that sense.

Things I would like to do to clarify this (if consensus looks
possible):
  1) Rename "caretaker" to "namegiver"
  2) Rename "supersession" to "donation"
  3) Make purpose/scope clearer in the poll description/title.
But the immediate problem is lack of consensus on this very purpose.

On decoupling in general: I really believe we must discuss the
problems we are trying to solve, before looking at detailed solutions.
So just as for the decision rules above, I recommend starting a new
thread with a question like this: "In situation S, we can forsee that
X will happen?  Isn't X a *problem*?  Don't we need a solution for
it?"
