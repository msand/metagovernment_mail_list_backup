Here's some more information on the online voting tool being developed by
the new economics foundation, this time from Stephen Whitehead. I said I'd
pass on his offer to share the software with the list, since I'm not into
the software development side of things.

At the moment, it’s in the form of a wordpress plugin which mimics a
traditional poll but with preference voting. It’s not yet perfect – it
requires voters to create a wordpress login which is a little fiddly, and
the reports could bear to be more comprehensive. However, we’re happy to
 share the software on an open-source basis with anyone who wanted to
develop it further.

Stephen Whitehead
Project Manager: Democracy and Participation, *nef
*stephen.whitehead at neweconomics.org
