I don't remember exactly where the thread is at the moment, but  
recently Michael and I debated on this list for a while and  
synthesized the term "real democracy."

The nice things about this:

1. It contains the word democracy, which seems important and which is  
much more resonant than "governance."

2. It implies that everything else branding itself as some kind of  
"democracy" is a lesser imitation... which is only too true.


I re-added this and one other ("electronic direct democracy") to the  
Vilfredo question:
http://vilfredo.org/viewquestion.php?q=53
