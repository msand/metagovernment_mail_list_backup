Alright but if you had a boss (partner) that told you to vote a certain way
for the good of the company (family) and they could look up to see if you
did in fact, vote that way. What would the retribution be? The company
won't be punished for pushing their own agenda, as little influence as it
might have. I think there needs to be something on the books that protects
a voter so they can have something to fall back on and say I won't vote
this way, it's illegal. You know?
