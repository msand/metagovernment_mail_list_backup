Anyone have a list of categories that can be used to classify
laws/policies? One that I know of (but can't find) was the delegation
areas in LiquidFeedback: If anyone has that, I'd appreciate it if you
could send it to me (even if it's in German or whatever).

I'm thinking I need something like this to help organize bills and
make them more recognizable (vs just giving them a number like the
government currently does). What I've got so far is the following,
including the ability to combine them (for example a tax on tobacco
would be an "HT", though one might quibble about whether something
like this might also earn an "F" and/or and "M"):

D - defense
F - personal freedom
G - operation and role of government
H - healthcare
I - international relations
J - justice
L - land use
M - morality
S - standards
T - taxation
