There is no such thing as "lending out money".
"Lending out money" means private banks creating new money out of thin air
and giving it to whomever they want.
People paying for loans means destroying money.
People saving money on bank accounts means "freezing money", so that
chances are more money can be created
through loans, because the economy needs a replacement for the money that
was frozen.

Other than that - wallets can bake in a mechanism to slowly redistribute
money or devaluate money which is being hoarded.
Blockchain technology is free to do whatever you want. It's just a
decentralized and distributed database.
