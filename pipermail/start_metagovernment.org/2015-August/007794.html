I guess I'm on a roll here, but it seems to me that both implicitly
assume a leadership/representative based system. Which IMHO means they
are destined to fail, since the problem isn't the inability to
propagate good ideas, it's that leaders/representatives are not
interested in good ideas, they're interested in power. We need to
change the system such that it denies such people access to this
power, freeing the ideas so that they can work directly on the
citizens who would approve or reject them based on their fundamental
merits, not on their relationship to the power structure.
