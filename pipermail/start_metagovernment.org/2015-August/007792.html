design.

happiness.

process might change itself into happy process producing happy
outcomes. But who would be interested in that happy-happy process now ?

(Just as a note, pleasure-pleasure process is similar to the destruction
our civilization is producing now.)

Philosophically speaking subjectivity is everything. Objectivity is
just subjective imagination.
Practically we use it a little bit differently and when our tribe
broadly agrees about something we call it objective. So the line
between common subjective and objective is quite blurred.

Fortunatelly enough we can sum some things into categories listed under
the category of fundamental human needs
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fundamental_human_needs
where is broad agreement across tribes about need of production of items
in this categories.
I think we should start the process with this objective needs and
consider adding some more subjective needs.
