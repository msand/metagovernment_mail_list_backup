I have been very disappointed with some of the recent developments in the
metascore community. I relay believe in our basic principle of openness in
everything and that we should not make any exceptions to this.
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Basic_principles#Openness_in_everything

What troubles me is the fact that some of us are willing to sacrifice this
not for money but only the prospect of money. In February a thread was
started where it was mentioned that the FP7 proposal cannot be published
beforehand.
http://metagovernment.org/pipermail/start_metagovernment.org/2009-February/001225.html

At the time I voiced my concern but it seems that this has not changed much.
Work for FP7 has continued without full transparency to the public.
http://pareto.ironfire.org/viewquestion.php?q=19

I would like to ask some questions.
- Why do we need money for anything other then servers?
- Are the Basic Principles only for decoration?
