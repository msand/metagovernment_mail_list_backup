FWIW, I kind of like how it's structured in White House 2, although I'm a
little biased.  There the two concepts are basically merged.   Instead of a
separate "debate", you specify the pros and cons of a given priority or
"objective" to use the StratML language.   So, I support this priority, I
think we should do it, it's my priority #3, and here's why.
