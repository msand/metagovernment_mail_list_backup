Assumptions of modern Communist theory.
The paper considers the general situation of contemporary
communist theory - the original theoretical basis
(Marxism, Leninism, Stalinism), the basic categories
economic condition of society and category management
society, as well as assumptions, goals and directions of the Second
socialist revolution.

The paper pointed to the need for the development of Marxist -
