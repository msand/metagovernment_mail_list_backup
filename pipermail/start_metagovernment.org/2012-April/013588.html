Hi Ed,

I really like what you've put together here. It feels strong and direct....but not critical or pushy.

One line that I think needs work: 

"We are not interested in asking our politicians to change things for us. We are not interested in violence. Rather, we are creating a new system which can peacefully and gradually put the people in control of our own lives."  

The "We are not interested in violence." jumped out at me as being incongruent with the tone of the rest of it....like why would anyone think you would be interested in violence? In other words, I don't think it's necessary to say that. 

Also, I think "put the people in control of our own lives." should read "put the people in control of their own lives.".

I apologize for not being more active in recent months. I'm very busy these days with a project of my own. I love what you all are doing and hope to be back at some point soon.
