On Vilfredo we recently found an RCShell.

Also some time ago a spammer registered and even participated in a
discussion, by voting for an option. But he didn't just vote for a
random option. In fact it turned out the option he voted was the one
that won (and that would have won even without his help). In other
words behind spammers there are human beings too.
unfortunately.
