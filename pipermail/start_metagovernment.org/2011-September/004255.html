@Francis
I may agree that the principles of Votorola may be 
simple, but this is not the point, in my opinion.  
The point is that most of the people need a software 
which is simple to use and everybody can understand.
I have read many times the theory behind Votorola, I do 
not think I am a stupid, but I have still to understand many 
points.

software that many people will not want to learn as it is too complicated to

proposal of law and ask other people to vote it.  
As you can see from the link here: http://www.beppegrillo.it/2011/09/scaricare_ogni/index.html,
