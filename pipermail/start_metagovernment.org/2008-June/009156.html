Hello Ed, hello Paul,

I think we should not lose sight of where we are going to, and where
we are coming from.

A bureucrat is a term (you can also call him ted, the term is
irrelevant) which has some 'power' on a mediawiki platform.

http://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Setting_user_rights_in_MediaWiki#bureaucrat

If we don't need something, let's not scratch our head on that, and
just make everybody bureucrat.

Let us remember that our aim is NOT to have a democratic process
through the wiki, but to design our own democratic system. Inspired by
wiki and /. ratings. But neither of them. It is irrelevant who is a
bureucrat and who is not for a group of our size. Let us concentrate
on what system do we want, instead.

I have volunteered to be in charge of mindmaps for the project. If I
take the role I might need to have some ways to implement them, either
by adding some plugins to mediawiki, or by being in charge of a
subdomain name, or by by having a directory with ftp where I can
upload the maps and the map visualizers.

Cheers,
Pietro
