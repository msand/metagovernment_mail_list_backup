I am still not very familiar with the back-end of MediaWiki... I had  
thought bureaucrats were analogous to moderators on other systems:  
having some extended roles, but not full administrative capabilities.  
Apparently that's not quite the case, and I don't really understand  
what the MW bureaucrat is for. Why would one want a non-admin user who  
can make other people admins? Until we see a need for it, we can  
probably forego with the bureaucrat and just have admins of the wiki.

It certainly seems acceptable to have more than one admin. Wikipedia  
currently has over 1,500:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:List_of_administrators

So is anyone volunteering for any of these admin roles? You don't  
necessarily even have to do anything... just fill in if I become  
unavailable to the project for any reason. Though certainly you are  
welcome to be more proactive than that.
