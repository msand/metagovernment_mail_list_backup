Please create a user page for yourself on the wiki. That is just about  
the last thing needed before I can take the wiki live. Currently, the  
wiki looks like it is maintained only by me. For example, an educated  
MediaWiki user will look at the Recent Changes page and see that I am  
the only recent contributer:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Special:Recentchanges

If a few more people could at least fill out your user pages, it will  
give the wiki a lot more credibility. All you have to do is register  
on the wiki:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Special:Userlogin
(recommended you use your real name for your user name)
and then click on your user name in the top-right of any page. That  
takes you to your user page, where you can enter whatever you would  
like.

I would recommend just using what you wrote for your intro to this  
list. That is what I did:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/User:Ed_Pastore
Most of your intros to the list can be found here:
http://metagovernment.org/pipermail/start_metagovernment.org/2008-April/date.html

Thanks!
