I believe the best way to move forward with the ideas developed in the  
list server would be to start transcribing them into the wiki. I  
started with the Scoring system and Adhocracy articles, but have not  
had time to work on a few of the others I had intended.

I will add some more as I find time, but anyone can do it... Just look  
at an old thread, and make the most obvious 1-2 word page that  
describes one of the concepts there. (We can always move the page  
later if we want.) Some threads may result in multiple articles; other  
threads may simply contribute one section to an existing article.

When making an article, just be as "encyclopedic" as possible. That  
is, you can describe your own view, but should also include the views  
of others, as dispassionately as you can. This is what I was trying to  
do in this section, for example:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Scoring_system#Proposed_scoring_methods
