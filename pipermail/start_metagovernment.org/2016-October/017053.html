I think it is very proven science that "don't feed the troll"
is a dysfunctional strategy that empowers individuals to
manipulate group structures. So if this list in unmoderated
I would suggest to make a new list or group structure.

So far, sociology has not figured out any other solution than
moderation. If we also want justice on top of moderation we
have to elect a court of appeals that watches over the moderator.

Welcome to actual Internet democracy. To anyone who may not
be aware I say, before even doing any technology we first
need social structures that produce a mutual respect base.
