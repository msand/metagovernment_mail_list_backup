This is basically a question of eating our own dogfood. Not in terms of software, but in terms of philosophy.

I do (hypothetically) moderate already, in that if someone starts sending outright commercial spam to the list, I will boot them. But if I take action against someone based on much fuzzier judgements of the content and motivation of their posts, it is very difficult to determine where to draw the line. If I do it based solely only on my own judgement, I am acting as an authoritarian, which I will not do. I am more than willing to moderate (and/or to enable others to moderate) if we have a ruleset that clearly defines what should be moderated.
