Mailman performs automatic bounce processing:
https://www.esosoft.com/support/mailinglist/mailman/bounce.html <https://www.esosoft.com/support/mailinglist/mailman/bounce.html>
https://www.gnu.org/software/mailman/mailman-admin/node25.html <https://www.gnu.org/software/mailman/mailman-admin/node25.html>

I have not yet found a good description of what mailman considers to be a bounce, but presumably either your email client or your email server is sending some sort of message back to the list-reply address which is formed in a way that is processed as a bounce.
