Please help me a little further with formulating this support request. Is it that Google is flagging list messages as spam? I am dubious that they will be of much help. Until recently, I worked in email marketing, and decoding email spam flagging was a major initiative we tackled… but also one that is a fairly deep art with very unpredictable results. Google does not publish their spam-flagging algorithms (of course), and it seems like a wild goose chase to try to remediate them.
One thing I know does work: if a message erroneously appears in your spam folder, move it to your inbox. This informs the filter-logic, and does help over time.
Now if the problem is that list messages are completely disappearing, that seems like a valid support request.
