Agreed, this is a hard problem, and probably not one they're going to
be very interested in solving (i.e., there's no money in maintaining
mailing lists). Which is why I think we should just move the list to
some domain that does this for other (e.g., academic) reasons.

Anyone on this list have a .edu address from a university that already
has an active set of mailing lists? Seems to me that's probably our
best option (I'm on a couple of lists hosted by brown.edu that are
rock-solid reliable, for example). We might even ask brown.edu: I
don't know if the lists have to be sponsored by someone affiliated
with the university or not...

resisting it, and look forward to the day when this kind of abuse
joins exploitation of slaves, workers, women, children, etc. as just
one of the things that we as a civilization have grown out of.
