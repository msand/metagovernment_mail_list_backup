Would like to open a discussion on the alternative we never explore, we
have a group in Airesis that allow us to have a forum a personal blog for
each of us and the capability to open proposal work collaboratively on them
and vote them.
By the way most of you are already members and we have the policy in our
group to be all administrators and adding other to the group will be an
open option to anyone.
I also invite all of you to join the Airesis Development Group where most
of the discussions are taken in Italian but is a proposal is open in
english it will be carried in that language and you will be confronted with
other developers that are working for the last 5 years to produce Airesis
in a democratical way!
I do not want to push it further, this must be a decision that need to be
taken by the whole group and the only way will be at least to open a
proposal and participate, something we have never did!
The notifications on the group work must be managed independently in your
personal preference page.
The language of the interface is already available in most european main
languages. https://www.airesis.it/ select the flag and pick your favorite.
the link of the english language page of the group is
https://airesis.eu/metagovernment
Appy new year!
Salute
