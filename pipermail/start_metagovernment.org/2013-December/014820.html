Ned, I don't think we risk becoming an organization that makes secret decisions in "smoke-filled rooms." But I do take to heart your point that there's no reason to stop the global group from seeking more organization. I just don't think it needs to necessarily precede the organization of the US corporation.

The latter will take well over a year to achieve 501c3 status, so I'd like to get it kicked off as soon as possible to get it started on that grind. But to start it off, it should still have a governance structure that is in accordance with the group's goals.

Separately, we should continue to approach organization for the global group. We have a well-stated mission and vision, and we have defined basic principles:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Mission_and_vision

Where we take it from there is a good question. This page is where we had last formally discussed it:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Startup/Organization
but it has not really been updated in nearly four years. Perhaps the next step is to update this page and see if we can take it from there?
