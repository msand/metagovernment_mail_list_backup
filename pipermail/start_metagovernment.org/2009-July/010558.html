"Isn't a system where everyone can vote and the votes are equal a good
description of what the goal is?"

As an example, that's not how NationBuilder works at all.  In fact, every
vote is weighted slightly differently, based on how high it is in their list
of priorities and how "sincerely" the individual uses the site.   It's
definitely in the spirit of democracy, beacuse it's meant to give everyone
an equal voice... it's not fair to let people who can vote all day long
dominate the system.  (like on digg)
