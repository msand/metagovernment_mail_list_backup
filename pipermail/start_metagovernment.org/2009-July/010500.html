I would like to add the term study somwhere.
As an academic I see one of the problems in reaching a metagovernment
also a technical problem.

By now we have realised that the technological factor plays a huge
role in the shaping of society, but we have not realised WHAT form of
technology better suits our needs. This requires not just building,
but testing, and studying the various possibilities.

So for example:
The mission of the MetaGovernment project is to promote the _study_,
development and use of open source software to act as the primary
self-governance mechanism of any community of users to create
Sustainable Development through introduction of Transparency and
Accountability Initiatives


Would it be too self promoting to suggest to try out on this Vilfredo.
Yesterday I did 14 hours of programming, and now the version is much
better. For once it sends email every time it moves on, and to the
author every time another person add his take.

If not we could use MixedInk.

I am less enthusiastic about it than I was yesterday morning, but for
this work seems to be perfect.
