Since the transition proposal seems to have passed without dissent, I  
would like to get the ball rolling on doing the actual transition.

July is a particularly hectic month for me, where I will be traveling  
(repeatedly) between five cities in the next few weeks, so my time to  
dedicate is limited. But as I see it, the next steps are as follows.  
Anyone may begin any of these, but the first 2-3 steps should be  
worked out before we go much further. Suggestions for the first two  
are included in the proposal.
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Startup/Organization/Proposal_2

1. Define the new mission statement

2. Re-write the Basic Principles

3. Define the strategy for ongoing progress for both Metascore and  
Metagovernment

4. Re-write the wiki Main Page

5. Update various wiki pages to be more specific to Metascore or to  
Metagovernment

6. Begin Metagovernment outreach to other groups


P.S. Note, people particularly interested in working on Metascore  
should join the Metascore list:
http://metascore.org/mailman/listinfo/dev_metascore.org
