Hello  Mehnroush,
let me  add to Manuel's first reply. (I meant to write this this
morning, but I was did not manage to)

Essentially we are a bunch of geeks which have observed how (a) the
internet is pervasive and always more powerful (b) information is
flowing (c) society is being shaped by information flow (see my talk
on this),
http://videolectures.net/cvss08_fenizio_hst21cbo/
and we are projecting in the future how all this will work out with
the government, to the point of hoping and trying to mould it into
what we would like it to be. We have big hopes, an unbounded ego, and
probably too much time.

What we are really lacking, on the other side, are people who study
society and/or law. So don't ask what the metagovernment can do for
you, but what you can do for the metagovernment ;-).
