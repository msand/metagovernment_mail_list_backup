I am surprised to hear that this is part of the group's mission -- I knew
that *some* members of the group saw this as a goal, but is this really
meant to be part of the metagovernment mission?

I would like to see, in the interest of facilitating the migration to a
meta-organizational role, some recognition that there are a diversity of
goals that share common technological or scientific needs (ie, the os gov
platform), from that stated below to less radical citizen action
facilitators.

If we keep this on as a stated goal, then I would additionally note that
'every institution of representative democracy' may be a bit extreme. Do we
as a group feel that there are *no* scenarios in which a representative
democracy might be better than open source governance? I can imagine a
hybrid government like the US senate & house of reps, where the house of
reps is replaced by OS democracy but the senate remains. Isn't it a bit soon
to say that such a strategy isn't ideal?
