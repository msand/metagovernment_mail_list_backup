I think Ed's is the best so far.   The big question I think is whether it
should explicitly state that it's for people to govern themselves, or for
any type of governance.

"The mission of the Metagovernment project is to promote the development and
use of software to govern a community."

vs.

"The mission of the Metagovernment project is to promote development and use
of software for any community to govern themselves."
