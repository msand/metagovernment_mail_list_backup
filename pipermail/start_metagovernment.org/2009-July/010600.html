Hello all,
Should the project be hosted on the wiki as well?


FOr example it would be very good for Vilfredo to have some wiki page, to
organise the FAQ, for example. I could set up my own wiki, but why do so?
What if Vilfredo pages were hosted as part of the metagov wiki. And this
could be-come the standard for most of the projects that we relate to. There
could be a FAQ for each project, like for Nation Builder,  or Votorola. And
the users will also help answer the questions.

I could also make a link to the wiki from Vilfredo.

I think we should invent a prefix or a suffix for pages like that like:
Vilfredo_FAQ
or
Frequently_Asked_Questions_on_Vilfredo

But each project would not be limited to few pages and could use as many
pages they need. The only important element is that no project should use
titles that are too general, thus limiting what the rest of the wiki can
use.

Your thoughts?
