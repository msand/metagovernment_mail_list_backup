> Would it be too self promoting to suggest to try out on this Vilfredo.
> Yesterday I did 14 hours of programming, and now the version is much
> better. For once it sends email every time it moves on, and to the
> author every time another person add his take.

I think we should use Vilfredo... in a little while. I like the dialog  
we are having in this format, and I would suggest that that is a  
limitation of Vilfredo. Right now, we are not just making suggestions  
and voting on which one we like best, we are commenting on each  
others' suggestions and making tweaks to them. I think this sort of  
process could be integrated into Vilfredo, and I have just suggested  
it on http://pareto.ironfire.org/viewquestion.php?q=17

In other words... currently Vilfredo does not promote cross- 
fertilization of ideas. People can propose ideas and vote them up and  
down, but there is no formal logic which allows a person to say why  
they support/oppose a proposal. So sometimes, I might not know how to  
answer a question, but I can tell others what I like or don't like  
about the proposals they have made so far and hope that they can use  
my suggestions to come up with a better proposal.

So anyway... for now, I suggest that we continue the discussion on the  
list until we see some refinements worked out, and then we place  
several of the most favored items on Vilfredo and see if we can come  
up with a winner.
