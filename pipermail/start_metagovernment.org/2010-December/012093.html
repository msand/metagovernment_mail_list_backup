Open Source Governance -> Open Source Democracy

Open Source Democracy -> Open Democracy

B) Criteria For The Ideal Phrase

The ideal phrase would find a good balance between the following attributes:
- accuracy
- clarity
- brevity
- descriptiveness
- uniqueness of meaning

It would also take into account the connotations of the words (common
usage and understood meanings), rather than their denotations (strict
definitions).

Timelessness, though desirable, is not a requirement:  the criteria
would recognize that meanings evolve, and so this phrase is not an
endpoint, but merely one stepping stone along the path.

It would be useful for the term to be appropriate to both online and
offline methods/activities.

Neologisms may or may not be useful.
