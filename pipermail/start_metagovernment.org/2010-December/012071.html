This message thread is closely related to the purposes of the U.S. federal
CIO Council's ET.gov site and process:  http://et.gov/ 

Unfortunately, the resources have not been made available to build out the
capabilities of the site to more fully support the process.  See
http://et.gov/stage2.htm & http://xml.gov/draft/ETLifeCycleStage2.htm &, for
more details, perhaps also http://et.gov/history/enhancementrequirements.htm


While it would be good to have a table on the Metagov wiki containing
information about the maturity and support for Metagov projects, it would be
even better to:

	a) enable individuals to document their degrees of interest and
support (inputs and processing) for any emerging technology of interest to
them, anywhere on the Web in standard open, XML format; and

	b) provide aggregation, indexing, and analytical services for such
documentation, as envisioned for the ET.gov process at
http://xml.gov/draft/ETLifeCycleStage2.htm.

In any event, if any Metagov project developer believes s/he might be able
to contribute services to help build out the ET.gov process, s/he (or anyone
else) could use the ET.gov site/process to identify their proposed
contribution(s).

If anyone is interested and needs any assistance, I'll be glad to help.

See also http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratizing_Innovation.

BTW, I rendered NEF's "about nef" statement in StratML format at
http://xml.gov/stratml/drybridge/index.htm#NEF or, more specifically,
http://xml.gov/stratml/carmel/NEFwStyle.xml
