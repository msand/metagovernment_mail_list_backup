Hi Everyone,
 
DemocracyLab is working with a UI Design firm here in Portland to create the
user experience for our initial launch next month.  I'd like to invite
anyone interested to view and comment on the preliminary work completed to
date.  You can view the pdf, along with notes from our meeting yesterday,
here: https://clearspace.democracylab.org/docs/DOC-1123.  I'd suggest
starting on page 7 of 23.  There's still a great deal of work for us to
complete, particularly relating to visualizing the data structure, but I
thought it better to solicit input sooner than later.  If you'd like to be
kept informed of updates to the document and discussion, you can register
<https://clearspace.democracylab.org/create-account.jspa>  to create a
Clearspace account and then elect to "Receive email notifications" in the
Graphical User <https://clearspace.democracylab.org/community/website/gui>
Interface and Web
<https://clearspace.democracylab.org/community/website?view=overview>
Development communities.
 
You may also be interested in a discussion concerning rating systems taking
place here: https://clearspace.democracylab.org/thread/1035.  
 
We'll freely share everything we create with the developers at Metascore,
and I think much of it will be compatible.  
I hope everyone's enjoying their weekend.  It's definitely turned to fall
here in Oregon.
