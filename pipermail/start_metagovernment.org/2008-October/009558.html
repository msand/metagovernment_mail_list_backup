I finished watching the new Zeitgeist Movie this evening, which heavily
focused on the collapse of the Economic Systems around the world. They also
came back to the idea that Technology should be used to free us from
scarcity. At the end the movie recommended supporting alternatives to
government that utilize technology to promote equality, with a link to their
Zeitgeist Movement (opening on the 10th of this month).
I interpret this to be the call by the people for a revolution, or a
movement -- but certainly not a chess club. As I stated earlier in a
different thread, I believe that a good starting point for our software
should be our own community. If we are able to manage ourselves effectively,
then we're headed in the right direction. Otherwise, we've discovered a gap
between our abilities and our expectations.
