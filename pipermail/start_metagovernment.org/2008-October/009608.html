I couldn't agree more. I would say, if there are to be groups we would want
more of them, and allow people to be members of as many groups in as many
ways as they prefer. this will cause them to be less like political parties
and more like NGOs, advocacy groups, etc.

While we will likely have groups of all types, making them fashion their own
ways to interact with the system will limit their number and size (to be few
and large) since there will be a higher technological (and thus monetary)
threshold for them to meet in order to participate.
