The slides from the summer school are finally out.
http://www.illc.uva.nl/COMSOC/estoril-2010/

In the last meeting I promised that I shall try to write an introduction to
voting theory for the metagovernment pages.

Now with the help of the slides I shall try to do it. My aim is to give a
brief but precise introduction of
voting theory
history of voting theory
ways to vote
ways to count the votes
paradoxes in voting

requirements from a voting system
Impossibility theorems (Arrow...)
Positive and negative points for each voting type.

As I write down the points I realize the enormity of the topic. And yet each
point is absolutely critical if we want to make sure our work is relevant.
Anyone wishing to help will be well accepted.
