Here's a rough prototype of a difference bridge:

  http://u.zelea.com:8080/v/w/Diff?a=2961&b=2962#L25

It's not ready for users yet, but it's technically functional.  You
have to understand that the two positions compared (a and b) are the
latest revisions of these pages:
http://u.zelea.com/w/User:ThomasvonderElbe_GmxDe/Sys/p/sandbox
http://u.zelea.com/w/User:Mike-ZeleaCom/Sys/p/sandbox
(Thomas is the candidate and I'm his voter.)

Note that the diff URL points directly to a particular difference,
namely in sentence "13" of the text.  So we could make it the topic of
a very focused discussion here:

  Thomas, what do think of this change I've made to sentence 13?

Next to code:

  b) Add cherry-picking merge controls.  So either of us (candidate or
     voter) could eliminate the difference by text transfer in one
     direction or the other, with a click of the mouse.

  c) Make it user friendly.

Maybe that would be enough to start alpha testing in various forums,
and see if it works as an interface?
