At the moment we are trying to create a channel that replicates the
properties that voters associate with physical voting, but with all the
advantages of a remote hybrid direct/representative system. Public voting
would be, at the moment, a huge cultural change that the voting population
is not willing to accept. Achieving credibility for our initiative requires
guarantees of privacy and universal verifiability (for starters). I have
already seen that there are issues such as vote buying, coercion, that are
hard to solve even with a secure system, let alone public voting. In any
case, my current responsibility is not political theory, but research and
engineering, so this is not a qualified opinion.
