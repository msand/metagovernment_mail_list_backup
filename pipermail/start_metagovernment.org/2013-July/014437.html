Alex, I for one am very interested in the subjects of "the rules of 
money" and "the rules of equity". I agree with you that, unless our new 
arrangements include changing these, the new arrangements will prove to 
be half-measures and empty gestures that will not lead to making the 
world a better place in the long run.

If you have proposals to offer regarding either of these topics, I would 
be very interested to read them.
