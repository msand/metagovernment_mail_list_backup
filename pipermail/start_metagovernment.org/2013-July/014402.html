Hi Mike,

    Mike> The distinction between primary guidance and authoritative
    decision is interesting, but only to a small number of people just

Ned> Honestly, the distinction is not of much interest to me. From the 
perspective of full direct democracy, it is meaningless -- there is no 
"other system" to guide. The distinction only has meaning and 
significance from the perspective of hybrid systems such as polyarchy.

My standing proposal is that we put our efforts into creating a full 
direct democracy, and render all of the hybrid systems obsolete.
