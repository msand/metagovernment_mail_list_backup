The Idea of Money itself is great.

A System, in which each persons "moneypoints" declare how much they have
done for the "collective" which is all participants in a monetary system.
This effectively decouples a person who is doing work from the person for
who work is being done for, thus enableing people to work and do stuff for
complete strangers, which has never been possible before.

We have come so far exactly because of money. But still, the money rules
are deeply flawed, because what i just said is an idealized version of what
money should be and it is not what it is today, but i guess it's not the
right list to discuss the equity idea i have in mind.

thx to all readers patience anyway
