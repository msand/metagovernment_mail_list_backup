Marc said:
> Are you talking about a pure "testing scenario"?  Or are you talking
> about the necessary structure to formalize discussion in general?

In the first instance, I think discursive baselines are needed for
technical development including testing of the practices and tools as
you say.  For that we need to create and maintain baselines, which
means learning the techniques for that.

But there's also the possibility that the same techniques will become
more widely used in future.  Maybe the first thing people will do in
order to raise a new issue (not much discussed yet) is to establish a
baseline for it.  Maybe it will prove useful for mature discussions
too, e.g. in raising sub-issues.
