Hi Conseo and Stephen,

Stephen, obviously to all you are the sort of user/visionary that e-dem 
designers and developers dream of working with.

Conseo, Stephen has just given you feedback, with which I heartily 
concur: the Votorola main page to which you linked is mostly 
incomprehensible. It cannot be used as part of a proposal to the Ashland 
Food Co-op. (Maybe you could write up "elevator pitches" for one or more 
of the practices?)

Conseo, how close is your team to putting up a fully functional practice 
(any practice), that Stephen (and the rest of us) could actually engage 
in? The actual experience of engaging in the practice might then enable 
him to write up a proposal of the practice that would be comprehensible 
and persuasive to his audience.

Stephen, in order to enable Conseo to "diagnose" which of his practices 
might be useful to the co-op, it would be helpful if you could present 
an analysis of existing democratic practices within the co-op. Here is a 
start:

    * The 8000 members vote on bylaws.
    * The 8000 members elect board members.
    * The 8000 members choose the grants committee members?
    * The General Manager is hired by the BOD?
    * The Board of Directors decide ...
    * The General Manager decides ...
    * The Grants Committee decides ...
    * ...
