I don't know what you mean with synthesis.
I assume that you miss "the next step" in this discussion; concretely some kind of
free text summary or proposal. And further, that then people vote on such proposal, which
in the end leads to a decission.
Well, my impression of Incoma is, that it's aiming on open discussions;
you can start with an open question and then can add stuff, which basically always can
seen as closed questions or thesis, on which you can vote on if you agree or disagree.
I can see no further elements of a discussion like a free text proposal button, where you
can recap all the dots or parts of the sum of all dots for getting closer to a decission.
Imho that's okay; imho much is won if you basically structure discussion like Incoma does.
