Dear Oliver
I see it this way
Problem->Discussion->Objective->Discussion->Solution (many some time and
often different)-> Deliberative Vote-> implementation.
>From the discussion I need to synthesize to get to an objective or to a
solution in a single cell or if I have different solution one cell for each
solution. In the incoma page is stated " facilitate the summarisation of
the huge amounts of individual contributions towards a common goal. " What
you call summarization I call'it Synthesis and I imagine it as a collective
work that summarize the collective agreement or disagreement.
If I still have to look and search for them on a bubble interface, OK some
is bigger but I can have opposite perspective and equally voted. There
still need a work of a relater to really summarize into an organic work.
This is the solution I am looking for.
Do you think INCOMA can be developed further into that perspective? I come
from an AIRESIS perspective and we are looking and working toward for such
collaborative organic results.
And again debategraph.org seem to me more advanced to do just a
summarisation of individual contribution.
Regards
Jacopo
