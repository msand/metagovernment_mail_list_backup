This (quoted stuff above) is always the case, because there is no absolute
truth or knowledge.
There is only things that i am very sure of and less sure of.
I said "No, it can't", because i have multiple experiences in that domain
that always ended with what i have written.
It were very different domains of discussion and we tried a lot to
formalize it, but it NEVER even remotely worked,
other than for a couple of days with a very narrow circle of participants...

It's 1 Year of "Mehr Demokratie e.V." discussions
It's 1.5 Years of "organizing FRAppe Student Entrepreneurs e.V."
It's 1+ Years of engaging in "AG Meinungsfindungstool" (pirate party)
It's 1+ Years of heavy discussions in "AG GOuFP" (pirate party)
It's a long time in "Metagovernment.org" discussions
It's 0.5 to 1 Year of discussion groups with the OCCUPY MOVEMENT

It never even remotely worked. Only that, for which there are tools that
support a certain behavior, has worked.
So it's possible to do a process manually for a very short time with a very
narrow set of people to achieve a proof of concept or clarify how it should
work out,
but other than that, i believe its hopeless...
