Whats the purpose of that?
I believe there can be no general answer to this question.
The only answer might be, to make the process of finding whats best for
everyone "more fair"???
This question has so many different levels it could be answered on, so many
different perspectives on it, that its just to hard to answer in just one
sentence.
It might only be possible with another general phrase.

I assume something and that is, that the person who knows whats absolutely
the best for person X is person X itself.
That might NOT be true directly, often persons do not know what is the best
thing for themselves nor for the community, but i believe,
this is always based on incomplete information or false information or an
incomplete perspective on issues or on missunderstandings, and so forth...

BUT: All persons are subject to the same problem here. Some might be more
"well informed" but its totally possible, that they have a very distorted
perspective the same time.
Some people are maybe not very well informed, but possibly they have a good
balance when it comes to the different perspectives they have learned about.

Then again, nobody can possibly know, how well informed others are and how
good their perspectives are. They have to assess this based on their own
perspectives, values and information and so sometimes they might come up
with an appropriate judgement, and sometimes not so much...

"Capitalist" is a word, that stands for everything and nothing. If you dont
provide an exact definition in a near mathematical sense, its only going to
divide people into factions.
WHY?
Because every concept that is blurry and not well defined will, like a
"statistical effect", make somehow some people decide in favour of it and
some against it.
Take a random word, load is with emotions but dont give it a precise
meaning and immediately u get people divided over nothing...
