Just to put the idea out there.  It follows from my work on this:
http://answers.onstartups.com/questions/50267

In a nutshell: 1) we crowdfund an achievement prize for the broader
technical field; 2) the funders own the fund and govern it with the
help of the techniques developed in the field; 3) this sets an example
of self-determination and self-government for the broader society,
progress toward which is fueled by the awarding of ever larger prizes
from the fund.

This should be compatible with most other start proposals.  Metagov
organizes this among its other activities.
