In an effort to increase the potential for participation in this mailing
list I thought I would provide an an example of what I feel is discouraging
writing so we might avoid it in the future. My feeling is that by making
the definitive statement like the one below you signal that you don't want
further discussion on the specific point and imply that you are probably
not open to discussion on most topics. This discourages participation. In
many cultures it would be considered rude.

Alternatives could be "In my experience it has not worked because..." "I
disagree with..."

I understand that calling this out also could discourage participation but
I hope the net result would be positive.
