Hi Max,

The AllAngles structured discussion of email-as-platform is at 
http://allangles.co/14

The concurrent informal discussion of email-as-platform (here on the 
email list) spread itself across at least half a dozen different threads 
-- it was a classic email mess. To me, the AllAngles version of the 
discussion was/is vastly more supportive of potentially reaching 
consensus and a decision. But, at this point, neither version of the 
discussion has led to reaching consensus and a decision.

If you have available-to-propose a directed graph for a "reach consensus 
and reach decision" process, that could use the material in Bentley's 
spreadsheet and/or the material in the AllAngles discussion as "grist 
for its decision mill", that would be of considerable interest. (There 
are several projects in the community -- e.g., Vilfredo, Votorola, 
Blinap -- that feature such decision system graphs.)

Decisions are made routinely within the teams of individual projects. 
But decisions made by the community are very few and far between.
