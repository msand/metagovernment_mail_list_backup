IMPORTANT!
Suppose 6 users have put some text from a message stream to their personal
pages, then everyone could look it up on each of their pages and would also
see a counter saying "6 People share this oppinion" (or something like
that) and if they click on it, they can see who those others are.
BUT: If someone of those 6 changes the wording of a section, it will be
different from the other ones, thus it will reduce the counter from 6 to 5.
In order to prevent this, there could be a "SUBSCRIBE TO CHANGES"
Mechanism, which will inform the other 5 people of the change someone has
made and they are offered to either ACCEPT or REJECT the change.
If they REJECT it, then the counters will show "5" and "1", making it two
different opinions.
The "1" could now revert his change to make the counter go back up to "6".

Maybe i'm browsing through the archives or maybe i was on holiday and come
