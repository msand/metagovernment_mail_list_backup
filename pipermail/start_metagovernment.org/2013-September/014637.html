4  I thought the lever idea had merit.

how close they might come to meeting.  But that is a very abstract

perhaps the Kabbalist treatment of Deity...oops, G-d, double oops, g-d, for

ownership but community pride in the their and your efforts toward a

cannot get more thoughtfulness we all will continue as we have been in
