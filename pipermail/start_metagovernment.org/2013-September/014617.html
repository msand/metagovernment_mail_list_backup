We could add all this stuff to a facebook timeline, but we shouldt become
to nostalgic about it :P
what about ongoing stuff?
what are metagovernment people currently up to and where could they need
help or what would they like to accomplish if others would join?

i can only speak for me and i would like to "advance email". A website,
where people could create "discussion mailinglists" for free.
to make it secure, people who do not join by themselves, but are invited in
or added by others first must confirm that they want to participate.

there will be summary emails which can be configured by each participant,
so he receives news around those topics that are of interest to him/her.
And then of course, there has to be some kind of "semi-automated" wiki, to
which one could add content or update or even delete content, so everyone
could link those snippets or whatever it is, back into the email discussion
stream.


I have already written a javascript based authentication mechanism, where
one could receive credentials via email and had to confirm it afterwards.
there was also a "forgot password" mechanism and then of course a "user
profil" site, where a user could change all those settings...


So, i guess it's not that hard to use javascript/nodejs to host a website
that allows users to create ne mailing lists.
The hard thing will then be, how to structure the discussion content by
adding additional html/css to the email and if possible maybe even
javascript.
Maybe instead of writing the email in ones usual email program, there could
be lots of links that open a browser with the content of the currently
viewed mail and where a lot more options are allowed, when it comes to
commenting, voting, discussion, quoting, persisting, forking, whatever....

So that's it and its what i would add to "currrently" on that timeline ;-)
