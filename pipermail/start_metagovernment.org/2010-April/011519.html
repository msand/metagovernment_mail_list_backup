Hello Ed,
I will be in the meeting tomorrow.

I am just back from a doctoral school in computational social choices, which
boiled down to a series of cruch courses in voting theory for the most part.
Amazing lessons.

We went through at multiple types of voting, theorems that show what is
possible and what is not possible, paradoxes and similar (situation where
you have 5 different type of voting, 5 candidates, and each type of voting
would select a different candidate). We also learned how to build those
paradoxes. I mean, really, it was fuuuun!

The slides should be online soon. Unfortunately (really!) there are no
video. But maybe now that I know what the players are we can find other
videos from other sources explaining the various voting types.

I any case I understood we already have quite an agenda for tomorrow, so I
