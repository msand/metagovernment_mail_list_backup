Ed Pastore wrote:
> This would be a good opportunity for Votorola to be a little better  
> explained to the general Metagov group...

To explain a little of the context: Votorola (V) is just one
pollserver among many (W, X, and so forth).

  (V) (W) (X) . . .
    \  |  /
     \ | /
      \|/
    (PW/SW)

All share the common infrastructure of a single pollwiki/streetwiki
(PW/SW).  The (PW/SW) is mostly what we care about.  (V just happens
to be the first pollserver actually making use of it.)

No.  And it's probably too difficult to explain in any general way.
If you get stuck, please just ask specific questions.

We won't have proper user docs till after basic usability testing and
UI refinements.  Those depend (in turn) on getting the major
facilities in place.  Still missing are:

  * Voter register (e.g. streetwiki, etc)
  * Vote mirroring (need at least one other pollserver)
  * Interface with discussion media (e.g. difference engine etc)

Meantime, it's all wires sticking out.
