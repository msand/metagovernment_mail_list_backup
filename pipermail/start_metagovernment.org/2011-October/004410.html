Dear community,

I have recently started re-organising my material on the web.
And among those things I started publishing the recordings of the talks I
gave.
As they are on the topic of eDemocracy I think they might be of interest to
this community.

This one is about Fair Participation.
http://home.pietrosperoni.it/index.php/research/epart/2011comsoc/

In other words what seem to me to be the requirements for a system to be
fair. I am here speaking not so much of the kind of intuitive sense of
fairness we sometimes feel, but of a more grounded mathematical fairness.
The idea that if you were to run the same system with the same proposal,
only presented by different people in a different order, the same one would
be selected at the end.
