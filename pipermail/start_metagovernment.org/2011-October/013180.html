I'm fitting in bits of updates on the script between travels and other  
responsibilities. Please have a look at it now:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Promotion/Movie/Script

I have included much of Michael's critique, in brief form. Please feel  
free to work on this with me, anyone.

There is a bit of a conflict of purpose here. To be brief, I have  
focused on Michael's core argument that modern democracies are not  
liberating. However, that excludes a lot of the other valid criticisms  
of representative democracy at:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Representative_democracy
Is the message stronger focusing on the one key point, or pounding on  
the many various points?
