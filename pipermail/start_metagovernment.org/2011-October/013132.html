Folks and Nillus,

I'd suggest having a look at wevote.net, where the currently featured 
activity is called 99stack, based on the "We're the 99" slogan, and the 
"stack" queuing of speakers used by General Assemblies at the occupy 
demonstrations.

That particular interface represents the early stage of an interactive 
crowd-sourcing platform I've been developing. It's optimized for entry 
of text and youtube embeds to support an amendable ranked-choice 
evaluation by participants.

My overarching project, the Indaba Application Network is one of several 
represented here. You can read more about it at

http://www.changemakers.com/citizenmedia/entries/indaba-application-network

Craig Simon
