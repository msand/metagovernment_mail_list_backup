http://heliosvoting.org/

Helios offers truly verifiable online elections.

We believe in democracy and we think verifiable elections are its 
cornerstone. We believe democracy is important at every level, whether it's 
your book club, parent-teacher association, student government, workers' 
union, or state.

So we've made truly verifiable elections as easy as everything else on the 
Web.

When you cast a vote with Helios, you get a smart tracker to track your vote 
all the way to the tally. No one knows how you voted, but you can track your 
vote and everyone can check the tally. Our technology is fully open.
