Hi. I'm a developer for the GOV Online Voting system for liquid democracy at gov.e2d.se

Its developed to meet the needs for www.aktivdemokrati.se

It currently include 3 different authentication systems, simple votes, multiple delegations, condorcet ranked pair priority voting and median value voting. It allows for different voting areas with different membership criteria, allwoing it to be used for votings in organizations.

I'm now working on extending the delegation functions for allowing different delegations in different areas or for different topics, and the handling of delegates by multiple users.
