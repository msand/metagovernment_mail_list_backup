Very nice UI on some of those sites.  Google's "chromeframe" looks brilliant - thank you for noticing that Paul. That plugin is for IE browsers only, which of course as any web designer will tell you, are the scourge of all browsers - causing 1000's of web designers to waste huge amounts of time trying to make M$ IE work like a proper standards-oriented browser.  Chromeframe looks like the perfect solution to that problem, and from the looks of it should take IE out of the stone age and make web development for hyperarchy and millions of other sites much simpler and more powerful.  Google deserves an award for that.


Also great to see the progress of those sites, and I think this is more evidence that this is a self-emergent movement which is unavoidable given the absurd politics of today - the essentially complete corporate control of media, the ever-expanding wars of aggression throughout Africa and the Middle East, the economics of usury, and the corruption in general.  It's just a question of best coordinating efforts at this point.
