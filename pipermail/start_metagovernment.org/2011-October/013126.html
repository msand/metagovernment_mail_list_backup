My question related to something else, to the assertion that it'll be
helpful to showcase the usefulness of the tools.  I asked, "How can
the tools be useful to us?"

It seems you answer, "They'll be useful in helping to showcase the
usefulness of the tools."

But that would be circular.  If the tools aren't *immediately* useful
to us (the alpha dogs) in our own work, then we have nothing to
showcase.  We won't gain what you call "first hand experience if the
tools really cut it", but only an empty show.  So I suggest we look at
the immediate utility of the tools for our own work, and ask what
exactly that is.
