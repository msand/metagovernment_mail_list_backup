My reply:Hi Conseo,the group of Agora 2.0 is made by a nucleus of 7-8 people who work on it and a group of other people who are willing to work but still not 100% committed.So far, discussions in the group have been quite theoretical and I had the impression that there is a lot of "amateurism" in there, this is why I strongly suggested them to take a look at Votorola.The reason why they have not done so is that they want to develop their software according to their specs and their software includes collaborative re-writing and collaborative amendment of laws, and they were not seeing this in the video done by Thomas.But they are quite interested in working with you, it seems, and I am pushing them in this way.They have asked me more or less like this : "Why if Votorola is willing to attract users, their group have not prepared one simple presentation about the functionalities to be shown?" ?As you can see, it is a chicken-and-egg problem.I am just
