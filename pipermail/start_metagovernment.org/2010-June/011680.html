While this is generally true at some levels, it cannot be true at all
levels.  The issue of timing and *need* requires decisions without the
luxury of consensus or a (both/and) grey middle.   Ultimately that is

"gut" decision and live with the/any consequences.  The idea of an
ultimate synthesis can only be achieved when there is something like a
One World Benevolent Governor (already incorporating *all* the
relevant inputs into a harmonious whole).

And without a stark black against a pure white, there can be no
rainbow in between.
