> Also, who owns/controls the domain name of the umbrella?  Under what
> terms?

I have always been seeking a way to cede control of  
metagovernment.org. Likewise, in Thomas' proposal, he expresses a  
desire to cede freevoters.org. It's just that whichever name we use  
needs some entity to be ceded to. The problem I have chronically  
encountered with Metagovernment is that most entities, in order to  
have any recognition by other institutions (namely governments and  
banks, with which they would have to show legitimacy in order to have  
control over resources) have to have designated, empowered leaders. It  
would be a violation of our own core principles to, for example, have  
me cede metagovernment.org to a formal non-profit called  
Metagovernment which is headed by an elected and empowered board of  
directors.
