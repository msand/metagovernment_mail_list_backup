Wikipedia defines *Coercion* as the practice of forcing another party to
behave in an involuntary manner (whether through action or inaction) by use
of threats, intimidation <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intimidation>,
trickery, or some other form of pressure or force. Such actions are used as
leverage, to force the victim to act in the desired way. Repeated
canvassing, media blast, etc. are also forms of coercion.
