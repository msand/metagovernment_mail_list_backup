>> On May 2, 2014, at 6:09 AM, Steven Palmer <notifications at loomio.org> wrote:
>> Outcome
>> Strong support for this feature, but some are unsure how well it could be integrated.
>> Proposal
>> Option to follow Public Groups
>> The "Ask to Join" feature of Public Groups is useful as it increases transparency of an otherwise Private Group or membership control of an otherwise Open Public Group. However, it seems nonsensical that in order to follow discussions you can already view, you need to 'Ask to Join'. Therefore I propose a 'Follow' option for Public Groups.
