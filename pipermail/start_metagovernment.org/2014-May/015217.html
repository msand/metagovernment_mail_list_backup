I just want to make an observation, Pietro, rightfully observe that many
time in a ML there is repetition of issues with rivers of words spent to
describe them and hours of smart people used to do so. It seem to me that
intelligence is wasted because there is not a method to use the energy and,
here I may be wrong, little or no outcome. Where is the problem? For me the
problem is on the ML structure, it is not a smart way of holding a
discussion when the participation is more than 3 people. I experienced the
same problem in the development group of the participatory democracy I am
involved where incredible ideas are found or developed but often get lost
in the river sometime we record them in a different environment and then we
can tackle them in a more productive way.
Unfortunately we are all used to use ML and reading a list rather than
using markup language or a GUI to navigate Ideas, Concepts, Solutions.
I do believe in consolidation of various platforms, driven by mass
decision, will be the key to develop the best platform, it must be flexible
but must to be one.
Why one? because should connect all the people on issues, and the issues
should be so well organized that will be difficult to say something new. I
often have ideas and after looking on the web I see other have developed
such ideas. To say something new today is becoming very hard, in a ML you
waist your time!
Regards
Jacopo
