I like the points being made, just to add a couple on the actual system, if
we can be that specific. I feel these should be emphasized on the home
page, though probably re-worded.


- We aim to distribute power evenly amongst every individual. We aim to
reach decisions that improve the overall quality of life for the largest
group possible. We aim to grow together as one entity.


- We aim to do away with political parties, political platforms, and party
lines. We are individuals who all have equally valid opinions that can vary
from issue to issue, we should never be defined or categorized.


- We aim to uncover all the information and analysis all the facts,
discussing them at face value, hiding nothing. Transparency is important
for discovering the truth. Numbers should always be recorded and accounted
for.


- We aim to own up to mistakes when they are made, to make the changes
necessary in a timely fashion. We do not to trivialize the issue in order
to save any face, we succeed together, we fail together, no excuses.
