Metagovernment exists for many years now and even though it does, a
relatively radical decision like the one to blank the main page of
metagovernment.org was decided acording to the rules we have set in the
past.
This is something i do like a lot, because i have experienced that given
such circumstances, rules are being ignored and instead people use their
power to sabotage something that they strongly oppose.

I have copied the last valid version of the front page to this etherpad:
*https://titanpad.com/Metagovernment-org
<https://titanpad.com/Metagovernment-org>*

It enables everyone to co-create new proposals and vote for those.
The problem is, that the content of that "external pad" will not go into
the archive of this mailing list, thus we might technically have a problem.


*PROPOSAL:* (and because the proposal of Ed of making every proposal start
with the word "Proposal" written as a subject is currently not a valid rule
yet)
I propose that metagovernment.org hosts it's own etherpad, like the hosting
provider of titanpad.com or the pirate party and many other organisations
already do.
It's a very flexible and open tool, which could be easily used for a
collaborative brainstorming process, given the discipline within this
community.
http://etherpad.metagovernment.org/
