Michael,

To clarify: As you said there is no active project. There is an active
group of people with a common mission of discussing tools and idea around
tools for government. I believe we are generally doing what the first two
sentences of the Metagovernment website says:

"The Metagovernment project supports internet tools to enable collaborative
governance. We are a global community of people working on numerous
projects which further this goal."

Although the "support" at this time is just a place to discuss and debate
what the tools need to do.

Maybe a better summary would be " Metagovernment is a discussion group
about tools that enable collaborative governance."
