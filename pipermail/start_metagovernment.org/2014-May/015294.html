Yes, let's reflect calmly.  The home page now clearly belongs to the
group as a whole, which is an unaccustomed responsibility.  Naturally
we feel a little nervous in the new situation, but let's not panic.
Nothing bad is about to happen.  There's no ticking clock of doom.
We're not actually drilling into dangerous rock yet, nor sailing into
dangerous seas.  We're just reflecting calmly.

We're free to define ourselves as we please, and to express ourselves
as we please in our home page, and to do all of this in our own time.
