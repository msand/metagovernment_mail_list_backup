One week to make a big decision like this seems like a unusually small
window. A leader who cared about his people would want to look at ways to
include and get all the facts. Not run as fast as you can while you have
the ball hiding behind the "system" saying it has already decided. If you
were on the other side of the line how would you want to be treated? It
smells a little authoritarian to me.

Ed has worked on this list for years where you have only been a month or
so? I consider it a professional courtesy not a statement of ownership.
