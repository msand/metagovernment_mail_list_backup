>  What are your top requested features and changes to Loomio?

> This was initially about a list specifically for a programme we were

> Suggestions from the discussion:
>
>    - Simple server installation for new Loomio instances
>    - Export facility for Loomio groups
>    - Import facility for Loomio groups
>    - list of Admins/ Co-ordinators
>    - Ability to add people to sub groups before they accept their invite
>    - Forking proposals
>    - quick polls, temperature checks
>    - mobile app
>    - api & oauth integration
>    - grouping and sorting passed proposals to build up a documentation of
>    a groups ‘policy and governance’ rules. Sort of like living legislation for
>    a group as opposed to proposals which are about decisions that aren’t
>    relevant once passed.
>    - Mobile Apps
>    - Engagement stats and metrics so you can see who is participating at
>    what levels over time,
>    - visualizations of decisions made, membership numbers, etc, on a
>    timeline
>    - Search comments, proposals, by date, by author, etc.
>    - WYSIWG editor
>
