> Steven Palmer started a new discussion in Loomio Community - Feature Ideas:
> Poll Vote
> I think it would help consensus to have a poll vote as an extension to the current discussion and hand signal voting methods.
> Similar to http://pollcode.com
> This would allow for greater individual autonomy, improved direct democracy, improved transparency of thought and simplified experience.
> i.e. a group should not have the power to deny an individual the ability to choose where to designate one’s own resources at every turn - participatory budgeting.
> 
> Use example:
> One major part of the decision making process that is critical to many groups, is what to spend individual and collective resources on. While Loomio allows for budgeting through discussion, it’s an overcomplicated process that becomes messy when designating resources to more than a single area, is not very transparent, unnecessarily subordinates individual decision making to group process.
> With polling as extension, autonomy would still be a subset of consensus as polls would be constructed out of discussion and current consensus method. Poll votes provide more accurate consensus through collective aggregation of vote count and undiluted individual decision making.
