 What are your top requested features and changes to Loomio?

This was initially about a list specifically for a programme we were applying for, but has evolved into a general requests discussion.

Suggestions from the discussion:

Simple server installation for new Loomio instances
Export facility for Loomio groups
Import facility for Loomio groups
list of Admins/ Co-ordinators
Ability to add people to sub groups before they accept their invite
Forking proposals
quick polls, temperature checks
mobile app
api & oauth integration
grouping and sorting passed proposals to build up a documentation of a groups ‘policy and governance’ rules. Sort of like living legislation for a group as opposed to proposals which are about decisions that aren’t relevant once passed.
Mobile Apps
Engagement stats and metrics so you can see who is participating at what levels over time,
visualizations of decisions made, membership numbers, etc, on a timeline
Search comments, proposals, by date, by author, etc.
WYSIWG editor
https://www.loomio.org/d/zZAPp6YG/what-are-your-top-requested-features-and-changes-to-loomio

On May 2, 2014, at 6:09 AM, Steven Palmer <notifications at loomio.org> wrote:
Outcome
Strong support for this feature, but some are unsure how well it could be integrated.
Proposal
Option to follow Public Groups
The "Ask to Join" feature of Public Groups is useful as it increases transparency of an otherwise Private Group or membership control of an otherwise Open Public Group. However, it seems nonsensical that in order to follow discussions you can already view, you need to 'Ask to Join'. Therefore I propose a 'Follow' option for Public Groups.
