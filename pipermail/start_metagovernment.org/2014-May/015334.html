Hi Steve, (I greatly regret not having more time for this at this point ...)

Regarding clearinghouse and notification functionality:

    * I agree with Ed that we desperately need an "overview"
      clearinghouse site and a general proposal notification system at
      this point.
    * I agree with you that using a wiki-page as clearinghouse and the
      list for notification leaves much to be desired.
    * I agree with you that Loomio handles its own proposals and
      notification well.
    * But it does not look to me like Loomio would be able to serve as a
      general clearinghouse site and notification system, for proposals
      being decided on other platforms. (I would be most appreciative to
      be shown that I am wrong about that.)

armchairs guessing about the outcomes.
