Voting will close tomorrow on the proposed metagovernment decision rule.
You can vote at https://www.loomio.org/d/ra2gemqI/decision-rule-discussion




the proposal is : Minimum of 1/3 of registered voters must vote for a
proposal (consensus/unanimity at that level of participation) or a simple
majority above that level.

Yes (3)
Abstain (1)

bloxx: Yes I basically agree - but how about a rule that 1/6 of registered
voters have to agree? Else, it might happen that a disagreeing person would
make the realization of the proposal possible by increasing participation.

Steve Coffman: Yes I like this as a starting point. If, down the road, a
proposal is made for something closer to unanimity that's acceptable to a
