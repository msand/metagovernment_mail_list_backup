Discussion is a very good think to do but I will love to see the group
choose the top tool to be developed by a major survey and then support it
with code development and active action.
I will start a proposal in the metagovernment  group i recently formed into
the Airesis platform to be developed into maturity, Some one ask me what is
the maturity value.
Basically during the proposal making there is contribution to it by the
participants and  as the proposal change and grow. participants are giving
their assessment to the readiness of the proposal, when the degree of
readiness reach a quorum defined ahead and or a specific time defined to
discuss the proposal the proposal reach the readiness. At this point there
is a reflection period and then there is the actual vote. During the
discussion every one will be given a temporary anonymity so that non one
knows who is who After the vote, if is choose as  secret the name are not
release if is palese the vote will have a name. I wish the group to reach
at least ten participants to make this decision and I invited Ed to take
part to this test.
