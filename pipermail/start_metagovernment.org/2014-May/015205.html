Hi Pietro,

That's a good description of the history.  I was there in the early
days too, though more as critic than a participant (and now here I am
back in that role).  About that description, it'd be an achievement
just to agree on that and publish it clearly for all to see (where
Metagov comes from). It would help in navigating the forward direction
(where Metagov is going) by making it more difficult to go in circles.

About the opinion pages in the wiki: I might be interested in reading
those if they're on the topic of Metagov's purpose (where Metagov is
going).  What I'd be more interested in, however, is a consensus on
that topic; not just any consensus, mind you, but a consensus between
2 or more people who can act on it, i.e. actually move Metagov in the
agreed direction.

I participated for years without such a consensus.  It didn't work.
Maybe I was thinking that consensus would eventually materialize just
because I put solid work into it.  It didn't.  Now I'm an outside
critic again and my recommendation to everyone (not just to you) is,
"Don't do that.  It's a waste of time.  It doesn't work."

Mike
