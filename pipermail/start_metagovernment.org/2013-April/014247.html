Hi, Anthony.

I am no expert on software licensing, but I do know that it is a big and well-thought-out field:
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_software_license

There are some wildly popular free software projects like Apache, Firefox, Linux, PHP, Debian, MySQL, etc., that appear to have maintained their rights even though they directly (and strongly) compete with very commercial software from enormous corporations.

Others on this list may be able to provide deeper information, but if you want to talk to the experts, the Open Knowledge Foundation has been very forthcoming when I've posted on their list (okfn-discuss): http://okfn.org/contact/

I hope this helps; and I hope to see more about what you've been working on.
