Great news, Pietro. Please share a link to the video or other surrounding info as it becomes available.

I have considered applying for a TED talk about the broad concepts of Metagovernment, because that seems like a great pairing. The great failures in the status quo and the amazing possibilities (or inevitabilities, as I see them) of collaborative governance should align very well with the TED venue.

My main obstacle has been the ongoing family medical concerns that keep me from being able to properly concentrate on this project. I am hopeful that there may be some significant improvement in the next month, so may be able to work on a TED application for Metagov before that deadline. Regardless, if anyone else would like to nominate themselves (or me :) ) to take on the big topic of collaborative governance... the more voices the better.
