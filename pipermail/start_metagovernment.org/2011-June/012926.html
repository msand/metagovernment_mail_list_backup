Hi Thomas,

yes, please do move the discussion of Votorola in the other list.

We spoke about crossforum before. I do not agree that Crossforum has a
special status. There are multiple reason why I don't do.

For once when we spoke about it, it could not handle the generations
system that is in Vilfredo. As such it is not "Meta" to Vilfredo. Has
that changed?

Speaking personally, I have noticed that each person feels a special
connection for their own project or for the project they identify most
or for the solution that envision.I do for Vilfredo, you do for
Crossforum, Manuel did for Candiwi, and Owen for StratML...

Sometimes we feel our project deserves to be the special project of
Metagovernment.
I personally do not feel the same for Crossforum, and I am not even
sure it is a good thing that any project is the "special" project. As
I feel this curbs the creativity of other projects.

If, for example, someone were to come out with a way to link different
projects alternative to Crossforum, what would the list need to do?
If Crossforum is the "special" project we would need to refuse the
other project, take a stance and say this is our way of doing things
because .... If not we can just equally embrace it, and explain that
in some cases is better using one and in others is better using the
other. My attitude is that the world is big, full of opportunities,
and as such we should always embrace.

As such I don't think it would be correct, for example, for crossforum
to be the Metagovernment/dev system, but the crossforum/dev or
Metagovernment/crossforum/dev

In any case, name aside, please do move the crossforum discussion in
another list. However you want to call it.

Also what is this meeting going to be: will it be a development
meeting on crossforum and pollwiki, or will we speak about ALL the
projects that are being involved, and present the projects to people
that have not been involved with them?

Cheers,
Pietro
