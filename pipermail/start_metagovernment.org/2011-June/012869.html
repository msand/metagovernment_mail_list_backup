We spoke offlist yesterday and came to the consent, that there is indeed 
a rule in regard to one's *physical* disposition in public space:

If one has the choices of:

(i) Converge, come together with those who agree.

(ii) Diverge, spread out amongst others. Reach out to those who agree 
across the intervening distances.

... he should always use (ii) over (i) if both have the same cost. But 
there seems to be a threshhold of number of people, below which it is 
more beneficial to choose (i) and above which it is more beneficial to 
choose (ii).
