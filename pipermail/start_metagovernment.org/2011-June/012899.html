Ed Pastore wrote:
> But, Michael, Thomas, and Conseo, I have to admit, I have lost
> perspective of the difference between Metagov, Votorola, and
> Crossforum as they exist in your mind. Is a pollwiki strictly a
> function of Votorola or of the crossforum? Or do you see it as
> simply a discussion tool that can be annexed into any voting system
> (i.e., free-range drafting)?

Pollwikis are databases.  They're the primary structural components in
the voting network: http://zelea.com/project/outcast/dep.xht#wiki
I added a summary of the network here, to clarify *it's* purpose:
http://zelea.com/project/outcast/

As you can see from that, pollwikis must remain independent of any
particular technical facility - Crossforum Theatre, Votorola
vote-server, or anything like that.

Further, a *multi-area* pollwiki (like we have) must be independent of
the areas it covers: http://zelea.com/project/outcast/dep.xht#area
So our pollwiki has no technical or "political" affiliations.
