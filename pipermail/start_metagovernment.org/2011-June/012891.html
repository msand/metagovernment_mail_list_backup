> The Appraisal System: Summary of the Method
>
> The appraisal system is a filter that receives proposals as inputs and then
> either "turns back" each proposal or passes it through to implementation.
> The three independent appraisers for each proposal answer questions about
> the proposal, and then the question/answer pairs serve as inputs to a
> software decision module that actually makes the decision.  The appraisers
> are information gatherers, not decision makers. The system effectively and
> efficiently implements "nobody objects" consensus. It makes no attempt to
> implement "everybody agrees" consensus.
>
