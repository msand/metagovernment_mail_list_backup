I would very much like to hear from any projects wishing to report,  
including hearing about any help those projects may be asking for. I  
am hoping that Matías will update us on Eudemocracia, and that others  
may report on their projects.

If crossforum cannot be meta to Vilfredo, then I see that as a  
technical failing, not an inherent limitation. I have found Vilfredo  
to be unique in its approach to building consensus, and I think its  
function may be a vital aspect of future deliberative processes.

I thought about replying to Michael's comment on the wiki, but I  
figured it could wait for the meeting. My feeling is that while there  
may indeed be a wealth of information in the months of list-based  
discussion, not everyone has time or ability to read through that much  
intense technical discussion and comprehend it thoroughly. I would  
very much like to hear more than a few minutes about the nature of  
pollwiki and crossforum. In that discussion, it would also be  
appropriate to cover the nature of these projects in the wider  
ecosystem of projects, and how they can be developed without  
undermining Metagov's umbrella status.

I'm altering the wiki to reflect the above, including rearranging the  
items on the agenda for better flow. I put "presentation of active  
projects" before solicitations for help, so that attendees can have a  
broader view of what projects are up to and make a more informed  
decision about where their help could be best applied.
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Startup/Meetings/9

I still have the question: can Skype handle a voice meeting format?  
And if so, how?
