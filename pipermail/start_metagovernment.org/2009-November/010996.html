Over the past week or so, there has been an argument over the addition  
of a link to the home page of the wiki. Coinciding with that is the  
rather lengthy discussion/dispute at the bottom of:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Talk:Main_Page

I haven't had much spare time recently, but the debate seems to center  
around one user's addition of a link on the home page to this page:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Why_this_project_is_needed.
And the other user saying that the link is not a consensus edit and/or  
that the above page is not well written.

I tend to side with the idea that the page is not really a good  
descriptor of the Metagovernment project as a whole, and I don't see a  
need for this link on the homepage. But I also certainly don't want to  
be some sort of edit-reverting overlord, so I am asking other members  
of this community to have a look and see what you think.
