Please keep on participating in the "Wall Of Text" discussion in Vilfredo.

As a programmer in Vilfredo I really need your input on this. After the
first generation we already found that the solution we were thinking (a hard
limit defined by whoever asks the question) is not well accepted at all. If
anyone thought that those voting were useless now you have your answer.

But we still need to find out a better way.
Today is the last day to suggest some new ideas, and then this european
evening I will move it on to the voting phase.

You can add your suggestions trying to integrate existing ideas, and bring
new ones here:

http://vilfredo.org/viewquestion.php?q=67&room=Vilfredo

(Notice the "room" part of the URL. If you go to the normal address you will
NOT find this question. We are starting to differentiate the place where we
pose questions, to give the possibilities to people to ask private
questions)

Another really good way in which you can help, is in finding proposals you
did not support
(you find them here:
http://vilfredo.org/viewhistoryofquestion.php?q=67&room=Vilfredo )
and then modify them so you would be willing to support them.
