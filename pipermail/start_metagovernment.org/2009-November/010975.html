Ed Pastore wrote:
> http://www.truthmapping.com/about.php
> What do you think? Could a project such as Candiwi or Votorola use a format 
> something like this?

Yes, but not by integrating it into the voting engine, or whatever.
Then you'd get a "wall of code", all closed off.

To keep it open, the interconnections among the media must be loose.
So then people are free to use *any* discussion medium off shelf
(truthmapping, debategraph, plain email list); any drafting medium off
shelf (wikis, candiwis, blogs); and any voting engine.  Only the
voting engine would need to expose some compatibility intefaces (very
simple ones).

However, it requires the difference engine at center.  You should take
a look at that, Ed.  The arguments for it aren't deeply technical.
But if the arguments are sound, there'll soon be a functional diff
engine.  Someone's gonna code it (it's a sweet project), and it'll
start pulling all the projects together, willee nillee.
