Thanks to the really good support of my friend Derek Paterson, Vilfredo is
now officially open source.
You can join the project at
http://code.google.com/p/vilfredo-goes-to-athens/

Also the first new version of Vilfredo has just been updated. Now when you
ask a new question you are given the possibility to set up a room for it to
appear. In this way your question only appears to the people that go to that
room.

You also have a link that shows all the question you have posed.
