You want a grassroots approach, a folksonomy.

  http://openkollab.referata.com/wiki/Category:Objective_property
  http://openkollab.referata.com/wiki/Category:Subjective_property
  http://openkollab.referata.com/wiki/Category:Self-expressive_property

Those pages list various properties.  Properties are kind of like
tags.  You are free to define your own (we ask only that you keep the
objective facts separate from the subjective expressions, and back
them up with references).  So it's effectively a folksonomy.
