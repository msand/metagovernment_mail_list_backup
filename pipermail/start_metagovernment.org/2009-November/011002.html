I too support the revision linked to by Matias.

Also, wanted to thank Marcos and Ed for responding to Main Page edits
so promptly, and to thank StalkingTime for his contributions and for
the opportunity to discuss.

That said, this edit war is *ugly* (yikes), but it is also, in itself,
a good demonstration of 'why the project is needed' -- or at least why
some of the projects in metagov are needed. Wiki's (apparently) are
not very good at either synthesis or consensus.

So, with these goals in mind, I pose the question to the group (using
a slightly different phrasing) :

::  What are *your* motivations for participating in metagovernment?

My guess is, there will be several different responses among the
members of the list, since this has been discussed recently.

And, that a large part of the controversy over StalkingTime's edits
lies in this diversity of responses.

Also, I would add a second part to the question:

:: What is the most efficient way to have this conversation? by Email?
in Vilfredo? Votorola? Candiwi? using StratML?
