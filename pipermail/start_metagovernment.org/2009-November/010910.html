Well,

speaking for Vilfredo, we are setting up a system so you can log in through
your facebook account. This should be active within the next week. Before
the talk that I will give. For now it should just be a system that let you
log in on VgtA if you are logged in on Facebook. Eventually there will be a
FB application that let you echo what you do in Vilfredo on Facebook.

But we are not just selling our soul to Facebook.

We are also selling our soul to... , ... ehm, we are also adding a system so
people can just log in using their twitter account, and so on. We can do
this because the Pareto front does not change, if 2 or more people vote in
the same way. So you can log in 100 times with 100 different accounts,
express your views, and the result will be the same.

I am not sure how all those systems that use this ancient prehistoric idea
of (ugh!) voting, and (aargh!) summing up the votes, will do. ;-)
