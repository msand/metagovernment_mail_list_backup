I've cleared a path for new users to register at least their
geolocals, so they can get themselves on the map.  If anyone can spare
20 minutes or so to test it, here's how:

  1. Start on your user page: http://u.zelea.com/w/Special:MyPage

     If you're a new user, that page won't exist yet.  Create it and
     you'll see the registration instructions. *

  2. Follow the registration instructions.

  3. Post a diff URL to the list.

     You must be one of the 2 authors in the diff.  That means you'll
     need a draft of your own.  The location of your draft depends on
     the issue you're interested in:
     http://u.zelea.com:8080/v/w/MyDraft?p=G!p!mho&pin=Ed-EdpastoreCom&rw=http%3A%2F%2Fmetagovernment.org%2Fw%2Findex.php5
     http://u.zelea.com:8080/v/w/MyDraft?p=G!p!m00&pin=Ed-EdpastoreCom&rw=http%3A%2F%2Fmetagovernment.org%2Fw%2Findex.php5

     Once you have a draft with some changes in it, then follow the
     icon to the difference bridge.  That's the URL you must post.

  4. Look for your post here, it should appear in about 10 minutes:
     http://metagovernment.tuxfamily.org/crossforum/#c=DG

     You probably won't see your location on the map immediately.  It
     can take a while for new geolocals to get into the voter
     register.  You'll probably have to wait 2-4 hours.

Is this OK for starters?  Please give it a try, and let me know.
