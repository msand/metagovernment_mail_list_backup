This is something I have feared since the beginning of this project.  
There certainly are good reasons for us to continue development  
regardless of what FB does, because they are supremely closed:

- Only Facebook users (who thus defer to the Facebook terms of  
service) may participate.

- Facebook can remove questions, posts and/or users as they please.

- Facebook can provide loads of info about participants to other  
interests.

- The end-results are unlikely to be oriented to either synthesis or  
consensus.

However, with all of that said, we may be able to simply subsume them  
(ambitious much?:) by integrating FB questions into the crossforum  
theater.
