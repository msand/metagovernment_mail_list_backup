Scoring needs a bar to score against. My proposal is to draft needs as well as 
contributions (one idea would be to simply use semantic properties for that, 
but the technical stuff is not yet clear to me). That way people can both 
describe the amount of work (or prerequisites) and what they are willing to 
support themselves. Better than an abstract mathematical algorithm to generate 
a score, this would give you a clear hint (like a budget summary) on how much 
support compared to its needs each proposal has. Work is imo the most 
important category as it shows how serious the participants are about their 
plan, but you could also use money or even weaker sympathy values.
The program would generate the budget summary and give a more sophisticated 
overview which can also be used to fine tune the rules for decision and also 
see how well supported a position is besides its vote count.

Anybody interested in this? Any further resources on that? This is what is my 
main interest, so any help is appreciated. I have (losely) summarized my pov 
here http://u.zelea.com/w/User:4consensus_WebDe/Budgeting_system
