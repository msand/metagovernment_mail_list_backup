Ed Pastore wrote:
> However, with all of that said, we may be able to simply subsume
> them (ambitious much?:) by integrating FB questions into the
> crossforum theater.

I don't think it would be overly ambitious, though it might pan out as
more of an exchange than a simple subsumption.  They have a
rudimentary voting facility that doesn't easily dovetail with other
media, such as drafting or discussion - not to mention other voting
facilities.  So they won't likely be strong on that front.  We could
maybe show them a thing or two.

On the other hand, they might still manage to attract lots of users.
Not all of them would remain 100% satisfied, of course, and some might
even stir up trouble for the company.  We could maybe take some of the
pressure off by offering connections to alternative toolsets.

There could be gains all round.
