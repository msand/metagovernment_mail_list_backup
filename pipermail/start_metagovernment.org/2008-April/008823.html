I would imagine this happens via edits and deletions -- survival of
the fittest. There are people who have taken it upon themselves to
monitor recent changes to particular pages (and the interface makes
this particularly easy)... kind of like a self-appointed editorial
system. The degree of consensus can be determined by the rate of page
edits over time, and the number of different people who submit these
edits. Wikipedia has a 'contentious article' flag that is placed by
bot on pages with particularly high edit rates. Not sure how this
would translate to a political system where the content is not purely
informative, but maybe this description is helpful??
