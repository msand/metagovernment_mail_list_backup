Certainly there will be certain aspects of government, which our system cannot
accommodate for. In any event that requires immediate action, you cannot wait
till the community has formed a consensus and you may need a government
body to handle those situations in a responsible manner.

The potential for abuse and corruption could however be somewhat mitigated
by action plans on how to handle such situations. The community can attempt
to anticipate and build consensus upon appropriate actions to as many events
as they can conceive, before they occur.
