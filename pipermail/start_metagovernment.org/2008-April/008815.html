wrote:

> A better voting system was my original idea too, but I think we have to
> go a little bit further than that. The System I was thinking of would
> let you cast your vote online, nut not only for candidates in an election,
> but for every law that goes through parliament.
>

Is online voting the solution?

Consider the definition of Web 2.0 -- which certainly includes online voting
as a primary component: "I like this music, video, or website. Show me more
exactly like it." But voting isn't the definition of Web 2.0, and I would
argue that voting isn't the definition of Metagovernment either.

One idea I had to usurp the system was to create an Open Party that would
allow people to elect officials that took an oath to Open Democracy. Members
could sign up and recommend new courses of action, and/or vote for
legislation with thumbs up/down controls. In this example, voting becomes a
small component of the overall solution.
