Hello to Everyone!

My name is Christopher Ritter. I'm a user experience designer from Dayton,
Ohio, USA. I've been professionally designing software for a variety of
companies (including McAfee.com, Cybertrust, and the U.S. Military) for over
a decade. Currently I am working for a venture capitalist on redesigning a
medical management application to help with the Healthcare Crisis in our
country -- at least, that's how I look at it!

A few years ago I attempted to disconnect from the corporate teat by
starting my own company: a "neighborhood network" which connected the
residents to the local businesses and authorities. The "pie in the sky" idea
was to digitally connect every part of the city from the mayor down to the
bums on the street -- along with every shop and cop in-between.
Unfortunately, the idea was a little ahead of the Eastern Standard Time
Zone.

My passion through it all continues to be "social networking," as I see this
as the great evolution of our species that truly connects us all. But in
order for us to retain our freedoms in an interconnected world, a
"metagovernment" is essential to an "open source" society. I look forward to
contributing my thoughts and abilities to the development of these ideals.
