Some of the recent posts have been moving towards a discussion about decision making tools. I don't mean voting (aka. opinion-polling), which in my view is just a substitute for decision making to be reserved for worst case scenario but proper holistic, integrative planning and decision making.

Albeit voting is improved using the no-one looses principle, that also dilutes the one thing voting has in its favour - ie it gets a decision (for good or for ill) regardless of not having enough time to bottom out the issues fully.

I've hinted that I think there may be a partial answer in some of the Constraints Management stuff I outlined but I'm interested in what else people have come across to facilitate and structure the process.

Anyone got any specific ideas in that regard?
