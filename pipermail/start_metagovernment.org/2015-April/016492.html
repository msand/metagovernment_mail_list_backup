So, every person X gets y votes in each voting period (a voting period can
be a day or hour or second).
Every person would loose their votes slowly if the person does not vote
with rate r, so this constitutes an incentive to vote.
The amount A of votes a voter X has, can never really drop below a certain
amount, which is the amount of votes that each voter is left with,
once the absolute amount of votes y gained each period is equal to the
amount of votes each voter loses each period because of percentage r of the
total amount of votes A a voter X currently has.

So these are kind of the basic mechanics.
Each voter X can now vote an proposals/projects/...  or on persons...
By doing that, the voter X looses the amount of votes he voted on that
proposal or project or person and the person, project or proposal and the
like that have been voted for, GAIN that amount.

The voting system i just described could be expresses in a couple of
formulas, but maybe something descriptive is better.
