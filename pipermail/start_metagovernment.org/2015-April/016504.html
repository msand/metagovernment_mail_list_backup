The more I think about it. It should be probably noted in
every proposal what majority it requires.
This would be nonsensical in representative democracy, because
everybody would propose 50%.
But ordinary folks are different, they understand that they cannot start
war with the same majority as they are able to decide about road sign.
That is probably one of the main characteristics of deliberative
democracy, that the actors seek balance, appropriate state,... not
maximum as governments.

So as the result, some people may refuse to support any proposal where
is simple majority required. Do you have any better idea ?
