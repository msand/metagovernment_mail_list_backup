Sorry, I'm lost. Exactly what problem is this multiple voting proposal
supposed to solve? I see no advantages and at least two big
disadvantages:
1) Single issue voters will hoard their votes and expend them all on
the issue they care about, skewing the results.
2) People don't worry about losing votes: They routinely fail to cast
the ones they already get! Providing more votes just decreases their
value even further, increasing the number that just won't be cast.
