Welcome, Jason.

I should point out that this list is more about opening the decision- 
making aspects of governance, not just the implementation. Many of the  
projects here aspire to the eventual obsolescence (or at least  
minimization) of elected officials. The idea being that real democracy  
is now possible thanks to the power of online collaborative tools.

But of course, there is room for politicians who are willing to push  
that process forward. The most notable resource in that regard is  
Demoex, which has successfully entered Swedish government on the  
platform of the representative acting in accord with decisions made by  
crowdsourcing. See:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demoex

Also of note are Partido de Internet of Spain (which has a couple  
members on this list) and Senator Online of Australia.
http://www.partidodeinternet.es/
http://www.senatoronline.org.au/

If you're not looking to go that far with pushing toward inclusive  
democracy, then in addition to Mark's reference, you might want to  
check out Sunlight Labs:
http://sunlightlabs.com/

Congrats, and welcome to the list.
