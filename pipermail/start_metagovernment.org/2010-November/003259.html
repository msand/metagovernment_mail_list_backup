OK, well my upgrade backlog has sat idle for a year, but I have  
finally gotten through most of it! You can thank the American holiday  
and failed travel plans for this found time. :)

Specifically:

- E-mail notifications are now enabled. Go to http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Special:Preferences 
  and look at the bottom of the User profile tab. Hopefully the  
hosting service won't balk at the increased e-mail load. Please be  
conservative in turning these options on. If e-mail for the site goes  
down entirely, please let me know at ed at edpastore dot com.

- A WYSIWYG editor is now enabled. Go to http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Special:Preferences 
  and click on the Gadgets tab. I am pretty sure that this will  
default to on for new users.

- The Cite.php extension is finally installed. Sorry for the  
incredible delay, Michael.

- The MediaWiki software has been updated. Let me know if you notice  
anything weird. It was lagging far behind at 1.12.0. It is now at  
1.16.0. The list of fixes/updates between those is enormous. The broad  
overview is here: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MediaWiki_release_history

For your reference, here is my upgrade backlog page (I still have one  
more to work on; hopefully soon):
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Upgrade_backlog

Plus there is the bulletin board software, which I am hoping to set up  
on the tuxfamily.org site. I'll write about that soon, but am done  
working on metagov stuff for right now.
