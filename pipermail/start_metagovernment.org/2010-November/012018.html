Hi Jason,

I don't know of a primer, but can point you to a couple of sites of
interest:

The Government Open Source Conference (GOSCON <http://goscon.org/>) took
place in Portland last month.  The videos from the sessions are in post
production now, and will eventually be up on the site for public viewing.  I
would think this will be a fairly rich source of info for you once they're
up.  I'll try to remember to alert the list once they're available.

Part of the event was IgniteGov, a format where a presenter has 20 slides
that automatically advance every 15 seconds.  The videos from that event can
be seen here <http://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=ignitegov&aq=f>.

Congratulations on winning your election!  We can certainly use more open
source advocates in government.
