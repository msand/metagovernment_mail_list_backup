others being subsumed by these:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Main_Page/2010-redesign-4

Please look them over and see if you like one of these, or if they  
spark you to want to draft your own.

If you think one should be changed a little, please just make a new  
draft (just change the numeral at the end of the URL to a new number  
and the wiki will present you with the option to start a new page;  
feel free to ask if you need more help from there). That lets the  
author of the original decide if he/she wants to support yours or to  
defend their original proposal.
