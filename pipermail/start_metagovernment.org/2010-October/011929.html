Hi Marcos,

It looks like you have some sketches of theory (not fully documented,
like you say).  I also have some sketches and I thought they would be
enough to run with, but now I'm doubtful.  I keep thinking that
whatever happens as a consequence of the technology is going to
depend, in part, on what was *forseen* to happen (or not foreseen).
That would put a premium on the kind of forward verification you get
from research, publication and scholarly critique.  (I know of no
other way.)  So I'm taking a challenge to post no more theory unless
it's in a roughly publishable form.  I don't think that's been done
before, because I can't find any comprehensive body of e-dem theory in
publication.  If you find any yourself (or finish drafting your own)
please share.
