I've started expanding and updating the Metagovernment FAQ here:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/FAQ

I'd like to invite you to help refine the answers - often I'm paraphrasing
from conversations on this list and sometimes I'm writing from my own ideas,
so I'm sure there's room for improvement.

Please also post new questions and/or answers, bearing in mind the aims for
the FAQ page which I outlined earlier:

1) To explain what is quite a complicated idea in bite-sized chunks, e.g.
"What does the name Metagovernment mean?", "What is meant by radical
transparency?"
2) To anticipate and counter some of the preconceptions people have about
what Metagovernment is and how it would (not) work, e.g. "Won't people be
influenced to vote a certain way by the media?"
3) To present questions which have more than one competing answer, e.g. "Do
anonymous interactions have a place within the metagovernment?"
4) To imagine parts of the project which have not been implemented, e.g.
"How will discussion take place?" (This question is currently being discussed
in Vilfredo <http://vilfredo.org/viewquestion.php?q=98> - hopefully we'll
reach an answer which can be posted on the Metagovernement FAQ)
