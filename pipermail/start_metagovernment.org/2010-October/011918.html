Cropssroad is a group that is working on the EU roadmap for ICT and egov.
They have asked for what are the key challenges for ICT in egov. The
question is important, as it might be used by the EU to define the
agenda for the next years, and who to give money too. The tool that
they are using is uservoice. So you can find the question at
http://crossroad.uservoice.com/forums/77431-data-powered-collective-intelligence-and-action-

Now, I think we can have our point of view evaluated if we:
1) develop through Vilfredo an answer to the question that we all agree on.
2) once we find a pareto front of the answers we agree on we copy them
on the board, and then ALL vote for them.

I have just made a question on vilfredo for that and you can find it at:
http://vilfredo.org/viewquestion.php?q=95

Please participate.
