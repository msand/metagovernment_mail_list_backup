so what if we permit to users to open a new question, from an existing one.

Maybe the two questions could be linked, so that people who are
answering to one could be invited to answer to the other. And even
from the website the link would be present. Also the person should
have a shortcut to repropose a proposal that has already been

versions of the same question.

rewrite the question than to bridge their differences.
