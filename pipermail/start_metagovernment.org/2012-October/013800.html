With regards to our 'Conceptual Overview Diagram' 
(http://wiki.piratenpartei.de/wiki/images/3/30/DSFS-ConceptualOverview-A.jpg)
the 'Information System' deals with the information more in a sense of a 
Wiki or Knowledge base. So you can say it is fact based information specific 
for a context. Whereas the 'Discussion System' deals more with the 
information in a sense of pro and con arguments. While the 'Decision System' 
deals with all kind of information it generates new ones in form of 
decisions.

So that way I personally see Votorola (as far as I understand it) in the 
genre of 'Discussion Systems'. Indeed all systems interact and share data. 
So the distinction between the three systems may sometimes be fluent...


But the most important point is, that there is an evolution of the 
'Discussion System' by design due to competion between different 
implementations of specific solutions for 'discussion techniques' and 
'opinion forming'.
