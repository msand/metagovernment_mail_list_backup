FWIW: IME the best time slot for something like this is around 1pm
Pacific / 9pm Berlin / 7am Sydney, on Saturday(pm)/Sunday(am). Depends
of course who's participating, but holding it on the weekend makes
sure it doesn't interfere with most work schedules, and that time of
day is at least doable for most.

http://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/meeting.html is a useful tool
for visualizing this.
