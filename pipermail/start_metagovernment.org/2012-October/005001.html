Hello!

I'm starting up http://nerdServe.org, a "listserve lottery", inspired by
http://thelistserve.com/, but specifically for the
civicHacker/Gov20/FLOSS/maker/nerd ****community.  And I think the ideas
discussed on this list would be particularly interesting.

The idea is to randomly select one subscriber each day to email all the
other subscribers with a few words on anything they like - but hopefully
something related to their work, their tooling, process, philosophy,
something they'd like to build, collaborate on, etc.

It's a daily email to inspire, expose you to unfamiliar technologies and
practices, connect you to folks who might not attend the same conferences.

I'll fire the list up once I get the first 100 subscribers, at which time
we'll make things a little exclusive by requiring a recommendation from an
existing subscriber to join.  We'll moderate out anything offensive.
 Lottery winners will have one week to draft their emails.  Winners will be
selected randomly, so there's nothing keeping you from being selected more
than once.  Your email will never be shared, unless you include it in your
message.  All content is
http://creativecommons.org/**licenses/by/3.0/<http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/>
.


I can't identify subscribers until they choose to do so in their messages,
but I will say we've already got some big names in the hacker/gov20 space.


So, hoping you'll consider joining: http://nerdServe.org<http://nerdserve.org/>


Thanks!
