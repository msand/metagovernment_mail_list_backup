Ahoi,

Yesterday our third 'International E-Democracy Talk' took place. This time 
Sai presented the concept of "MakeYourLaws.org" (MYL).

In addition the session started with the regular weekly meeting of our 
working group to decide on some topics rerarding 'Die Grundidee'.
You can have a look on our short notes here:
http://meinungsfindungstool.piratenpad.de/2012-10-15-Notizen (in german 
language only)

As we are used to in our last meetings, we also recorded the mumble session.
It may be worth listen to it. You can download the audio here:
https://dl.dropbox.com/u/107231073/AG%20Meinungsfindungstool/Session%20recordings/Mumble-2012-10-15-21-08-18-mumble.piratenpartei-nrw.de-Mixdown.ogg


WHAT HAPPENED?

In the beginning of yesterdays session we decide on the following topics:
Top 1 - Dropbox-Account für die AG Meinungsfindungstool: this is going to be 
decided via a doodle poll that's to be created this week.

Top 2 - Die Grundidee / Basic Idea: this is also to be decided via a doodle 
poll that's also to be created this week. The text of 'Die Grundidee' is 
translated after it is created as a wiki article.

Top 3 - Abstimmung weiteres Vorgehen und Anpassung der Roadmap: postponed to 
the next meeting.



To See all details please refer to the sessions note pad mentioned above.


At around 9.30pm Sai's presentation of the concept behind "MakeYourLaws.org" 
(MYL) started. He gave us a very interesting insight of what MYL is all 
about. Than we had a very intersting QnA afterwards.
Please find more details on the session and the QnA in the following pad:
http://meinungsfindungstool.piratenpad.de/InternationalEDemocracyTalk


WHAT IS COMING NEXT?

Currently we have no next presenter for an upcoming 4th International 
E-Democracy Talk. So if you want to present your concept/idea/tool don't 
hesitate to contact us!

Due to some time limits we may need to schedule the presentation talks to a 
different time slot than our regular weekly working group meeting. Therefor 
the date and time for upcoming presentation talks may be doodle in the 
future.


WHEN WE MEET AGAIN?

The working group AG Meinungsfindungstool meet on next Monday, 22nd October 
at 9pm UTC+2 for the regular 90 minutes to discuss current outstanding 
topics. Agenda follows on subsequent email. Eventually we are going to have 
an additional ad hoc meeting this week.

The International E-Democracy Talk will be scheduled to another time slot as 
soon as we have the next guest speaker.


If you have any queries, don't hesitate to contact our working group at
Ag-meinungsfindungstool at lists.piratenpartei.de
https://service.piratenpartei.de/listinfo/ag-meinungsfindungstool

Cheers
marc
