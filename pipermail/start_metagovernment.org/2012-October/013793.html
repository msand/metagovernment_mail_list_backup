Ahoi,

Yesterday our spontaneous mumble session took place. Main topic was 
"Presentation of various Concepts and Ideas regarding Opinion Forming".

The notes to the session can be found here:
http://meinungsfindungstool.piratenpad.de/2012-10-03-Notizen

We also recorded the session and you can download the audio here:
http://bayfiles.com/file/mUgZ/1IjnH9/Mumble-2012-10-03-21-20-04-mumble.piratenpartei-nrw.de-Mixdown.mp3


WHAT HAPPENED?

After a short round of introductions to each attendee, Slash gave a short 
overview of the current status of our working group 'AG 
Meinungsfindungstool': he expressed, that from our point of view, the 
process of decision-making can be seperated into three interacting systems: 
Information, Discussion and Decision System. Our working group deals with 
all questions regarding the Discussion System.

Then we talked about the intention of coming together and the current 
problems we face with Liquid Feedback and other tools of this genre.

The highlight of the session was definetely Pietros presentation of Vilfredo 
goes to Athens (Vilfredo.org), especially the concept of Pareto Front. This 
is a realy amazing tooling for automated moderation of discussions. But with 
some drawbacks also.

You should listen to our audio recording to learn more about the concept and 
ideas Pietro talked about!


WHAT IS COMING NEXT?

We plan to have a single session for each concept or idea to be presented. 
The main intention is to get closer in touch with different projects and 
learn more about other concepts and iedeas.


WHEN WE MEET AGAIN?

The next session is coming on Monday, 8th October 2012 at 9.30pm (CET+2). In 
this session Ronald is giving us a deep inside into his concept of 
"Democracy 2.0".


If you have any queries, don't hesitate to contact our working group!
