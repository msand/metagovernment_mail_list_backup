WHAT?

Karsten Weihe presents the concept and idea behind the internet portal 
"fundiert-entscheiden.de".

  fundiert entscheiden = decide on a sound basis
  A collaborative plattform for systematic, factual, to-the-point edition of 
controversial questions of all kinds.
