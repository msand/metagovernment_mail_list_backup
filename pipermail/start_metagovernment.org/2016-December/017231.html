We use mail in here in Oregon.  Also now automatic registration.  No
polling place but  ballot drops are around if you like.

It costs us something extra a ballot because the mailer is very expensive.

If I were improving this, I would add a user computer aspect to generate a
hash  from the filled in ballot.  On processing, the hash is recreated and
published with my name.  Since we mail the ballots out a couple of weeks
before election day, we can publish this continually before we know how
people voted.  Here we process before officially counting because the count
total release is a different button that is not pressed until the election
is over.  So we just need an extra button.

We already track who has voted to see who has not voted and then the
csndidates follow up. And if the county rejects the ballot then the county
trys to follow up.
