Hey, guys, I don't see any reference to XML in the agenda at 
http://www.adobegovernmentassembly.com/program.php  However, I don't know 
how an informed discussion of the topic of open government can be conducted 
without reference to XML, and if XML will be mentioned, I'd like to post a 
link at http://xml.gov/index.asp#November

Also, I see that Alan Cohn, DHS's DAS for Policy and Strategic Plans, is 
scheduled to speak.  It would be good if he could mention AIIM's emerging 
Strategy Markup Language (StratML) standard, since it is highly supportive 
of the concept of open government.

It would also be good if:  a) the PDF Fillable form --  
http://xml.gov/stratml/forms/StratMLForm.pdf -- that Ed developed could be 
updated to conform to the latest version of the StratML schema --  
http://xml.gov/stratml/index.htm#xsd -- and b) Adobe could issue a statement 
about it at the same time that AIIM announces publication of StratML Part 1 
as an ANSI/AIIM standard.

Daniel, sorry I won't be able to attend the World eParliament conference. 
I'll post a link to the agenda at http://xml.gov/index.asp#November
