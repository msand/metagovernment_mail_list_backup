I've attended the last three years. It's not cheap, but I've been able 
to save money by staying at a friend's place in New Jersey, and using 
public transportation. Anyone who is considering going should also stay 
alert to related, but free conferences that are put together in the 
preceding days. For example, this year, some students NYU put on a 
Participation Camp that was excellent.
