The fact we have not unsubscribed from the listserv suggests that we haven't 
given up hope, but from my perspective, it still seems to be unclear whether 
many, if any of us are pursuing the same objective(s).

At least it remains to be seen whether we are sufficiently committed to 
devote the necessary time, effort, and resources to accomplish any 
objective(s).  If so, we should be able to transform a strategic plan into a 
performance plan, which could in turn produce measurable results.

Please recall the reason I subscribed to the Metagov listserv was to explore 
prospects for using Metascore to enable feedback on goals and objectives 
documented in StratML format.  Thus far, I have not seen much evidence that 
anyone else shares my objective in that regard.  That's not to disparage 
anyone else's objectives but merely to suggest that perhaps we may be 
lacking the basis for producing anything together.

Time will tell and the results (or lack thereof) will speak for themselves.

Owen
