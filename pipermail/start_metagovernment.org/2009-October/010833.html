Hello everybody,

Votorola is beginning with alpha-testing and we would like to invite you 
to play around with it. ;-)

Votorola is a combination of a poll-server (the vote-engine etc.) and a 
poll-wiki (the drafting medium).
I started a poll named "Open Governance 2010" which we could use, to 
plan the proposed conference.
I voted for Ed because I like the conference plan that he posted in the 
Metagov-wiki. But I thought it needed a few changes. So I posted this 
revised plan:

http://t.zelea.com/wiki/User:ThomasvonderElbe_GmxDe/Open_Governance_2010.

What do you think? If you agree, please vote for it here (Together we'll
convince Ed ;-) ) :

http://t.zelea.com:8080/v/w/Pollspace/?p=MetaGov&u=ThomasvonderElbe+GmxDe.

Or you can vote directly for Ed. And maybe post a plan of your own,
like I did.

By clicking on a candidate in the poll-browser all his voters get shown. 
The link right in front of the candidates/voters name links to his 
position draft in the wiki.

If help is needed for the login or something else, there is a help page: 
http://t.zelea.com/wiki/Help:Contents.

Have fun!

Best wishes,
Thomas
