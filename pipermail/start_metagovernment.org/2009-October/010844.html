Thanks for bringing this up, Matias. I had mentioned it in the meta- 
organization proposal
http://metagovernment.org/pipermail/start_metagovernment.org/2009-June/001623.html
and I think others in that thread agreed with the idea that we should  
move to something like Open Governance.

In addition to Open Governance, other names were suggested in a  
Vilfredo process (initiated by Jacki):
http://vilfredo.org/viewquestion.php?q=53
where Collaborative Governance won the latest round (though not many  
people participated). You can see the history here:
http://vilfredo.org/viewhistoryofquestion.php?q=53

What do others think? Open Governance? Collaborative Governance?

If you have a better idea, perhaps you could add it on the Vilfredo  
proposal.
