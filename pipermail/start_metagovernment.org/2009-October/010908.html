Clearly we can offer a testing service to active projects' developers. (Not
a paid one, of course.) Feedback is something they need and seek but not
always easy to get.

I think I have accounts for:

   - our wiki,
   - the mailing list (naturally),
   - Vilfredo,
   - DemocracyLab,
   - Candiwi (OpenID),
   - and WeVote (Facebook).

They are a lot! Surely now you know where I want to arrive with this. Isn't
there a way to make life easier to the tester? To unify all these accounts?
Maybe OpenID, a common database, or an LDAP server running on our hosting.
This will be the first step to the standard too.

Both software being used by Metagovernment, MediaWiki and Mailman, support
OpenID and LDAP. Candiwi does use OpenID so Manuel and Fabe can help other
projects to implement it. This is a straight-forward option. (Although the
only thing shared between projects would be usernames and nothing else.)
