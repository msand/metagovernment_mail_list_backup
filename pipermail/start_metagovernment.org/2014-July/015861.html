Here’s an interesting article on the Cephu phenomenon.

http://www.slate.com/articles/health_and_science/human_evolution/2012/10/groups_and_gossip_drove_the_evolution_of_human_nature.html

It describes gossip as being the most widely used (and powerful) form of controlling aberrant behavior in a tribal culture.

Given that contemporary tribal culture is operating to such a large degree online, I’m trying to imagine how that would work….(e.g.) on a troll in a forum environment.
