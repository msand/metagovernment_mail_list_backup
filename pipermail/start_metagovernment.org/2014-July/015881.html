I claim there is no path forward other then the present path.  Therefore
something unusual must happen.

What tool would you spread around in anticipation of a specified unusual?
Or an unspecified unusual?

Here is something amusing:

The physics types would agree that the two pillars of the Queen Of Science
are General Relativity and Quantum Mechanics.  Everyone knows one is
wrong.  Some say both are wrong and even both are totally wrong.  For say
Deutsch the necessary solution for dealing with this and related matters
will be of the response "oh, of course, how could it be otherwise".

I wonder what that might mean for human social organization.
