I vote no.  As I've been pointing out on Vilfredo, I'm opposed to
including environmental (or other) justifications for this project,
since they are not directly relevant to it.  I also would prefer to
avoid touchy-feely sounding stuff like "having society work together".
I actually prefer Bentley's original proposal because I believe the
primary motivator for must of us is simply that the existing systems
are broken and it seems to me we need to distinguish our philosophy
from them as a key point on the home page (i.e., insisting on direct
vs. representative democracy).
  Regards,
    Scott
