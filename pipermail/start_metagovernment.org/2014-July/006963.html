Research about belief vs fact is getting a lot of play recently.

Whatever your training and skills facts that conflict with deeply held
values will be ignored.  This is even so if you consciously accept the fact
as true and relevant.  You blink once and you will be shifting yourself and
your stories so that the fact is not there anymore.

Nice.

This brings up the super elector issue as a solution.  You might think
consensus would take care of the matter but not in the real world.  Here we
have a difference between appearances and the reality of democratic
practice and this difference is desired.  One of the reasons is that it
takes a generation to get any consensus.
