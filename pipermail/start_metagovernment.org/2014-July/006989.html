I think the 80% majority required to change the decision rule is too
high and threatens the integrity of the community.  As we grow, the
difficulty of changing our own decision process will increase to the
point where it becomes next to impossible, leading eventually to the
break up of the community.  I propose that a 65% majority is enough.
