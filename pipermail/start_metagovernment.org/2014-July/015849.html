OK, so it sounds like you disagree with the term "few".  I believe F2F
direct democracy starts failing once you cross into double digits, and
becomes unworkable above 3 digits (and, no, I don't count referendum's
as direct democracy because the people have no input into the process
of developing the proposals).  But maybe you'd prefer a different
term?  "Dozens", maybe?

Also, I think the text is not ahistorical: it points out that direct
democracy is how LPA/EEA bands tend to organize themselves, which is
actually *prehistorical*.
