Suppose we ask each, "By what steps do you hope to advance democracy
or otherwise achieve a beneficial outcome?" - and record the answers:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Overplan/soc/person-22

This is a tough technique.  Looking at it, there's probably no way it
could fail to move things forward.  I think the most likely outcome is
that everyone agrees on the first few steps without difficulty, and we
move forward by taking them.  No matter what the outcome, however,
it's hard to see how it could fail to give us useful information on
the question of moving forward.

So we could try this.  Or does anyone have an alternative suggestion?
