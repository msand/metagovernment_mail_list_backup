The decision rule is not really in force.  It never went to a vote in
the entire community to begin with.  Only the sub-group of Votorola
users had a vote.  See 'Metaquestion':
http://metagovernment.org/pipermail/start_metagovernment.org/2011-April/thread.html
http://metagovernment.org/pipermail/start_metagovernment.org/2011-May/thread.html

The reason we currently follow the decision rule is because we have no
other formal rule to follow, *not* because we're forced to follow it.
We follow it freely.

That means we're not forced to obey the supermajority of 80%, either.
When we (all of us, not just Votorola users) decide on a decision
rule, then it'll be our first *binding* decision rule.


I'll back-link to this clarification discussion from:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Talk:Rules
