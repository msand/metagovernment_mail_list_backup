Help People to become "Self Employed" and make it ridiculously easy for
them to collaborate with other "Self Employed" people.
Thus:
 - An open source self hostable plattform where i can apply everything a
company applies while doing business, but to my private leisure and work
life => balance sheet/taxes/planning (break down wishes into executable
small tasks), ....
- show others my skills and interests
- browse others wishes and apply with my skills to solve them => get shares
or money for doing so (bitcoins and the like preferred)

... THIS will bring "every day democratic practice" to peoples life's,
preparing them to take on more and more broad macro layers of government,
until they start to govern the planet with all their fellow world citizens
:-)
