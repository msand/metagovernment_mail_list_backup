Setting a high quorum in the beginning was a bad idea but understandable,
our experience in Airesis where different quorums could be set in each
proposal by the proposer itself shows that many proposals stuck because
where requested high participation, therefore we change and we add the time
constraints that can be set at the beginning of the proposal for the debate
time (obligatory),for the reflection time and finally for the vote windows
time. (the obligatory was by tree choice We discussed for months about this
issue and we had more than 8 different and complicated solution evaluated,
We settle for this and we have being happy with it. The result is that if
you care you participate!  The right quorum number, if you could not live
without could be searched by the median algorithm, Pietro proposed
this system when is necessary to search for a single numerical value I

Problems
WRITE AND VIEW CONTRIBUTIONS (1/1)

The ability to customize the quorum is really very beautiful, but in my
opinion too loose, exposing a group made to suffer for the decisions due to
inertia and lack of participation (see eg. Quorum Fast, really not very
demanding in terms of constraints) . It could be a weapon to drive some
decisions with actions lightning, giving little time for other members to
participate in the discussion and then bring it to a vote and tick
resolution by acting at the hands of a small group of willingness to agree
... if we think through a quorum with discussion time at night than 2 days,
is deliberating in favor of a severance millionaire in a CDA of a large
company acting through Airesis ...

Solution 1 :

Solution 1
WRITE AND VIEW CONTRIBUTIONS (1/1)

1. Allow for each active participant within a group to declare the term of
consent and which it considers to be optimal with respect to the quorum
(via survey or voting within the context of the proposal to the
participation of the same proposal), in order to create a quorum weighted,
shared and dynamic (but with certain date, see below).

Set at the same time a maximum between 15-30 days. than the declared value
by the user, so in case of emergency if the participants agree on the need
... will be worth the weighted average. The maximum limit serves mainly to
limit the impact of the event in which participants Late \ obstructionists
fit values of the quorum required at time intervals of years in the future
... in order to block the proposal or artificially rising and
instrumentally the mean value.

Establishing a maximum value claimable, the resulting weighted average,
even if dynamic, in any case lead to a certain date with regard to the
conclusion of the debate.

2. The definition of the date and duration of the vote should go for a
share-level participants in the proposal.Each participant may declare the
value of life you want and declare its readiness to vote in a certain
period (up to 15-30days?) After which the system would be to weight the
mean value and choose the dates depending on the time frame in which it
would provided the largest number of users potentially voting. You could
take a cue from Doodle.

3. The same system (the active user and participant declares that the
values considered optimal and it yields an average of the maximum values
are fixed to avoid exploitation), could also be applied to the definition
of a quorum for voting.

In this way we will have greater sharing and democracy , eliminating much
of the centralization of power in the hands of today's spokesperson grupp i.
