James,

Thanks. That is a good point that the behavior is separate from the belief.

I slightly disagree with "Your not going to be able to convince them to not
be religious just as much as they could never convince you to be religious."
I have known many people to change their position on this and other core
beliefs. Those changes were often because of information from other people.
I would prefer not to limit our possibilities. I agree it is a very
difficult conversation to have but in some cases it is necessary. In other
cases religions have changed on their positions. There are indications that
many evangelical Christians were pro-choice before the 60's.

I think our biggest challenge is oversimplification.

Cheers,
Bentley
