I'll go along with that, and it's not really even a "euphemism" (i.e.,
this is a feature, not a bug).  So along with "treason", we've got to
become comfortable with the concept that social controls on behavior
are a necessary component of The System, just as they are out here in
the real world (i.e., there are "idiots" who try to cut in lines, talk
on their phones in theaters (or yelling "Fire", ha!), and a wide
variety of other things we've collectively decided are not acceptable,
and it is not "removing free speech" to use whatever social-conformity
tools we have available to restrict them from doing these things).
