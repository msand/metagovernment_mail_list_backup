Would make more sense to someone who just joins the list as well. I do like
TL:DR: but we might start getting questions about what it means more often
then we would like. And, we certainly don't want to confuse someone that
just joined.

Also, the post below is 133 words. So I would propose we move the limit to
below 200 words. That is plenty of space to write a summary.

example due to it pertaining to religion but I think it illustrates the
point)

The Mormon church produced a video about the Book of Mormon. Anti-mormon
users of Youtube mass down-thumbed it and labeled it as inappropriate.
Youtube then auto flagged it as being over 18 required material so you had
to sign in and have a birthday above a certain point to view the video.
Even if you aren't religious you can see the problem. Imagine what would
have happened to Martin Luther King's Speech if it was uploaded to Youtube

James
