Right on!  I've been lurking through this discussion and am feeling both frustrated and inspired.  I'm frustrated because I don't understand why we aren’t using some of the tools that are already out there, like Loomio and Incoma (or whatever) to begin developing something more comprehensive, together.

The authoritarian/dominator system is about to cook us all….and here we sit dithering via e-mail.   Hmmmm.  I wonder, what would it take to begin developing a single project, or platform together?    Isn't that what the spirit of this group is all about?
