I quote sort of:

Put together a mission statement for an account in a *popular* social
network for advertising mission/vision statements.

Here I am thinking *now* and for obvious reasons I am thinking Facebook or
google.

Further:

Here is what I want you to do:  Reply with a +1 or -1.

Note that I am interested in a two character go/no go response not in your
opinions or questions otherwise.  It am not particularly interested to
spending attention on optimizing the proposal for you in this thread.

One thing to remember there is no particular resource requirement but
attention here.  Thus just do a competing thread for the version of the
above you like. But you do not get to talk this to death on this thread.

Still:

My interest in vote counting on my proposal ceases on May 1 2014 CE.

You may be confused by the meta behaviors and misbehaviors we like to do.
There being no obvious way to agree on a mission vision statement, I have
proposed a mission/vision statement as a stub of a mission/vision generator
and it requires very minimal resources for me to act upon creating the
stub.  You also do not get to discuss the stub or the generator or the
decision model in this thread.  You will be limited in efficient criticism
of the social network account I create as a generator.  But I do not much
concern myself with what is generated.  I think it would be good if it
managed to be something that generated an action.  Even if it involved
pizza ordering.

So to emphasize this, any further replies that are longer than 2 characters
counts as a -1.  If it is less than two characters it counts as -1.  If the
two characters are other than a single member of the set {-1,+1} it counts
as a -1.  If it irritates me it counts as -1.
