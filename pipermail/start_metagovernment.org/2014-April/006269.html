Put together a mission statement for an account in a popular social network
for advertising mission/vision statements.

Characteristics:

Free
Easy to create accounts
Use feels easy
Has some sort of liking capability
Have some sort of comment system
Have a way to link out and in

Vision is to integrate with business plan, grant, project, decision
software.

Here is what I want you to do:  Reply with a +1 or -1.

If there are more + than - then I will create some social network account,
announce it, and the + people can come in.  The - people we are not
interested in.

We will then play in that account for a bit and see what happens.
