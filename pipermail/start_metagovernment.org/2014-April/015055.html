TL; DR

Use a popular social network to propose missions/visions

I now start a new thread.  What i am thinking is that no one serious thinks
we have democracy and no one really thinks the reality of democracy would
be wonderful.  And it is not like the decisions that are made however are
identifiable successful.  On the last then maybe an action oriented
approach rather than a decision approach is a goal.  Oh i am not big on
equality either.  So here is a decision mode:  those that can agree on a
mission/vision and go from there.  It is not like there are a shortage of
missions and it is not like i should have to worry about someone thinking
their mission is different than mine so let us nuke them from orbit.  So
the new thread will now be created.
