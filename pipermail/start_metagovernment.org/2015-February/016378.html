Thank you.
As you say "Money is power" (meaning "resources are power") knows 
everybody and at the same time many people still talk seriously about 
national democracies.
I suggest the precise "global plutocracy" is important, because it 
directly negates "democracy", it prevents doublethink. It is also short 
enough to be a headline it is avoiding old terminology that is ridiculed 
by propaganda, it has a "fancy new" word in it (we do not use much 
"plutocracy" in Czech) and it is in international dictionary.

Also thank you for your suggestions. Before your email my idea was that 
first of all I will try more direct approaches of damaging propaganda. 
And I still see it as an interesting experience for me, so I will try.

I also believe that people in metagovernment have similar mindset, but 
as we have seen it is still difficult to express it in words. It is 
difficult to feel as one community if we do not use one worldview and 
terminology (or a finite set of views and terms). But that is history 
for me.
