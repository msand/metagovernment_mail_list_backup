I would strongly suggest you to take a look at the iojs.org community, as
they experiment with improving "open governance" and have a highly
successful real world project.
Other than that, i co-built a community of freelancers and otherwise self
employed people in berlin/germany meetup.com/codingamgios and we currently
try to experiment with ways how to collaborate openly as partners with no
employees and a high degree of transparency.

If you are a programmer, maybe we can outsource each other work and try to
come up with fair conditions, brainstorming on how to use "open governance"
methods and technology like "blockchains" and emerging "crypto contracts"
to make it even better...
