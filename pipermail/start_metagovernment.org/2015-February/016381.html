Hi Tom,

I was also considering a "democratic business" (for others, see wiki 
"workplace democracy", "syndicate" or "syndicalism").

I was thinking that I want to do "rational things" in a democratic business.

But when I do the rational things, that is nohow preventing the others 
from the "irrational things". In fact the may focus more on their 
"irrational totalitarian" business, because I do the rational better 
than they did. Is it so ?

In the moment when I have considered that I may make some "irrational 
business" just for money and *power* of the business, I looked back at 
what I do. I do as others do, but we just share our money in a different 
way. In a way that prevents corruption, but just in my business.

I see future in "democratic business", but first we need to outlaw 
"totalitarian business" IMHO.

Kind regards
Michael
