given the opportunity to govern because to control the power the third
party (the looser) split into two and on side formed a coalition with the
second (looser too by a fraction), the result is. STATUS QUO.
We are governed by a non winner because of secret deal. I am ashamed by
this political scenario but I believe that was directly imported from the
US.

where driven to vote by a famous Italian comedian promising to establish a

Tecnologiedemocratiche.it like me that we even try it out is a classic top

made by the elected.
Consider that Airesis was strongly promoted within the new movement and was
offered 2 months before the election to the comedian but never implemented.

movement!
What i want to say here is, even if you get the unaffiliated to vote the

that is already well oiled to keep the status quo. You have critical mind
so I guess you can see that the only way IMHO is to create critical mass by
a bottom up structured sw. A silent revolution that with overwhelming
number will change the sick status quo.
