I will repost here the five phases of direct democracy projects as they 
do not show up well on my phon

1. The *creative phase*: the single or group proponents of the project 
gather and start to discuss about the project. A document is written, 
and a site is opened.
2. *euphoric phase*: the group starts to involve other people, the 
project is developed, people work hard. The atmosphere around the 
project is euphoric .New functionalities are added every day.
3. The *stagnation phase*: the project fails to get much traction. 
People are starting to get frustrated and there is a deep gulf about the 
big expectations and the poor results.
4. The *decline phase*: the group accuses the public at large of being 
lazy, the media of being non collaborative and biased, the governments 
of mobbing and the other groups of sabotaging. There is little or no 
self-criticism about why the project is not going as planned. People 
start to leave the project.
5. The *death phase*: the once big group is now very much slimmed down 
and only a few people survive. There are no major addition and changes 
to the project in months. The project is not being discussed upon in the 
media anymore. In a few months or years, the project is definitely 
shelved for good.

I have also been involved with the Partito Liquido and the Italian 
Pirate Party.
They both failed to get significant traction
