The two papers I cite:
http://www.matchism.org/refs/Gerber_2012_SecretBallot.pdf
http://www.matchism.org/refs/Gerber_2013_DiscussVoterChoice.pdf

The page where they're cited (and, looking at it again, I see that
that page needs to be broken up some more):
http://www.matchism.org/system/

There are lots of other references on that page that I think everyone
interested in direct democracy should be familiar with. As for the
design of the Matchsys itself, I also see that my thinking has moved
more toward quality proposals prepared by experts and
multiple-response voting (you pick one or more options rather than a
simple up/down vote) and away from the need for voter participation in
proposal production and moderators to try to keep that process from
breaking down. It'll get updated, but this is my busy season work-wise
so it probably won't be for a couple more months.
