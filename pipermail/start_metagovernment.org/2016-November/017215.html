Note that copyright is not at all the same as a trademark or a patent:
Copyright only protects the particular order of the words you send them and
offers nothing in the way of protection of the ideas they express or even
the names you give them. Then again, IMHO if you're concerned about
assuring that you get credit or the ability to monetize your ideas you
probably shouldn't be working in this field anyway...
