I had the chance to connect with Ed Pastore over email recently, and am
excited to learn about your project.  I'm involved in a similar effort
called DemocracyLab, which you could learn about by visiting
www.democracylab.org <http://www.democracylab.org/> .  
 
I think our efforts have many similarities.  One notable difference is that
DemocracyLab is implementing a data structure that presupposes that
political thinking can be broken into distinct Concepts: Values that people
hold; Positions taken when applying Values to Issues; and Policies
advocating the implementation of Positions.  These Concepts can be supported
or opposed by Evidence and Opinions.
 
We are in the early stages of software development, but are working
diligently towards an initial launch in October of 2008.  Our code is open
source.  To learn about our development efforts, or view instructions to
access the source code, please visit
https://clearspace.democracylab.org/community/website.
 
Best of luck in your work.  I'm looking forward to future collaborative
opportunities!
