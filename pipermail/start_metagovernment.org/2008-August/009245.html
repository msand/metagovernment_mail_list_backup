On Aug 13, 2008, at 6:21 PM, Manuel Barkhau wrote:

> For the moment I have no intention of implementing any
> _interpretation_ of the scoring that will be available in the system.
> Probably I will leave it at a very rudimentary version, to demonstrate
> the api and make it easier for people to experiment. All I want to do
> for the moment however, is _record_ the scores. Various scoring
> algorithms, will interpret the scores differently, so I think this
> neutral approach is appropriate for the time being.

That makes a lot of sense. Considering you're working on something  
like version 0.1, I suppose I shouldn't be looking for features that  
could be used later, as we get some real-world feedback.

> Staying consistent with my use of tags, I would introduce a synthesis
> tag. You can as any other tag, give it a score. The tag references the
> resolution it is synthesizing, so the score you give to that tag
> should represent how much in your opinion, this resolution synthesizes
> that resolution.

Beautiful. Even if the scores aren't interpreted at all, people will  
still have something to point them from one resolution to another.
