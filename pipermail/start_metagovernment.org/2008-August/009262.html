In less than 1 day, 3 hours, and 54 minutes (from the time I started writing
this) Digg will present the top questions to Speaker of the House Nancy
Pelosi at the Democratic National Conventions. Everyone is free to register
and post their own questions which will in turn be discussed and dugg by the
community.

Sound familiar?

What I find most fascinating is that the concept of interacting with our
Representatives through a Digg-like interface will be presented to millions
of people watching the Conventions -- as well as everyone hit by the media
fallout on such a novel idea.

In fact, I posted my own comment saying how much I appreciated this and
asked her if she would consider an open source version for our House of
Representatives!

http://digg.com/dialogg/Nancy_Pelosi_1
