I touched upon this briefly with the Obama/Palin post - if you have an
ontology which would allow you to categorise the values that lead you
to believing that Bush is in either category, then those same values
would tend to have follow-through consequences on other leaders too
which one might not think that they agree with - forcing to individual
to re-evaluate their contradictory opinions or to fix the 'broken'
categories in the system. Either way is a plus.

Even if you and your aunt disagree on some big stuff, and according to
your view of the world, she holds some massive contradictions which
she conveniently argues away _outside of the system_, you can still
agree on some of the smaller stuff. You weren't ever going to convince
her that Elvis isn't living in a moon-hotel anyway.
