> Instead the tree discussion structure, a la livejournal, instead helps

(Stepping back in the conversation a bit...) Pietro, I have seen you  
mention e-mailing a few times, and I want to clarify what you mean.

If you mean what I suspect, then I see a couple of large logistical  
problems. If I get an e-mail every time someone responds to me... that  
works great for a condo board, but sounds horrible for a global  
discussion of nuclear disarmament. The quantity of e-mail would flood  
users' mailboxes and quickly overwhelm the server.

Worse, if one can reply to a bunch of people at once and have them all  
receive e-mails as a result... this sounds like spammer heaven. The  
one thing I can think of to correct for that would be to require a  
certain level of karma (not sure I like that word) before one can  
trigger an e-mail.
