The last bit about the movement resonating deeply with our culture is true
in many more ways than we've begun to imagine.

Reading about artificial intelligence above brought to mind all the fear we
have surrounding robots taking over the world, while the idea of an Open
Intelligence sounds more like a Higher Intelligence.

The same goes with an Open Economy, Open Education, Open Religion -- really,
the Source of anything that produces knowledge can be Opened for the good of
all mankind.
