On Fri, Sep 5, 2008 at 1:53 PM, Mark Frischmuth <mark at democracylab.org> wrote:
> Great discussion!  As an FYI, at DemocracyLab, we've taken a different
> approach to this issue.  We've divided comments into two categories,
> Evidence and Opinion.  Each could link to one or more Concepts (Values,
> Positions, Policies in our model, or to other instances of Evidence or
> Opinion).  Users can rate the link between a piece of Evidence/Opinion and a
> Concept on two scales - Support/Opposition and Relevance.  The ratings
> determine the positioning of the Evidence/Opinion in the User Interface.

At DemocracyLab, do you ever have circumstances where something
doesn't quite fit into a single category or concept? Or where new
information requires it to be moved into another category (e.g. moving
from opinion to evidence)?
