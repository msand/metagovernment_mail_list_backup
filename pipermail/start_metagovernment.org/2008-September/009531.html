On Sep 30, 2008, at 4:16 AM, Manuel Barkhau wrote:

> Of course if it should be considered law, it has to follow a
> certain format, but otherwise there are no limitations.

I don't think that's true, as the nature of a "law" varies depending  
on the community. The format of a law for a city differs from the  
format of a regulation for a swim meet or a procedure for a swim team.  
It seems better to allow the format to grow organically within any  
particular community, and let synthesis scoring ensure that better- 
formatted resolutions come to the fore.
