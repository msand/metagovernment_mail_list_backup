it should be findable in the list archives (yes, I recognize the irony  
in this statement).

further scatters the discussion. We already have two venues and adding  
a third breaks us up more.

What I think would work best would be to further transcribe early  
discussions into the wiki. That provides an archive of sorts but also  
gives it life: ideas don't have to be stuck in their original form but  
can grow over time. My only holdup in this has been that I have a lot  
of other obligations and haven't gotten around to it. Anyone can go  
about transcribing and I would strongly encourage any/all to do so.
