Thanks again for sharing this Ed.

For DemocracyLab's presentation in Oregon to politically minded college
students, we're starting research based on the following outline (we've got
a 90 minute time slot):

21st Century Civic Engagement

I. Role of the Citizen in Democracy
	A. Rights of Citizens
	B. Responsibilities of Citizens

II. Evolving Role of Citizens Throughout History of Democracy
	A. Ancient Greece
	B. American Revolution
	C. French Revolution
	D. Industrial Revolution
	E. Information Revolution

III. Evolving Role of the Internet in Politics
	A. 2000 Election
	B. 2002 Election
	C. 2004 Election
	D. 2006 Election
	E. 2008 Election

IV. The Nature of Web 2.0
	A. Participation
	B. Collaboration
	C. Transparency

V. Web 2.0 and Politics
	A. Partisan Political Purposes
	B. Emerging Political Tools

VI. DemocracyLab
	A. Mission, Vision, Values
	B. Organization and Infrastructure
	C. Methodology
	D. Input => Output => Outcomes

Once we have more meat on these bones, I'll be happy to share it with the
group.

Also, I've created an outline for DemocracyLab's prototype application,
which you could view here:
https://clearspace.democracylab.org/docs/DOC-1118.  If the group thought it
desirable, I'd be happy to post this to the Metagovernment site as a
Prototype B (or C, as appropriate).

Best regards,
Mark
