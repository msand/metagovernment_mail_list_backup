While searching for the word MetaVote I stumbled across an image on Google
that seems to represent our discussions to date:

http://dearinter.net/static/images/dearMeter.jpg

I can also imagine a cool user control that would allow them to click the
positive or negative -- depending on their vote.

When I checked out the website that owned the image I found an excellent
User Interface for us to consider:

http://dearinter.net/

Their homepage is extremely basic -- but a great place to start the
development process. The navigation is just as basic, but again, the
simplicity makes it an easier starting point than the alternatives.

The page that really sold me was their Answer-O-Matic, which seems like a
simple way to step through the questions that are of interest to a specific
user:

http://dearinter.net/answeromatic/new
