Would *MetaGovernment* be the platform on which an *Open Democracy* be
built?

By that definition, could there also be other types of *Open Government*,
such as *Open Socialism*, *Open Communism*, and my FAVORITE *Open Fascism*?

"As an Open Fascist I believe that only those States that employ Open
Policies be allowed equal representation."

Furthermore, I see *Open Governance* being relevant to describe smaller
communities without such broad definitions as Democracy, Socialism, etc.

This train of thought allows us to look at the possible naming conflicts
with *MetaGovernment* and come up with something creative to describe the
platform.
