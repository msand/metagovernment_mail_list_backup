I didn't specify explicitly, but I was looking for a definition as it
relates to new media, rather than a definition which goes back over
400 years. For example, Youtube reserves the option to regard a site
with advertising revenue as "commercial use":
http://help.youtube.com/support/youtube/bin/answer.py?answer=71011&topic=10551

The CC Draft guidelines also seem to lean that way - although they
adamantly refuse to get off the fence:
http://wiki.creativecommons.org/DiscussionDraftNonCommercial_Guidelines

Neither specifically answers the question, perhaps because as soon as
a definition is in place, it would be gamed.. but I think it is
apparent that common usage of "commercial use", as it relates to
digital content, is not as simple as determining whether the content
is being offered for resale.
