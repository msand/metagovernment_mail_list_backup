While the marketing guys may have coined the phrase 'open source' to avoid
the 'free beer' slogan, I believe the word 'Open' has come to mean much more
in the 21st century than simply being free to download and distribute.

Pietro correctly identified the Revolution that is currently taking place in
the news and information sectors. I consider this to be the Open Revolution
which believes that all knowledge -- including our laws and governance --
should be free to distribute and acquire.

If we are attempting to create a form of governance for the Open Revolution,
I believe that Open Government is as succinct as you can get.
