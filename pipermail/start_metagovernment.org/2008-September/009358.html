Christopher,
that is exactly what we need.

The person writes the tags.
Each tag has vertical slide.
The vertical slide is filled so that they follow a power law, and the
sum is equal to 1.
Users can then adjust the slides by rising them or lowering them.

In the perfect world, if they rise them beyond something on the left,
they would switch order.

Wonderful!
