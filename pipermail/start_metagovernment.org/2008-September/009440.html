We launched our wiki three months ago today (or tomorrow morning,  
depending on your time zone).
http://metagovernment.org/pipermail/start_metagovernment.org/2008-June/000393.html
Here are some stats you may find interesting:

- The counter at the bottom of the home page indicated that it has  
been accessed almost 9,000 times. It will probably top nine thousand  
later today.

- The wiki now has 42 registered accounts, and growing.
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Special:Listusers

- The wiki has 51 regular content pages (not including the Copyright  
page, About page, etc.)

- Our site gets at least 100 unique visitors each day; often more.

- Third-party sites are beginning to show our increased traffic:
http://siteanalytics.compete.com/metagovernment.org/
http://www.alexa.com/data/details/traffic_details/metagovernment.org
(Though August was a particularly high month for us because of a  
couple mentions in SlashDot posts plus being featured on  
StumbleUpon... don't be surprised if September shows a slight dip back  
down.)

- Some additional stats on the wiki are available here:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Special:Statistics

This is a great start! Especially considering that we don't even have  
software to show people yet. Still, I think this project could benefit  
from some additional help. I have been writing to selected people and  
organizations recently, trying to pique their interest. I would urge  
you to do the same. I will send a separate e-mail with some suggestions.

I can hardly imagine what the next three months will bring... :)
