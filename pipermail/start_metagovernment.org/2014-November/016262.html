Hello,

I went through the available language standards and drafts for EvoB.
I have found five relevant language names, but just two with 
documentation. For more see 
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/EvoB#Possible_languages

After reading all two documentations, UVML and EML, I think "Election 
Markup Language" is better.
I believe the requested dictionary is already present in the language 
and it is also based on XML, so it can be further extended. The language 
is actively maintained and used. The only characteristics where may be 
EML rivalled by UVML is human-readability.

What do you think ? Do you like EML ? Would you like to use EML ?

     Kind regards
         Michael
