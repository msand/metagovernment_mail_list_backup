Max,

I see that requirements document could make this concept more 
understandable and attractive. On the other hand I do not dare to write it.
E.g. if I take your requirement "requirement that everyone has access to 
all of the software and media and can largely do as they like with it." 
It can stop some projects that will provide anonymity. It may be a small 
server hosting 100 members. They hide the source data and publish just 
list of members and the sums of their opinions. (It may be number of 
sums according to different aggregation methods.) I do not see reason 
why to stop a project like that, do you ?

If you feel, that you know what to write into requirements document, 
please, create the document. I do not feel I know what to write there.
