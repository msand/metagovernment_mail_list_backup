It will be presented to the general assembly in about 2 weeks.
I'm not sure how friedrich thinks about mirror voting, but he
advocates the open data movement, so i think he would open up the data
in a way that might enable mirror voting,
at least for getting the data out of adhocracy into any other system.
I'm not so sure about the other way round, but if enought systems
participate, adhocracy might be forced to open up for data input.

By the way, adhocracy will only be a pilot project, and it's used
intensively and people findthat it does not scale well, they might
also be willing to change the system.
