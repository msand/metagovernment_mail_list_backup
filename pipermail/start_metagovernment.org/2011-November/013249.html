Aktiv Demokrati has a fairly advanced concept of a time rule. Very  
basically: the more people participating, the lower the value of t.  
This has a natural sense to it: if one person wants to change the law,  
they just have to wait infinity days for it to come into force. If  
millions of people want something, it can happen very quickly.

They describe it rather well on:
http://aktivdemokrati.se/manifesto/
Scroll down to the "More on continuous voting" section, near the  
bottom of the page.
