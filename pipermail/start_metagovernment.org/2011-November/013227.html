Personally, I am not fond of Facebook at all. I find it to be  
primarily a tool for a few corporate interests to collect and exploit  
enormous amounts of personal information from people. Ironically, for  
the for-profit interests I work with, I manage three different  
Facebook pages, two with moderately high memberships.

If others want us to have a presence on Facebook, I won't stand in the  
way. But I would ask permission to be made an administrator, at least  
so I can change the graphic to something better. And also so I can do  
some promotion for it: if it's going to exist, it might as well thrive.
