I thought about that a lot,
and eventually I decided it is a false problem.

You do not need to classify issues.
Each issue will have a time t that is decided at discussion time.
Then we just observe what categorizations emerge bottom up.
If you really want to categorize you should do it pragmatically.
Rules that make rules for other rules must have a bigger t than those rules.
But I would first experiment without this metarule. I think the easier it
is the better it is.
