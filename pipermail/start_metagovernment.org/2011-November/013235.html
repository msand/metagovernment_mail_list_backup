The "Facebook Page" is not for chatting with each other. We use the mailing
list to do so and we could use the facebook group.
The "Facebook Page" is just to make it simple for people who like the
purpose of metagovernment to spread the word viraly ;-)

It is impossible to put all the information of metagovernment.org into the
"Facebook Page", so people who are really really interested and would like
to contribute will contact us, they will visit metagovernment.org and will
join the mailinglist or facebook group.
The "Facebook Page" might be a starting point but i don't think it will
ever compete with metagovernment.org, rather it will complement it, don't
you think?

If we increase our public visibility, chances are that a lot more people
will recognize metagovernment and a fraction of those might contribute.
