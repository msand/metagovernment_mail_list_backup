Hello Alex and all,

We had started a non-exhaustive list of e-Voting Solutions on E2D
International and we were hoping to compare them by features, licensing,
etc. but there are many projects missing on this list and we never really
got around to finishing it:

http://e2d-international.org/wiki/index.php?title=IT/Website_taskforce#e-Voting_Solutions

I also immediately see some overlap between the list of Active Projects on
metagovernment.org but also ones that could be added to both lists:

http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Active_projects

Finally, we had recently been talking about creating an actual Wikipedia
article to compare all e-Voting Solutions, similar to how other
software/websites are listed in tables in Wikipedia.

For example, list of social networking websites:

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_social_networking_websites

I don't know if such an article has been attempted yet but Wikipedia might
be the best place to get everyone in the world to add their links in one
place.

Cheers,
