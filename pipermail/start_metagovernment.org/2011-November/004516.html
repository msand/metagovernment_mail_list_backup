Hola and welcome everyone!

For your information, I've just posted an entry regarding Partido de
Internet's election results on the main page of E2D International (FYI -
this would be one of the tasks involved in the administration of the main
website (WordPress)):

http://e2d-international.org/2011/11/23/partido-de-internet-pdi-runs-for-spanish-congress/

With regards to the Google Group Mailing List (
e2d-international at googlegroups.com ), there are two ways of joining 1) we
can send an invitation directly to emails to join (if you know people who
would be interested, let us know) or 2) outside members can send in a
request (which needs to be approved by an admin -- another task to the Tech
Task Force list<http://e2d-international.org/wiki/index.php?title=IT/Website_taskforce>
).

Finally, please be patient with us. This group is just starting out and we
are all volunteers with limited time to contribute (and are also involved
with our E2D parties to which we need to contribute if we are to make this
happen! ;). That being said, if you wish to help, please browse the
Forum<http://e2d-international.org/forum/>and
Wiki <http://e2d-international.org/wiki>, as pointed out by Sven, get
involved wherever you can, and let's make this happen!

Cheers,

-JF Frenette
jf.frenette at onlineparty.ca
