Dear Pietro and Paul,

There is also the method of full public disclosure.  We lay all the
cards on table, and let them speak for themselves.

For more on this, and the larger debate of private vs. public, see:
http://groups.google.com/group/contactsummit/browse_thread/thread/98088894ec1b39c1
The main topic there is actually a bid by the Dynamic Democracy
Initiative for a monopoly consortium, which they're calling "Upgrade
Democracy".  But I think the two topics are related (as I'll try to
explain there) because giving the users a free choice of voting media
is the only way to ensure overall trust of the system.

ContactCon is an interesting forum.  Like Occupy, it brings in new
voices.  Also of interest is this question about the US Pirates:
http://groups.google.com/group/contactsummit/browse_thread/thread/773a5f5a6d72871c
