Perhaps we can sidestep the issue of "who gets the money" by instead  
just using Metagovernment funds for meta purposes such as  
administration, promotion of collaborative governance, outreach, etc.  
Some of that administration could include helping member projects  
secure their own grants.

So, given that idea... where do we stand with the draft charter?
