Hi Jonas,

Thanks for the link.

Yes, it's the aspects you mention under Liquid Advisers that would apply
here:
-providing information
-linking to reliable sources
-arguing a position on an issue

But I don't envision a filter whereby each user only (or mainly) sees the
posts from the people they trust (like you see posts from your friends on
Facebook). Rather, I'm thinking of an aggregated scoring system which
allows the users who are most trusted by most people to gain the most
exposure.
