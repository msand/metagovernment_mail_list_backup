> Also, can I just write my own draft version and vote for it? I have

Yes, I think it would be good, if you would write your own draft version 
in the Metagov-Wiki, so we can compare our versions, maybe merge parts 
of it etc.. With Votorola you can not vote for your own draft. I guess 
we figure, that it is clear enough that you are in favour of your own 
version. Though I myself would also like to express that explicitly with 
a vote for myself, hm ... But you can ofc collect votes for your draft. 
If I like yours, I vote for it.
