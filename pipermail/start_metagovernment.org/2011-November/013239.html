Hi Alex, this opens another issue, which is the issue of resources.

There are decisions, but there are resources. Suppose I suggest that the
metagovernment group should prduce a website that does this, this and this.
But then I am not able or willing to code it. The other participants to
metagovernment might think it's a great idea, but they are also too busy.
In short although we all agree the website should be done, in practice it
remains on the paper. This is the reason why websites like kickstarted are
so good. Somehow a proposal that to be realised requires resources (even 1
hour time from one member) it should also include who will provide those
resources.

Cheers,
Pietro
