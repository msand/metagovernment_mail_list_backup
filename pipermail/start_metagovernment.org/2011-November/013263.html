On Sun, 06 Nov 2011 14:34, Ed Pastore wrote:
> On Nov 6, 2011, at 3:43 AM, Thomas von der Elbe wrote:
>
>> Conseo is working on a solution for that: To each vote the voter can 
>> connect the amount of resources he is willing to contribute. This 
>> could be Dollar, working hours, ... . So everybody can decide for 
>> himself where his money goes.
>
> But what if we have a formal organization which has received grant 
> money? Who gets the power to determine how that money is spent?

One possible way could be, that this grant money goes into the 
disposition of the people who have already pledged money/time themselves 
(maybe proportional to their pledged amount).

Another idea I get: to have a sort of trust network. One is only 
eligible to vote, if he is trusted by a minimum number of (themselves) 
trusted members. Maybe the Votorola trust-network could be used for that.

Somehow I'd like to avoid rewarding people for spamming our list/wiki, 
which would be the case, if eligibility is bound to the number of posts etc.
