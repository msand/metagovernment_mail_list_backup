I've just came home from a 4 hour meeting session with the occupy
frankfurt working group for liquid democracy.
They've decided to go with "ADHOCRACY" for usability reasons. "Liquid
Feedback" that is used by the pirate party ranked second and
bettermeans third place.

We set up an adhocracy server on occupy frankfurt servers and the next
meeting will discuss how voter registration will take place.
They sympathize with hashing peoples real names, after they showed
some documents that state their identity, but to not save their real
identity.
They also want to print QR-Coded that can be used at assambleas to
vote on issues. A webcam should register the QR-Code and count the
vote.

Some representatives from occupy munster and occupy hamburg might join
the next working group meeting and hopefully they will also host
adhocracy.
I'm in contact with a person from spain and he told me they run a
system, called N-1 that is already beeing used by 7 different
assambleas, and he would like to join the cause.
Therefore, a method to synchronize adhocracy with N-1
(https://n-1.cc/pg/groups/586698/iccvpb-help/) by vote mirroring might
be very welcome.
