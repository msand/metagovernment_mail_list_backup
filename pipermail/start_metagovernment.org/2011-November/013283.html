@ALL
Occupy Frankfurt already explored "Liquid Feedback" but decided its to
big with too many features ...too complex to get people started.
They want to try adhocracy, because they feel it has an easy-to-use
intuitive user interface.
I think they liked the idea of votorola but found it to be a bit
overkill to start with. I told them that i think adhocracy really is
easy to use compared to other projects,
but it might run into problems if it really starts to scale, so they
haven't decided yet.
Another thing is, that they are not sure how to go about voter
registration. They want it to be anonym but they also want people to
register at a camp and they see the risk of people creating sock
puppets.
Next, they also need to integrated the GENERAL ASSEMBLEY into the
plattform. Maybe there is a way to generate personal QR codes using
openpgp or something and enable people to vote through cell phones and
approve by making a photo of the QR Code? ...or maybe use another
token? any idea how to integrate the general assembley?
many people who participate in the general assembley like their
privacy or anonymity and many also dont own a cellphone or computer...
We were also thinking about creating a polling booth tent....

They also would like to evaluate more tools and asked me for
powerpoints and videos that describe the tools that are already out
there and ready to use.
Everybody on this list, if you read this and are associated with a
tool that could be used and have videos, powerpoint slides or similar
material to give a short presentation, send it to me :-)
...or give me links ;)
