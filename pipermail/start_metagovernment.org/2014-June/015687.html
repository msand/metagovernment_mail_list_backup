Pietro asks:
> (Michael, can we consider proposal 3 as the "none of the above"
> proposal?  Is that ok for you?)

Myself, I think the proposal is invalid if it forces a particular
outcome.  We might not like *any* of the Vilfredo pages and instead
prefer to keep the home page as it is.  The vote must allow for that
possibility by including a "none of the above" option.

Otherwise anyone could immediately use the same trick in a separate
proposal to undo the decision by forcing a reversal, or worse.

Would it be a good idea to first post the exact question you want to
ask, and the options, before taking votes?  Then we could make sure
nobody's going to dispute the validity of the process.
