No.

Use a word other than "system".

I am going to be reductionist here.

And also a bit stupid.

Use the words "application programming interface" and "contract" and move
up to "module" and then "application" and then "system" and then to
"ecosystem".

Somewhere in there it becomes possible to alpha a custom decision
application easily.  Ideally the application writer does not even need nerd
credentials.

I am not much inclined to tell you what you should be doing.  Further if
you want to build a system then that is really great.  Thinking very
carefully about interfaces is also a great software engineering practice.

I do specify that at the moment this viewpoint is not ripe for coordinated
action.  Still, the long view is also great.
