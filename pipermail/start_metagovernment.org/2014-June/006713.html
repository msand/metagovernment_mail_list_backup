Ed Pastore said, on the 12th:
> The decision rule of the group is still that a decision must
> maintain a consensus for a week in order to become valid.  If [some
> other platform] is used to build a consensus among a subset, it
> still must achieve consensus here, in the full community.

Yes, I think that's correct.  Announce each sub-group proposal here to
the full community, together with any votes to be carried over from
the external platform.  Only then is it considered a proper Metagov
proposal.  At that point, full discussion begins.

Remember to register your proposal and reserve a number:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Proposals

Please raise any concerns or questions in this clarification thread.
I'll backlink to it from the discussion page for the decision rule:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Talk:Rules
