I propose adding Bentley's 'stifled' text to the home page, if only as
a temporary stopgap.  What I like about it (just speaking for myself)
is its sincerity.  It really comes through and resonates.  But I see
that others like it too, because it's been doing well in Vilfredo. *
So I ask the group, can we take it as a first step forward?

Bentley, I've added your vote already; I hope you won't decline.


   PROPOSAL 3
   ----------
   Atop the home page, add Bentley's 'stifled' text:

       OUR LIVES ARE STIFLED BY A BROKEN POLITICAL SYSTEM

       Our politicians spend more time bickering and trying to get
       re-elected instead of solving issues.  We can only periodically
       choose between the lesser of a few evils.

       We want to have the option of being directly involved.  We want
       to use technology to work on these issues together when it is
       convenient for us.  We want to explore new ways of having
       society work together.

       Metagovernment is group of people coming together to discuss
       and work on ideas and technologies to improve our dysfunctional
       governments and societies.

   Then leave the door open to further proposals, changes and content.

   Against: (nobody)
