You're right, I speak with too much confidence about the solution.
Although I *feel* confident, someone might easily find a fault in it.
What I'm *most* confident about (but not 100%) are the first steps:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Overplan/d/SoP

   CoP    (CoP) Compare the overplans
    |
   SoP    (SoP) Select the best overplan
    |
   ToP    (ToP) Track discussion of the overplan
    |
   XoP    (XoP) Execute the overplan
    .
    .

I admit that I could be wrong here.  But can anyone show that I
actually am?  Given that an overplan is "a comprehensive plan for the
future of humanity", can anyone see a reasonable way to proceed into
that future *other* than by following the steps above?

Or can anyone see how we might follow those steps faithfully, yet
*still* become lost or stuck, e.g. as in the past?
