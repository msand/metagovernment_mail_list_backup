This is a gem.  Who wrote this, please?  Where does it come from?

   OUR LIVES ARE STIFLED BY A BROKEN POLITICAL SYSTEM

   Our politicians spend more time bickering and trying to get
   re-elected instead of solving issues.  We can only periodically
   choose between the lesser of a few evils.

   We want to have the option of being directly involved.  We want to
   use technology to work on these issues together when it is
   convenient for us.  We want to explore new ways of having society
   work together.

   Metagovernment is group of people coming together to discuss and
   work on ideas and technologies to improve our dysfunctional
   governments and societies.
