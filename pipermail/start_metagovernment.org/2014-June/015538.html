Mike seems suddenly to be discussing layout on the list, but the focus 
in Vilfredo is on content. Each proposal must be a full answer to the 
question, so that it will be a full alternative to the other proposals. 
Otherwise the selection algorithms don't work right. So, to propose, you 
would sit down and write out all of the text that you would like to see 
on the home page, and that would be your proposal.
