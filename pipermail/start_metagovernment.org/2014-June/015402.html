Hi Bentley,
thank you very much for this message.

I have to admit I understand Jacopo's position when he says that 2650 is
too general. It is more like an introductions. So I am very happy for it to
be in the Pareto Front, so it will be an important ingredient in the next
generation. But not the sole winner.

Similarly what I miss in 2649 a paragraph that clearly states that we are
working on different projects, and links to the list, dividing between
projects represented (someone from that project came here once), and "in
house" projects (someone is constantly here and we are following the
development quite close).

2651 has such a paragraph. So I don't think I will change my vote, instead
as soon as the voting phase is over I am planning to do a bit of paragraph
shopping, and mix up a few ideas.

I am quite happy that we do not reach a consensus at this generation, as I
feel no proposal is complete enough.

Pietro

P.S. it's the first time that a person discusses in public the ins and outs
of some proposals, speaking directly to the key players, very interesting
twist.
