Interesting turns on the list here.  I've been following with interest (aka
lurking). Chiming in quickly now.

I think this diagram may provide an interesting anchor for discussion:
http://democracylab.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/ProcessDiag_Behavior_0.png

It identifies the stages in the lifecycle of a problem.  The tools that are
needed at each stage are quite different.  It may be helpful for the
developers of the various tools being proposed for use to think about which
segments of the lifecycle they're best equipped to support, and that
together we consider a sequencing of usage that would allow us to
effectively make a decision and get to the "Act" part of the workflow.

Cheerfully,
Mark
