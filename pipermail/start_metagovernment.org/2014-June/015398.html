Hi Steve,
yes that's the basic idea.

Vilfredo goes a few steps forward from this. In particular it analyses how
people have voted, to suggest possible actions.

For example (I will only speak about what is going on, trying not to flood
you) right now it is probably asking to Jacopo and Bentley to change their
support for a specific proposal (a different one). This because they are
the only person missing that is preventing that proposal from dominating
another one (thus simplifying the situation).
And it is suggesting to some of us, to convince them. And to do this it is
telling us what is the critic they brought to that proposal.

All those actions are in the box just under the graph.

Said that I think that we should go at least to a second generation. So I
will not contact neither Jacopo nor Bentley on this... for now.

(future versions will permit the contact to happen inside the system, so it
might even be possible to work in a group of anonymous users)
