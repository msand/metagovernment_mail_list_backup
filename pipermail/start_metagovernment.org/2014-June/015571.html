Hi Michael,
I am an expert. I have a degree in mathematics, and Voting theory is a
field of mathematics. I have taught at summer school. I collaborate with a
colleague of mine, who is a professor at  Dauphine University in Paris, and
I am preparing a paper on a voting system.

And I am telling you. Plurality voting is a terrible system. And for as
much as I can tell there is pretty much an agreement on this. There are
voting systems that are better, others that are worse. None is perfect. And
Plurality is considered among the worse, easiest to manipulate, whose
result represent less what the majority of people want.
