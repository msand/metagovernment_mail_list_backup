Hi Bentley,

Most of the mess is my fault, and should mostly clear up in the next 
generation. I had not fully appreciated what Pietro just told us (that 
the proposals need to be full alternatives to one another), so when I 
put in the old material I broke it into pieces. (My intent was that we 
provide Ed and other interested parties feedback on why various parts 
were not included in the final cut. I take that sort of feedback to be 
very important.) But now I see that I made a mess.

My feeling had been that we still need to look at and compare and select 
individual sentences (concepts) before tackling the assembly of the 
pages, but perhaps the nature of Vilfredo together with the hot 
"political" situation (that suggests we step right along) make that not 
a good strategy within a single question. Perhaps either we need to spin 
out a set of questions (as Pietro suggests), or simply have full-page 
proposals in the next generation of this question.
