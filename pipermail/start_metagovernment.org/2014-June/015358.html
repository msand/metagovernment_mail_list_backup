I apologize I have not had time to properly investigate Airesis, Jacopo. Can you link to the proposal you reference? I signed up, went to the Metagov group, but the only proposal I see is on another topic:
http://www.airesis.eu/groups/metagovernment/proposals
Sorry, I may not have time to participate beyond this for a few days, but hopefully a link will help.

Concerning which method we should use to make decisions, I think it is vital that we do not choose just one. Say we decide on Loomio for all our decisions. Then none of us ever look at Airesis or Vilfredo, etc. Plus we implicitly say as a group that all other systems are substandard. It would be much better for us to switch platforms to suit the need of the proposal. Hopefully eventually we will find that we are always using one system (and hopefully that system will be a hybrid of several currently-extant ones). In that case we’ll know we’ve found the truly best.

This was Mike’s idea for a plug-in framework:
http://metagovernment.org/wiki/Plug-in_framework
(allowing people to use whatever platform they please), but it did not get much traction. I would love to see us try it again.
