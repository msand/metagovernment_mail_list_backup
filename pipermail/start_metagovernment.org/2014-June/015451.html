Mike,

You bring up some interesting points about participation by people not in
the system being used. We do need to decide if to make a decision official
it also has to go to a vote in the email list or if the rule of the system
being used are sufficient. I'm equally torn. People should not be forced to
us a system they don't like but also using these system,s is a core of
MetaGovernment.

 I think everyone is open to other decisions being made in other systems or
the email list. The concern is making two very similar systems in two
different systems in a similar time period.

I disagree with your proposal specifically because the text is not
complete. Not because of the system being used. I voted for it in Vilfredo
because I was learning how it works. I'm a little disappointing that I can
only vote up or down on an item. I would refer a ranking. I imagine that
would make the math exponentially harder. I voted for the best several
proposals so they would move to the next round but I do not think any are
done. I have proposed a new version with text taken from other items and
some items removed. I would prefer to not see this old text on the home
page now even though it is better than a blank page. I would prefer to not
have the page change then change again so quickly. I don't think I will
agree to a proposal on the home page until I can drink up the other ideas
and build an informed opinion.

In my experience Home Pages are one of the most contentious issues in group
governance. If we can make it through this we will be in a good place.
