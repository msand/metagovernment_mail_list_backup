Hello folks,

I'm in the process of refactoring and re-branding the ranked choice straw poll application that I've been operating under the aegis of the Indaba Application Network. I value the conversations that I've been monitoring here on the Metagovernment list, and I presume some of you might be interested in what I'm doing now. 

My goal is to use the analytic portion of the system for straight up political polling in places like Iowa and New Hampshire (where the US presidential primary season begins) and to pursue other revenue-building ideas. All of this aims at helping fund creation of a full-fledged liquid democracy platform that includes a fairly robust proxy-voting mechanism. That part's not available for demonstration yet, but I'm stepping up my efforts to explain the ranked choice tabulation portion, while simultaneously working on revamping the data schema and creating a new API.

I've created a video channel called Sygnol to introduce aspects of the project. The first video is at

 https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QQp4myhEx-A

I'd be happy to hear your reactions.

cheers,

Craig Simon
