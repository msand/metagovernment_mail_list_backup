Pietro  said:
> seriously you claim plurality is the best voting system???

In a continuous straw-poll for an Anglo-American/French single-winner
election, yes.  Not in Italy, where the elections are multi-winner.

The crucial word is "continuous".  Complicated voting methods are
generally designed to overcome problems associated with *one-shot*
polling for decision purposes.  But straw polls aren't for decision
purposes, and can run continuously.  Then plurality is ideal.
