I will play bad labelitis here.

I start out wondering what the title will be for a winning proposal on the
home page.  If I knew that I could call in various mental labels and then
evaluate the proposal against the mental label.

The design pattern concept comes from that internal dialog.

Let me illustrate:

Mission to me means something different than purpose, preamble,
declaration, manifesto.

One of the things a mission means to me is it is really  going to happen.
We add vision and plan and call that the MBA design pattern. If you visit
with an Angel they are not particularly interested in the detail but that
you were able to get it written.

So what is to be the label?
