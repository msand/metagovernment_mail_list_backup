I very much support trying Airesis after Vilfredo (and me taking a vacation
from all those explanations ;-) ). I would suggest trying it onto another
page (unless we cannot find a satisfying result for the Homepage with
Vilfredo).

This because we should not send to Airesis pre-baked answers. Airesis
should be tested fully on an independent and different question. And
participants using Vilfredo should not feel that their work is less
relevant (people who think their vote is not relevant tend to vote
randomly, and the the consensus is harder to reach).

So, with this postilla, +1.

Cheers,
