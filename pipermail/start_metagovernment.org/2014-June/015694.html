I'm not buying it: The single largest reason we need something like
LEDDAs (or some other form of major economic reform) is *because* of
the inequality that inheritance causes.  Eliminate inheritance and
we've just saved ourselves a whole lot of trouble implementing
workarounds for the damage it causes.  I'm also quite sure that it
would also be a hell of a lot easier to get this implemented than
Piketty's "wealth tax" or LEDDAs.  All we need is a mythology (or
propaganda!) to convince the masses to take the wool off of their eyes
and see this.  I think we have some expertise in this area on this
list, perhaps one of them will come up with the magic incantation...
