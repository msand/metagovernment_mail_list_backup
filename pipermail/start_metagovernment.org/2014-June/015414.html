Thanks (yes Derek has a great merit in translating my ideas into code - and
asking me not to do it :-D ). Probably the last time you participated was
even before the 3 ways voting, and surely the comments (which will change)
after a chat with Robert Bjarnson. Also at the time there was no recursive
voting. You voted, but could only see at the end of the voting phase what
the results was. This had the unintended effect that people never really
understood the graph because too much time passed from when they voted and
when they saw the results. Now seeing it immediately, and being able to see
the changes right away, was a big step.

Still I feel the participants need to be walked through it. Not only the
system is not intuitive, but there are also no good tutorial. This is why I
am keeping a strict discipline with myself in not explaining too much all
at once :-)
