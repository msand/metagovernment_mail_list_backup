Sorry I have little time to reply. I think you may have misinterpreted my meaning. 

We *should* use Loomio. And every other platform (including using multiple platforms at once). 

But as we move from platform, we need a consistent way for the casual observer to follow where the latest proposals are. Especially if we are in more than one platform at any time. 

The email/wiki solution is just for the bare minimum of keeping everyone in the loop regardless of what is going on elsewhere.
