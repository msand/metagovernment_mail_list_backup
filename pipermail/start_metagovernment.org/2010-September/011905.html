I'd just like to pick up on a point from this discussion.

Richard asked:
Could it [anonymous participation] apply to voting, at all?

And Ed replied:
It could in a mixed discussion and voting system. Though prominent systems
in this project seem to be leaning away from that model.

What do you mean by a mixed discussion and voting system, Ed? Surely the
voting systems in this project will also allow for discussion? Or will it be
the case that an open governance system will have to mix and match a voting
tool, a proposal writing/editing tool and a discussion forum in order to
have the complete package?

I think it's paramount that these elements are as closedly integrated as
possible so that users engage in the whole process rather than just voting.
