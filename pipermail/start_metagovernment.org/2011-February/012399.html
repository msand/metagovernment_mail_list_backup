The group can decide on what whey *want* to do. They can make
prioritized lists of suggestions. This list may be continually updated.

Each member will have a clear view of what is needed. Any parson making
a decision to do voluntary work may take that prioritized list in to
consideration.

That is a bit like how a bug tracker works.
