I'd love to see this kind of logic applied to: 

a) the identification of the resources (inputs and processes) required to
achieve objectives (documented on the Web in an open, machine-readable
format, like StratML) and 

b) the actual commitment of those resources by those subscribing to
("voting" on) each objective (by allocating their own resources).

It's one thing to agree upon the wording of the objective but quite another
to understand what is required to achieve it, much less to allocate the
necessary resources to do so.  (It's much easier to expect others to assume
the burden than to accept responsibility ourselves.)
