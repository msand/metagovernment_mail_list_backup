On Fri, Feb 11, 2011 at 9:57 AM, Mark Frischmuth <mark at democracylab.org> wrote:
> I think this is one of the most important questions this group could ever
> hope to answer.  It's well within the realm of what we know how to do to
> inform the decision making of existing power structures, but how can groups
> take binding decisions from asynchronous online participation?

By recording all votes, publish a deadline as a strategy to make and
pare down a decision (assuming a constraint), and then record the
result, giving an implementation period whereafter any dissent can be
revisited.  Repeat as necessary.  Bonus: record results and commentary
to avoid re-occurance of error.
