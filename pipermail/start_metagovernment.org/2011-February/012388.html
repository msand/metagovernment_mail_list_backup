I think this is one of the most important questions this group could ever
hope to answer.  It's well within the realm of what we know how to do to
inform the decision making of existing power structures, but how can groups
take binding decisions from asynchronous online participation?  I think
there first would have to be a decision using the existing decision making
structures of the group to cede authority (probably first in a test case on
a specific issue) to a well defined online process.  But what online
process?  There are many possible decision models, many of which could be
appropriate in different decision contexts.

I have to run out the door to a meeting now, but will try to contribute some
thinking on this subject soon, and look forward to learning from others on
this issue.

Cheers,
Mark

> really big limitation. But what other options are there?
