David,

short answer (I'm currently on one of my customer sites):

I have no problem at all with delegation provided a) it's ad hoc and b)
conscious.

For example, ask me to pronounce on the merits of a proposed new anti
cancer treatment, and I'll happily defer to an appropriately experienced
medic with a proven track record in the field. But a) I'm only delegating
to that specified individual for that specified question and b) I
understand and accept the consequences which may flow from that
delegation.

I'll probably expand on that later...
