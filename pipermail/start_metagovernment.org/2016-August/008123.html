Hi,



I was thinking (no, really!) there are certain things that motivate authoritarians and psychopaths to choose some positions in society. Some preferences are given by structure (like to be on top) but some are not (and I would like to find these). I do not mean it as a solution, I mean it as preparation, removing some problematic feedback loops, that are blocking solution.



My first identified target are titles. Military titles are mostly working the way we want (except highest ranks maybe), motivating psychopaths to the positions where they are functional.

In medicine it seems to be a double-edged sword, works for surgeons, not much for drug prescriptions.

In law, politics, religion and education, it seems to be dysfunctional.



What do you think? Are titles strong motivation for psychopaths? authoritarians?

Is it an easy thing to remove titles from education, if properly explained? It may be replaced by RG score or some other number.
