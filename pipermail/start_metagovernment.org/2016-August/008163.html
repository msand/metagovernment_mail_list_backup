Hi

I am part of team that build new deliberative web-app (delib.org).
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p6JoKmMJi_c

 I have looked at previous projects, and believe that there should be
several factors that should help a team to have a successful project:


   1. User Experience: most of the projects, lacked in this area. A team
   should understand what his users needs, and solve the with great UX.
   Without UX, users will have difficulties using our system.
   2. Good understanding of deliberative theory. Deliberation is a very
   complex field. Having good understanding in the filed, may help the team
   understand what they should develop.
   3. Money: developing a software is a long run effort. Most of us
   couldn't run so long, without monetary support.
   4. A good way to work together and develop together. Most of the
   projects I had met, where not so cooperative in their developing strategy.

Tal Yaron,
