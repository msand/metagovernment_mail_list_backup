Hi Mchal
I wouldn't go for "good decision index", because nobody can know if
decision is good. You may make a decision that will earn you 1 million
dolars, but after that, you'll lose all your friends. was the first
decision was a good decision?

There is well knowen index for deliberation: deliberation qualety index
(DQI): http://www.delib-democracy.org/wiki/index.php?title=DQI
But this index is about the process, not the decision.

I would suggest two main ways to try to evalute a decsion:
1) that it answerd most of the needs of the group members, with minimal
burden (high ROI).
2) that it passed John Rawl's Veil of ignorance
<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Veil_of_ignorance> (a just decision).
