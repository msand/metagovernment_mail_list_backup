> Hi Michal, speaking for myself, I also am fully occupied in working on 
> my own project (though I am monitoring all that you are presenting, 
> and appreciate it).
>
> The other thing is, I do mind being outvoted. Casting, collecting and 
> counting votes can be very useful in guiding the iterative, recursive 
> process of thesis-antithesis-synthesis that can lead to "collective 
> best" proposals and decisions. (Vilfredo is an example of a decision 
> system that uses voting in that way.) But any decision system that 
> features "outvoting" as the primary basis for decision making is in my 
> view a bad system.
>
> "Outvoting" decision systems have been producing bad results for 
> thousands of years. We can do better.
