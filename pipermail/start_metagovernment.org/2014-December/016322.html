> Please everyone, i challenge you to go to:
> http://github.co and create a free account.
> It's the largest open source collaboration platform of the world.
> Then use your github account to sign up with:
> http://waffle.io and http://gitter.im
> ...which both are free and do not need to register another account.
>
> Afterwards, go to github and create your own ORGANISATION (again free of
> charge)
> and create your first PROJECT under that organisation (again free of
> charge).
>
> You can then INVITE members/friends/like-minded into the project.
>
> lets assume your personal account will be
> *http://github.com/myNickName <http://github.com/myNickName>*
> and your organisation will be
> *http://github.com/myOrganisation <http://github.com/myOrganisation>*
> then you might create a project in your organisation
> *http://github.om/myOrganisation/myDemocracyProject
> <http://github.om/myOrganisation/myDemocracyProject>*
> which will give you for free:
> *http://waffle.io/myOrganisation/myDemocracyProject
> <http://waffle.io/myOrganisation/myDemocracyProject>* (for project
> management)
> and
> *http://gitter.im/myOrganisation/myDemocracyProject
> <http://gitter.im/myOrganisation/myDemocracyProject> *(for a project chat)
>
> In that CHAT, you can set up automatic notifications about project
> progress of your github project AND other projects that you like, for
> example updates and activities from:
>
> *https://github.com/fairdemocracy/vilfredo-client
> <https://github.com/fairdemocracy/vilfredo-client> *(which could be a
> project that you are interested in
>
>
> and BECAUSE, all the CHATROOMS for your and all the projects of your
> friends will be listed on the sidebar of your GITTER CHAT, you can easily
> jump back and forth and we talk more structured about the potential for
> collaboration.
>
>
> *PLEAS FOLKS, I REALLY CHALLENGE YOU TO MAKE THAT STEP :-)*
> (even though its just for trying it out and let yourself be inspired by
> the collaboration mechanisms of github)
