> after a longer pause I am still working on EvoB.

> Today I was thinking about Active and Related projects as a way how to 
> bridge the gap between publishing (e.g. newspapers) and social choice 
> (e.g. elections).
> There are some standards in these fields, where we might want to start 
> our search for common speech (some markup language -probably, -for now).
> In social choice is it Election Markup Language [that wants to be 
> extended e.g. in schema 350b by some communication (publishing) language].
> In publishing we also have standards (RSS, Atom).
>
> My proposal is: Let's use EML where EML is explicitly defined. And 
> Atom for publishing and (public) generic communication.
> I hope this will cover our field sufficiently and that we will be able 
> to build our standard on these languages.
>
> EML here is 
> http://docs.oasis-open.org/election/eml/v7.0/cs01/eml-v7.0-cs01.html
> Atom here is http://www.w3.org/2005/Atom
>
> I am eagerly waiting for your comments.
> What do you say? yes ?
