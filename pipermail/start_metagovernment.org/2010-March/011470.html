A Gentleman's Agreement is entirely informal, and it seems you are  
talking about something that would introduce at least a bit of  
formality to the relationship between Metagov and the Active Projects.

For reference, this is the part of Matías' original proposal we are  
referring to:
> "An umbrella organization is an association", then we should have a
> contract with active projects, specifying roles and obligations, this
> way we know when the association starts and ends.

A License Agreement might be the most appropriate.
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/License
