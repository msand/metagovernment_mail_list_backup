I am acknowledging Metagov by including its plan(s) in the StratML
collection at http://xml.gov/stratml/drybridge/index.htm#Other 

W/re standardization, it would be good to document in StratML format the
goals and objectives of: 

	a) each of the projects affiliated with Metagov, and 
	b) the standardization project.  

Hopefully, doing so would help us "get our heads around" them.

If anyone is inclined to do so, they could use Joe Carmel's XForms form to
document their plans, in open, standard (ANSI/AIIM 21:2009) format:
http://xmldatasets.net/XF2/stratmlxform3.xml  Alternatively, the PDF
Fillable form could be used:  http://xml.gov/stratml/forms/StrategicPlan.pdf


I will be happy to include any such plans in the StratML collection:
http://xml.gov/stratml/drybridge/index.htm
