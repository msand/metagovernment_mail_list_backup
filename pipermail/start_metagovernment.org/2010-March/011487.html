W/re voting systems standards, perhaps these references may be of interest:

2002 Voting System Standards developed by the U.S. Federal Election
Commission:
http://www.eac.gov/voting%20systems/voluntary-voting-guidelines/2002-voting-
system-standards 

FAQ at http://www.uselections.com/voting/faq.htm 

Professor Jones' recommendations:
http://www.cs.uiowa.edu/~jones/voting/fec3.html 

OASIS Election and Voter Services TC:
http://www.oasis-open.org/committees/tc_home.php?wg_abbrev=election 

Election Markup Language (EML):
http://docs.oasis-open.org/election/eml/v6.0/cd01/EML-Specification-v6.0.pdf


BTW, the Election Assistance Commission's open gov page is at
http://opengov.eac.gov/
