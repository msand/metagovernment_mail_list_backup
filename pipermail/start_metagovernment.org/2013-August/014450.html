discussing money is a waste of time. you should rather discuss something
which might become actionable. there are lots of designs for different
"money systems" and people waste their time to discuss them over and over
again, sometimes they even create regional money systems (there are quite a
few in germany) and they all have their own cult of followers, but
regarding the overall picture, they dont make a difference, because they
are not a mainstream thing. when it comes to money, the 'network effect' is
so strong, that is a total waste of time to try to attack EUR or USD or the
like in a direct manner.

But money is in some ways tied to equity or lets better say, the core ideas
behind money and equity are similar and so are the rules and if you change
one of them, its possible to change the other one too. i believe its way
easier to tackle the equity issue which is also related to ownership and
"the right to vote" then to tackle the money issue.

...this is everything i have to say when it comes to "money"

just my 2 cents
