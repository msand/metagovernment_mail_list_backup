Here are a few thoughts on rules that provide clarity and room for
interaction. I suggest limiting a statement to be one concept without
support. Supporting points and explanations would be in their own
statements. Each statement could be it's own thread. That is how I'm
framing it for my experiments. I currently have an arbitrary limit of 400
characters. You could also show a warning if the use the word "and" or
similar conjunctions.

What other formalizations increase clarity?
