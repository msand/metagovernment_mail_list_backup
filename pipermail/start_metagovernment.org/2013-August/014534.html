>         I would prefer a system that has no voting at all. I have a
>         prototype of a way to decide if a proposal should succeed that
>         does not use voting. Instead of voting people add reasons for
>         or against passage of the proposal. Each statement for or
>         against can also be affected by statements for or against it
>         in a hierarchy. An objection could be made against the
>         proposal but then other people could object to the objection,
>         for example, by adding a statement that the objection is a
>         logical fallacy. The system weighs all the pros and cons and
>         displays the confidence in the proposal (and all the
>         objections). I feel this is an example of a way to handle
>         objections without voting.
