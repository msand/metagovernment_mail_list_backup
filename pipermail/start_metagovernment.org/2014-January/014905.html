@transitive delegation
If a proxy delegates further, then the original voter will receive more
"updates", because everytime is direct proxy does something, it affects the
usage of his vote, and if the second order proxy does something, he also
decides what happens with the vote of the original voter.
... so voters build their "newspaper", by receiving updates, and if he
feels that he receives a lot of crappy "NEWS", because so much is going on
with his vote and maybe even stuff that he doesnt like, every "NEWS ITEM"
will include a button to UN-DELEGATE his vote.
...thats motivation for "voters" to always review whats going on and how
his proxies really act.
... that will be motivation for proxies to think at least twice, what they
will do with their received votes, because they dont want to loose votes
which were delegated to them
