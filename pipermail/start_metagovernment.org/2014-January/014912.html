Hi Mike,

> Interesting... so qKonsens allows an individual to formally express
> agreement (vote) for a text?  I can't get a clean translation of:
> http://wiki.piratenpartei.de/Datei:Definition-qKonsens-2013-12-DRAFT.pdf

YES! This is what qKonsens is about: individuals can vote for whole 
documents by rate and argue on their paragraphs. The documents are called 
*Konsenskiste* and the paragraphs are called *Kernaussagen* (that means 
essential sentences). Normally a *Konsenskiste* (document) consists of one 
to many *Kernaussagen* (paragraphs).

> It seems to describe micro-voting on arguments or discussions that are
> related to a text, not macro-voting on the whole text itself...  but
> then it's hard to tell from the translation.

As I said, the methodology allows for subdividing documents into multiple 
paragraphs to rate and argue on each of them independently. This is by 
design, because it seems to be the case, that one just agrees with *parts* 
of a document and not with the whole.

BUT I think it is possible to enhance the qKonsens also to be able to rate 
and argue on the document level!
