RRO trivially allows agreement with a text such that the content becomes
the position of the assembly.  The distinction is often to be made in a
committee report to the assembly.  But to receive  a document is to simply
acknowledge it's receipt.  To accept is to adopt it in whole.

The world does not come from a collection of independent quarks and muons.
More like a soup.
