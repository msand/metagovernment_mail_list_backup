I recall Ed wants to create a nonprofit.  There is much interesting
discussion about how to decide anything.  Something i take away from the
discussion is that it depends on what is to be decided.

Jim White's letter suggest to me several concrete decisions to be made
around Ed's nonprofit.

Here is one:

The nonprofit will make a significant effort to influence legislation.

I propose then a basis: the decision is to be legal per the IRS.  We label
this L.

So somehow we need to decide if this is a valid basis.  We have no charter
or members.

I say yes on the L basis.

The basis is therefore approved.  Per me.  The only participant.

What happens next?
