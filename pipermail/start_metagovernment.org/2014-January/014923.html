I should point out that there is a separate wiki page on decisions (though focused solely on public matter decisions):
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Decision

Gustavo, one point made on that page is that a community needs (or typically has always had) a charter: some document which lays out in advance what decision rule will be used. If the decision rule is to change from time to time, what decision rule is going to decide what decision rule to use? Isn't it a vicious circle?

(Note, what started off most of the threads on this list for the past couple of months is my attempt to get at a defining a charter for an American nonprofit to serve this group.)
