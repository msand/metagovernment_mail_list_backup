I suggest that the purpose of these system try-outs is to further
Metagovernments stated main goal:

"The Metagovernment project supports internet tools to enable collaborative
governance."

It appears to me that there are a lot of proposed and suggested tools but
not much use of those tools to provide in-depth feedback to improve or
narrow down the tools. This may be the most effective use of volunteers at
this time and could provide a common tangible goal and value to the
community.

Bentley
