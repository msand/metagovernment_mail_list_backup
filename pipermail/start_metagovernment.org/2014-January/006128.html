Greetings All (but especially Ed),

Here is a new book: (by Melissa Schwartzberg):
Counting the Many: The Origins and Limits of Supermajority Rule
http://www.amazon.com/Counting-Many-Supermajority-Cambridge-Democracy/dp/0521124492

Here is a pdf of Chapter 5 from that book:
http://www.yale.edu/polisci/conferences/epistemic_democracy/mSchwartzberg.pdf

I am in the process of reading the Chapter 5, and it is so far 
excellent! (Which is not to say that I agree with her ...)

Long story short: as an expert in the field, she does not see 
super-majority rule as necessarily better than majority rule.

(But, I haven't finished even the Chapter 5 yet, so perhaps this is a 
mischaracterization of her overall view.)
