I expect there will be steps forward and backwards in this direction.  Of
course there exists the possibility that politicians will take part in
responding to what the public says but there will be so much public input,
that they will be able to selectively respond to questions, and they will
continue to mold their campaigns in whatever fashion they choose UNLESS
there is the rating system for other peoples' comments.  The important
points must rise to the top and the politicians must be accountable to
answer the questions the public views as important in sufficient detail to
satisfy the majority of the public's concerns.

The opportunities that this means of communication brings are great!  Of
itself, it doesn't necessarily stand to improve anything though; we need to
make sure that the system is not manipulated or its value will be negated.
