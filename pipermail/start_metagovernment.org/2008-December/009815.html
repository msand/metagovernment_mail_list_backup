Guys, Matias,

I am quite interested in all aspects of this initiative for open-source
government/governance, but I do not have exhorbitant amounts of time to
devout to reading scores of emails (start-list).  Although the archives are
useful, I think they are a little difficult to peruse - even if by subject -
due to the extensiveness of discussion.  I wonder if setting up a more
modernized, graphically appeasing organization of topics and the current
state-of-the-art in this endeavor would be more useful in getting this
project on its feet.

I was think perhaps if we could set out a basic outline of what's going on
with metagovernment and then posting either bullets of relevant items under
each item under discussion or putting together some sort of chart that shows
prioritizing or how we're allocating responsibilities under different
aspects of this ideal.  Or are we so caught up in the philosophical ideals
that we are not working towards any 'functional' goals??

Please advise so I can best assist on the user-interface aspect, etc.
