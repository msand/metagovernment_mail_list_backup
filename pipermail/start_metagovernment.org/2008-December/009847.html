I believe that user ratings accomplishes this without always focusing on
that one guy.

The best comments, regardless of who makes them, generally rise to the top,
unless there is some bias built into the system. That one guy in my team who
has little interest in the user experience is our project manager -- an
obvious bias, as he decides which tasks have a higher priority for the team.
The result? Whatever that one guy ignores generally goes unnoticed by the
rest of the group.
