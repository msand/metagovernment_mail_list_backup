>>  * Brief history of the project, including past governance decisions.
>
> Project was initiated in March 2007 as a static website. In April  
> 2008 a list server was initiated with eleven members. That list was  
> quickly opened to the public for subscription, and now has over 50  
> members. That list is the sole governance mechanism of the project,  
> until such time as we use our own software, Metascore, as our  
> governance mechanism. See:
> http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Startup
>
> Because this project is centered around developing a new form of  
> governance, we deliberately have no single leader and no formal  
> structure. Until our software is mature, we act an adhocracy. When  
> our software is at a better level of development, it will be the  
> formal and exclusive governance mechanism of the project.
