welcome paul, mr.schofield.  we would certainly appreciate your support and
participation.  hopefully we'll be able to direct your areas of expertise to
assist us better very soon.  Ed, in helping to get the new visitors
start-page going, are we going to be reworking this from the ground up
essentially?  I think the overall user/new-visitor experience would be
enhanced if we were to revise the look on the metagovernment.org url.

i would propose that we change the site in this way:
1) the first lines should say more than just the goal of the
metagovernment.org project; this SITE stands for more than that.
http://metagovernment.org has been developed to support the
metagovernment.org project and it is running on mediawiki software
(including reference to the license model is optional); The political
theory, software ideas, technical expertise, and interface support provided
freely by supporters collaborate through the wiki and listserv software (ie:
"mailman") (link to
http://metagovernment.org/mailman/listinfo/start_metagovernment.org,
optional) are what sets the pace for this project.  To get involved please
determine which of these categories matches your interests and skills and
begin following the discussions.  More information is also available on the
startup page.  .........and remove the how to help section at the bottom or;

2) place all the language from above into "how to help," and place a link
such as "new users click here" near the very top.

Until we explain what is really going on here with the site i think we will
be losing a lot of potential support.

-Chris C.
