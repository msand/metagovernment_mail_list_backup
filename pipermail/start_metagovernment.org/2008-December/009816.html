Perhaps the system we could use to organize the group for those who need to
get brought up-to-speed quickly on the status of metagovernment topics would
be blog-publishing.  Consider the following blog that is incredibly simple
to setup/arrange/administer - http://meta-g-beta.blogspot.com/

I really think we need something because although I'd like to contribute in
a meaningful way (be that through research, programming, QA, whatever), I
don't know where this group is heading regarding any of these aspects, and I
do not have time to devote to this 100%.  Certainly, we should retain all
the help we can get without limiting involvement to those with time to read
scores of emails from the start-list.

All in favor, say +,yay, (add comment to the blog), reply-all with a similar
response, create a facebook group, do something that shows action versus
idealism.

The future success of this group likely depends on your decision to DO
SOMETHING to advance the state-of-affairs rather than pontificating it.

Best,
Chris
