Hello,

I've just discovered this list and the work you're doing at
metagovernment.org.

First, let me introduce myself briefly: I'm Giovani Spagnolo
<http://www.linkedin.com/in/giovani> and I work as program director for
the Telematics Freedom Foundation
<http://www.telematicsfreedom.org/en/about-us>, a non-profit
organization based in Rome, Italy. Our general goal is the furthering of
democracy, including media and global democracy, through the promotion
of telematic solutions that enable the protection and extension of the
communication and participation rights of all people.

Within this mission, we promote several projects. Particularly
"Continuous Democracy
<http://www.telematicsfreedom.org/en/projects/continuous-democracy>",
"User-Verifiable Telematics
<http://www.telematicsfreedom.org/en/projects/user-verifiable-telematics>"
and "Rule2Gether
<http://www.telematicsfreedom.org/en/projects/rule2gether>" are projects
that have a lot in common with metagovernment.org (rule2gether is
already working in Italy <http://www.decidiamo.it/democracyforum> under
the name "decidiamo.it <http://www.decidiamo.it/>" -> "let's
decide.it"). We have a long feature roadmap
<http://www.telematicsfreedom.org/en/project/10/documentation> for
rule2gether, with many features tackling problems of direct democracy
implementation online.

I'm contacting you to question about any possible way of merging some
goals of our projects. Telematics Freedom is trying to build a
consortium to bid under the European Union 7th Framework Programme
<http://cordis.europa.eu/fp7/ict/> next year, and get a funding to move
forward with our projects. Maybe you could evaluate a way to participate
directly, or indirectly through the Software Freedom Conservancy which
seems you're already involved with.

Please let me know what do you think about our work; if (and how) we can
collaborate and if would you be interested somehow in joining us to
submit a proposal to EU FP7 next year (of course if you have interest we
can deepen conversation on goals, team skills and resources, required
budget and project outcomes).

Thank you very much for your consideration,
