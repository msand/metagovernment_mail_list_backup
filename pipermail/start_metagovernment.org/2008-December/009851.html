For proposing a change to the home-page, the way I have done such  
proposals in the past is to make a new page with the month in the  
title and ask people to edit it or approve it for the home page.  
Examples are at:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Main_Page/2008-08
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Main_Page/2008-10
This just makes it easier for people to see what you're saying and for  
people to make changes as they think appropriate.

So you could make a new page at:
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/Main_Page/2008-12
and ask the list to review it.

I definitely like the idea of having a link at the top inviting new  
users to go to the new users page.

Also, in addition to the links I provided below,
http://www.metagovernment.org/wiki/FAQ
is also a good start, though it is now somewhat out of date.
