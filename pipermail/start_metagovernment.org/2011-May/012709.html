> Here's how vote cycles work: the pair of diagrams below shows a cycle

> receive his own vote.

>        /     \   (W)         /     \   (0)
>      (H)     (D) /         (7)     (7) /      |
>       |       | /           |       | /       | vote flow

>          (F)                   (7)
