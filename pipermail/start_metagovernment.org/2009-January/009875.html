MixedInk has launched:

  http://mixedink.com/

They have a polished UI, but nothing much under the hood.  It's a bit
of a let down.  Here's where I fit them, in the bigger picture:

  http://zelea.com/project/votorola/compare.xht

They have a peer-to-peer drafting medium (recombinant text),
essentially the same as Votorola.  But there's no peer-to-peer voting,
to go with it!  It's just plain old scoring.  So there's no
encouragement to build consensus.

I think they're in for a surprise.  Won't the participants be forced
to spend all of their time in attacking competitive proposals -
assigning zero scores to every new proposal - rather than working
together?
