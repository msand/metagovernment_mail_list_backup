> Here, here. Now lets get down to brass tacks: Where is the science

> proposals? Even better: I struggle with the (potential) issue of

> As for the whining about the mailing list, I sense an intense irony,
> perhaps rising to the level of hypocrisy, in the positions of you two,
> and indeed that of all of the deliberation/consensus promoters. My
> claim has long been that these systems cannot work for defining public
> policy because of the "last man standing" problem, and that it doesn't
> matter whether the debate is held in person, via email, or in some
> fancy deliberation software system: Anyone willing to continue
> espousing their positions long enough eventually gets to define the
> policy, or at least water down the proposal sufficiently that it

> see that by even threatening to leave or withdraw participation that
> you are actually proving my point?
>
> Not that I have any problem with Disputation Arenas like this list for
