I very much like the idea of a simple URL, which makes participation  
so much easier. The shared workspace is great too.

The problems I see are:

- Limit of 16 users. Probably not a problem for us yet, but could be  
(we got close to that last time I think). And would be eventually.
http://etherpad.com/ep/about/pricing-free

- Chat space is small. With a lively discussion, it might be hard to  
follow since posts will fly off the top pretty quickly.

- Terms of service are... weird. When I click the TOS link, I end up  
on Google's TOS...?
http://etherpad.com/ep/about/tos

Can anyone find a way around the above? Hm... at least for my third  
objection (and maybe the first?) we could use PiratePad:
http://piratepad.net/fiuASvRqss
http://www.piratepad.net/
(same thing, but hosted by the Pirate Party.)
or another of these:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Etherpad#Clones
