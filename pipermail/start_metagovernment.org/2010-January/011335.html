I am, or more specifically, was involved in the beginnings of a
similar project called OVC (open vote consortium). It's very similar
to what philippe forwarded in that it's an open source framework for e-
ballots and voting machines.

It's possible the framework could be used in conjunction with the
streetwiki prototype and other registration efforts, since the voting
mechanism itself (at least ovc's design - I haven't seen ovn's)
doesn't depend on the hardware interface used to cast the vote -- this
is deliberate, to allow for flexible solutions to problems related to
accessibility.

A hybrid solution, where voting could be conducted via the Internet
but doesn't have to be, depending on the user's preference, might be
an interesting development in the world of e-dem.
