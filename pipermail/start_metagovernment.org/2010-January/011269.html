1 - Official registered user can open any thread on specialized forums

2 - Discussions follows, a corum (to define) of users can decide after a

3 - The recommandation is publicly evaluated by expert/specialists.
4 - The evaluated recommandation is submitted to the user definitive vote.

1.idea  ---> 2.recommandation ---> 3.evaluation ---> 4.vote -->
