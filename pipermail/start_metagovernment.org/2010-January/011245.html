Point of clarification.

Thomas von der Elbe wrote:
>  If you want to translate a 
> ranked voting (-10 to +10) into a binary, information will be lost. But 
> it is still a valid information to tanslate the +10 votes into a "Yes". 
> I would even say all votes from +1 to +10 are translatable to "Yes".

The -10 to +10 style of voting you describe is called range voting. 
Ranked voting is ordinal.. 1st, 2nd 3rd, etc.

I'm not convinced such votes are translatable in either case.  Too much 
jamming of carefully formed square pegs into unexpectedly round holes.
