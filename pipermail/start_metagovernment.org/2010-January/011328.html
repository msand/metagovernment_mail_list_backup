Manuel pointed out a way to make the chat window bigger in PiratePad  
using a Firefox extension. Here are instructions:

1. Install Firebug into Firefox:
https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1843

2. Open the meeting page in Firefox
http://piratepad.net/fiuASvRqss
and click the Full Window button at the bottom.

3. Right-click on the chat sidebar and choose "inspect element"

4. In the resulting window, go up a few lined and select the item <div  
id="padsidebar">

5. On the right side, change the element: width: 290px;
Change the 290 to something larger.
