Ned, I understand your position, but where’s the urgency now? The blanking of the homepage was a nuclear option that worked once, but what next? Do we just blank it again? It seems to me that a real tangible reward provides a really nice incentive (and a positive one, not a negative one).

Jacopo, a similar reply. I agree that we need to continue working on finding some core principles, or yes even just one. But I think we’d work harder if there were a bigger incentive. What if you had the chance to get €50,000 to work on Airesis for a year? Perhaps this group could agree to provide you that money. (We’d just need to find a way to raise the money first.)

We could look at it a different way: don’t raise the case prize, but rather assume it already exists. If we were to spend a bunch of money on developing something for this community, what would it be? If we come up with a good enough answer to that question, then actually finding the money may not be too difficult.
