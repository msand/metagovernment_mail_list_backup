I think the ODG should be a collaborative work, I will place for example
the definition of the MG principles, possibly a discussion over the just
finished experience with Vilfredo (this I think can be can be mediated by
Pietro), define a new proposal to see the whole process in the Airesis
platform, this is what come to my mind, I am sure there will be MG point to
be discussed.
I feel the first dilemma should be the time, the date, the list of the
people. I am also concerned about using google services but until we do
find a better cloud service that offer such a span of features in a single
platform is hard to make a different choice.
Every one of us have the habits of using a platform and shifting to another
is seen with suspicious eyes.
Doodle for the event?
Peace Jacopo
