My thought is that we should concentrate on exploring and mapping our 
agreements and disagreements concerning foundational principles and 
framings and strategies and such (that is, regarding metagovernment 
sorts of issues). I don't see that putting money into the mix at this 
point would help that process at all, and the motivation provided by 
blanking the home page suggests that it is not really necessary.

In various of my Vilfredo proposals, I listed numerous fundamental 
issues that we could explore and discuss together, and that would need 
to be settled as part of the run-up to any joint money-gathering attempt 
or any joint attempt to spend money.
