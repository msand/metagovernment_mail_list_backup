Some of the people that build e-part believe that the end result will be
the same, a new era of participation. When I ask them to join our
association Tecnologie Democratiche and join effort to build the PB module
for Airesis, they reacted like politician do when I present them Airesis, a
big enthusiasm and a no follow up.
Is like a self survival mode, where regardless of the political direction,
left or right, they form a kind of partnership against any form of direct
democracy effort.
This happen every time the signature to request a referendum, never
propositive, in Italy the referendum is only to abrogate a parliament
approved  law, they figure al sort of rules to stop it from happening. This
is as far as we go in terms  of direct democracy.
I do believe in critical mass and this is where i bring my fight with
Airesis. If critical mass will respond in real time to bad politics there
will happen the real change.
Internet and  social network  will play  a crucial role in the
dissemination of the potential of critical mass. Established mass media are
and will play against untill the last minute. Survival is a very strong
dissuasion energy!

Cheers
J
