https://freestateproject.org/

To my thinking, this is exactly the opposite of what we should be
doing. Even if it works (and in this case I predict spectacular
failure, unlike most commune-type projects which usually just
gradually decay into oblivion) it doesn't provide us any useful
information about how to structure a government that works for all of
us. The problem isn't liberals or conservatives or any other
particular personality type, it's that some personality types
(particularly SDAPs) are attracted to leadership roles and that
existing types of government actually solidify and amplify this power
once they assume it. Job one of a properly engineered form of
government is to correct for this flaw in older forms of governments
(including the currently popular "misrepresentative democracy" form).
