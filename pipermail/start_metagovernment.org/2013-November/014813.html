I have looked intensively into Holacracy and there is already a post in the
past of this mailinglist where i believe i mentioned it and wanted to talk
about it.
Its similar to sociocracy from the netherlands,which has a longer history
and is in many aspects very similar.

Its not bad compared to status quo forms, but very lacking compared to the
fundamental values of our group.

"centralised" body.

organize ourselves through "contract law".

on international law or something??
