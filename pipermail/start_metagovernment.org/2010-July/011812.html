Here's something new we're working on:
http://u.zelea.com/w/Site:Discussion_retrofit

  A discussion retrofit is the injection of technical links into an
  online discussion in order to make it serviceable for
  e-democracy. The retrofit is carried out by a team of two "fitters"
  who join in the discussion thread for that purpose.

We're mostly working on step 1, at the moment.  This involves looking
for threads that are ripe for retrofitting, where the discussion is
*already* focused on concrete solutions.  These might be proposals
that were put forward by the participants themselves, or by others
(e.g. a law recently passed by officials).

Threads like that are harder to find than you would think.  Has anyone
come across one, recently?
