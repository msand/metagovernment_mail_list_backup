Hi,

you may not believe it, but you may improve your happiness even in long
term (see e.g. Martin Seligman
<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Martin_Seligman>, Ed Diener
<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ed_Diener> and Ruut Veenhoven
<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ruut_Veenhoven>)

And you may also design a search engine (like google) that will provide you
with result that are likely to make people in society happy.
It may be not obvious, that if you try to make people happy you make it
also on the level of society.

But that is not all, you may even design an app for smartphones that will
occasionally ask you how happy you are to see how it the engine working.
You may also need some more analytical questions and research to improve
the search engine, but the important is - it is possible.

When it comes to implementation, I will try to persuade some people behind
some existing search engine to start one more engine and give it a try.

What do you say?
